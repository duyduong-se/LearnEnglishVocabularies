### 151. exist *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** tồn tại

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| exist | existed | existed |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -ed (regular)
- exist là nội động từ, không có tân ngữ
- cease to exist = không còn tồn tại

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

[something] + exist(s) → Điều gì tồn tại
- Many species that existed millions of years ago are now extinct.
- The technology to make this possible already exists — we just need funding.

exist + in/as → Tồn tại ở dạng nào
- By the time the project ends, a more effective solution will likely exist.
- The problem exists in different forms across many industries.

cease to exist → Không còn tồn tại
- Several smaller companies ceased to exist after the market downturn.
- Traditional bookshops may cease to exist if digital reading continues to grow.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- exist in reality, currently exist, exist side by side, no longer exists, exist naturally

### 152. contain *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** chứa, bao gồm

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| contain | contained | contained |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -ed (regular)
- là stative verb, không dùng tiếp diễn
- contain = have inside
- contain (kiềm chế cảm xúc)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

contain + [noun] → Chứa cái gì bên trong
- This document contains all the guidelines you need for the project.
- The package contained fragile glassware packed carefully in foam.

be contained + in → Được chứa trong
- Each module will contain at least three interactive exercises and a quiz.
- All relevant information is contained in the attached report.

contain + emotions → Kiềm chế cảm xúc
- She could barely contain her excitement when she heard the news.
- He found it difficult to contain his frustration during the heated discussion.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- contain information, contain chemicals, contain oneself, hard to contain, contain damage

### 154. relate *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** liên quan, kể lại

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| relate | related | related |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -d (regular)
- relate + to (liên quan đến; có thể đồng cảm)
- relate + [noun] (kể lại)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- relate to others, closely related, directly related, relate to the topic, relate an experience

### 155. depend *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** phụ thuộc, dựa vào

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| depend | depended | depended |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -ed (regular)
- depend + on/upon
- "it depends" (tùy tình huống)
- dependable = đáng tin cậy

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

depend + on/upon + sb/sth → Phụ thuộc vào ai/cái gì
- Children depend on adults to provide a safe and nurturing environment.
- The project's success depends heavily on the cooperation of all team members.

it depends + on → Phụ thuộc vào/tùy theo
- Whether we launch on time will depend on the results of the final testing phase.
- It depends on the client's feedback whether we proceed or revise the design.

depend + on + sb + to V → Trông cậy vào ai làm gì
- The success of the project depends largely on team cooperation and communication.
- We depend on our suppliers to deliver materials on schedule.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- depend on others, depend entirely, heavily depend, depend on the context, independent

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- depend on: dựa vào, tin cậy → *You can always depend on her in a crisis.*

### 156. vary *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** thay đổi, biến đổi, khác nhau

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| vary | varied | varied |

**Lưu ý:**
- y → ied (regular)
- vary + from...to (biến đổi từ...đến)
- vary + by (thay đổi theo)
- variable (tính từ/danh từ)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

vary + from...to → Biến đổi từ...đến
- The temperature in this region varies from minus ten in winter to plus forty in summer.
- Prices vary from store to store, so it pays to shop around.

vary + by + [factor] → Thay đổi theo yếu tố gì
- Teaching styles vary greatly depending on the subject and age group.
- Results vary by region due to differences in local infrastructure.

vary + widely/greatly → Biến đổi đáng kể
- Customer preferences will vary as we expand into different international markets.
- The quality of service can vary widely from one provider to another.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- vary greatly, vary widely, vary by region, vary in quality, vary over time

### 159. prove *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** chứng minh, chứng tỏ

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| prove | proved | proven |

**Lưu ý:**
- V3 có thể là "proven" hoặc "proved"
- prove + [noun/that clause]
- prove + to + V (chứng tỏ là)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

prove + (that) + clause → Chứng minh rằng...
- The experiment proved that the new formula is twice as effective as the old one.
- He proved that consistent practice leads to measurable improvement.

prove + to + V → Chứng tỏ là
- This investment strategy will prove its value over the next two to three years.
- The new approach proved to be more cost-effective than expected.

prove + [adjective] → Chứng tỏ/hóa ra là (prove useful)
- She proved her leadership ability by guiding the team through a difficult crisis.
- The backup plan proved essential when the main system went down.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- prove a point, prove one's worth, prove effective, proven track record, hard to prove

### 160. refer *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** đề cập, tham khảo, giới thiệu

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| refer | referred | referred |

**Lưu ý:**
- gấp đôi r trước -ed (regular)
- refer + to (đề cập đến; tra cứu)
- refer + sb + to (giới thiệu ai đến)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

refer + to + [noun/sb] → Đề cập đến; tra cứu; gọi là
- Please refer to the user manual if you encounter any technical issues.

refer + sb + to → Giới thiệu ai đến

be referred + to + as → Được gọi là

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- refer to a document, commonly referred to, refer back to, refer for advice, cross-reference

### 162. compare *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** so sánh

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| compare | compared | compared |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -d (regular)
- compare + A + with/to + B
- compare + favorably/unfavorably
- beyond compare = tuyệt vời không gì sánh bằng

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

compare + A + with/to + B → So sánh A với B
- Always compare different options before making a major financial decision.
- She compared the two proposals and chose the one with the better long-term value.

compare + favorably → So sánh thuận lợi hơn
- The report will compare performance metrics across all regional offices.
- Our product compares favorably with competitor offerings in terms of quality and price.

compared to/with → So với
- Sales this quarter are much stronger compared to the same period last year.
- Compared with traditional methods, the new system is far more efficient.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- compare prices, compare options, favorably compared, compare notes, beyond compare

### 163. result *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** dẫn đến, là kết quả

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| result | resulted | resulted |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -ed (regular)
- result + in (dẫn đến kết quả)
- result + from (là kết quả của nguyên nhân)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

result + in + [outcome] → Dẫn đến kết quả
- Poor planning often results in delays and cost overruns on projects.
- Good communication generally results in stronger teamwork and fewer errors.

result + from + [cause] → Là kết quả của nguyên nhân

as a result → Kết quả là
- Consistent training will result in measurable improvements in your English skills.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- result in failure/success, result from neglect, as a direct result, end result, final result
