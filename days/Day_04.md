### 51. read *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** đọc

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| read | read | read |

**Lưu ý:**
- viết giống nhau nhưng phát âm khác: read /riːd/ (hiện tại), read /rɛd/ (quá khứ)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

read + [noun] → Đọc cái gì
- She reads at least one book every month to expand her knowledge.
- He read the entire report carefully before the board meeting.

read + to + sb → Đọc cho ai nghe
- She reads bedtime stories to her children every night.
- The teacher read the poem aloud to the class.

read + about → Đọc về chủ đề gì
- She reads about the latest research findings to stay up to date.
- He loves reading about ancient civilizations in his free time.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- read a book, read carefully, read aloud, read between the lines, read widely

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- read through: đọc hết toàn bộ → *Read through the contract before signing.*
- read up on: nghiên cứu kỹ → *I need to read up on the new regulations.*

### 52. spend *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** chi tiêu, dành (thời gian)

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| spend | spent | spent |

**Lưu ý:**
- spend + time/money on sth
- spend time + V-ing

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

spend + [money] + on → Chi tiêu tiền cho cái gì
- She spent all her savings on a world tour after graduation.
- He spends a lot on books and online courses each year.

spend + [time] + V-ing → Dành thời gian làm gì
- I spend about two hours every day commuting to work.
- She spent the whole afternoon preparing for the presentation.

spend + [time] + with → Dành thời gian cùng ai
- She spent the afternoon with her mentor going over the project details.
- He tries to spend quality time with his family every weekend.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- spend time, spend money, spend the weekend, spend wisely, spend hours doing

### 53. grow *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** phát triển, lớn lên, trồng

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| grow | grew | grown |

**Lưu ý:**
- grow có thể là nội động từ (grow up) hoặc ngoại động từ (grow vegetables)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

grow + [noun] → Trồng cây/rau
- She grows tomatoes and herbs in her backyard garden.
- He grew up in a family that grew rice for generations.

grow + [adj] → Trở nên (grow older, grow stronger)
- She grew up in a multilingual household, so languages came naturally.
- The company has grown significantly over the past five years.

grow + into → Phát triển thành
- Our team will grow from ten to twenty members by the end of the year.
- The small startup grew into a successful international brand.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- grow up, grow rapidly, grow steadily, grow a business, grow in confidence

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- grow up: lớn lên, trưởng thành → *She grew up in a small town.*
- grow into: phát triển thành → *The small startup grew into a global company.*
- grow apart: xa cách dần → *Old friends sometimes grow apart over time.*

### 54. open *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** mở

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| open | opened | opened |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -ed (regular)
- open cũng là tính từ (an open question)
- open up = mở ra, thổ lộ

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

open + [noun] → Mở cái gì
- She opened the meeting with a brief summary of last week's progress.
- He opened the envelope carefully to avoid tearing the letter.

open + for → Mở cửa phục vụ
- The store opens at nine in the morning on weekdays.
- The new café will open for business next Monday.

open + up → Mở ra (nghĩa rộng hơn)
- The new branch will open in March next year.
- He finally opened up about his feelings after months of silence.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- open a door, open a meeting, open an account, open to ideas, open for business

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- open up: thổ lộ, mở ra → *He finally opened up about his feelings.*
- open out: trải rộng ra → *The road opens out into a wide avenue.*

### 55. walk *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** đi bộ

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| walk | walked | walked |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -ed (regular)
- walk có thể có tân ngữ: walk the dog, walk someone home

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

walk + [distance/direction] → Đi bộ bao xa/theo hướng nào
- He walks to the office every morning as part of his fitness routine.
- They walked along the beach watching the sunset together.

walk + sb → Đi cùng/đưa ai về
- I will walk you through the entire process step by step.
- She walked her dog in the park for an hour every evening.

walk + into → Đi vào, tình cờ gặp phải
- He walked into the office and greeted everyone with a smile.
- She walked into a great opportunity without even looking for it.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- walk to work, walk away, walk the dog, walk freely, walk in the park

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- walk away from: bỏ đi → *She walked away from the deal.*
- walk into: tình cờ gặp → *He walked into a glass door.*
- walk out: bỏ ra → *Staff walked out in protest.*

### 56. win *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** thắng, giành được

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| win | won | won |

**Lưu ý:**
- win + [noun] (thắng giải gì)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

- win a competition, win a prize, win support, win the championship, win hands down

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- win over: thuyết phục, chinh phục → *She won over the audience with her charm.*

### 57. offer *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** đề nghị, cung cấp

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| offer | offered | offered |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -ed (regular)
- offer + to V (đề nghị làm gì)

offer + to + V → Đề nghị làm gì

offer + sb + sth → Đề nghị cho ai cái gì

offer + [noun] → Cung cấp dịch vụ/cơ hội
- We will offer a special discount to loyal customers during the anniversary sale.
- The university offers a wide range of scholarships for international students.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- offer help, offer a solution, offer a discount, offer support, offer an apology

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- offer up: đưa ra, hiến dâng → *She offered up a compromise solution.*

### 58. remember *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** nhớ

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| remember | remembered | remembered |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -ed (regular)
- remember + to V (nhớ phải làm gì) vs. remember + V-ing (nhớ đã làm gì)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

remember + to + V → Nhớ phải làm gì (chưa làm)
- Please remember to send the confirmation email before leaving.
- Remember to lock the door when you go out tonight.

remember + V-ing → Nhớ đã từng làm gì (đã xảy ra)
- I remember meeting him at the conference two years ago.
- She still remembers learning to ride a bike as a child.

remember + [noun/that clause] → Nhớ điều gì
- Future generations will remember this moment as a turning point in history.
- I remember that she mentioned this problem last week.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- remember clearly, remember fondly, remember correctly, hard to remember, remember by heart

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- remember as: nhớ đến như... → *She is remembered as a great leader.*

### 59. love *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** yêu, thích

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| love | loved | loved |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -d (regular)
- là stative verb, không dùng trong tiếp diễn
- love + to V hoặc love + V-ing

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

love + [noun] → Yêu/thích cái gì
- She loves reading historical fiction in her spare time.
- He loves music and spends hours listening to jazz every evening.

love + to + V → Thích làm gì
- He loved every moment of his trip to Southeast Asia.
- She loves to explore new places whenever she travels.

love + V-ing → Thích làm gì (cùng nghĩa)
- I know you will love working with this innovative and passionate team.
- They love spending weekends outdoors hiking or cycling.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- love deeply, fall in love, love unconditionally, love the outdoors, love a challenge

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- love up: yêu thương chiều chuộng → *She loves up her pets every evening.*

### 60. consider *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** xem xét, cân nhắc

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| consider | considered | considered |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -ed (regular)
- consider + V-ing (không dùng "to V")
- consider sb/sth to be...

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

consider + V-ing → Cân nhắc làm gì
- The company is considering expanding to the Southeast Asian market.
- She is considering applying for a scholarship abroad.

consider + sb/sth + [adj/noun] → Coi ai/cái gì là
- She was considered the best candidate for the senior manager position.
- He is widely considered one of the most talented engineers in the field.

consider + [noun] → Xem xét điều gì
- Please consider my application and let me know your decision soon.
- We need to consider all options before making a final decision.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- consider carefully, consider all options, widely considered, consider the consequences, under consideration

### 61. appear *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** xuất hiện, có vẻ

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| appear | appeared | appeared |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -ed (regular)
- appear + [adj] (có vẻ như thế nào)
- appear + to V (dường như)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

appear + [adjective] → Có vẻ như thế nào
- The solution to the problem appears quite straightforward at first glance.
- She appeared calm during the interview despite being nervous.

appear + to + V → Dường như đang/đã làm gì
- A strange error appeared on the screen without any warning.
- He appeared to know the answer even before reading the question.

It appears (that) + clause → Có vẻ như là...
- The new product will appear in stores across the country next month.
- It appears that the issue has been resolved without further action.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- appear suddenly, appear calm, appear in public, appear on stage, appear to be

### 62. buy *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** mua

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| buy | bought | bought |

**Lưu ý:**
- buy + sth for sb hoặc buy + sb + sth
- buy into = tin tưởng vào

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

buy + [noun] → Mua cái gì
- She buys fresh vegetables at the local market every Saturday morning.
- The company will buy new equipment to modernize the production line.

buy + sth + for + sb → Mua cái gì cho ai
- He bought his parents a new television as a Lunar New Year gift.
- She bought a birthday cake for her colleague at the office.

buy + sb + [noun] → Mua cho ai cái gì
- Let me buy you a coffee — you look like you need one.
- He bought everyone on the team lunch to celebrate the project launch.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- buy online, buy in bulk, buy at a discount, buy time, buy into an idea

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- buy out: mua lại toàn bộ cổ phần → *The firm bought out its competitor.*
- buy into: tin tưởng vào, mua cổ phần → *I don't buy into that theory.*

### 63. wait *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** chờ, đợi

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| wait | waited | waited |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -ed (regular)
- wait for + sb/sth
- wait to + V
- can't wait = rất mong chờ

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

wait + for + sb/sth → Chờ ai/cái gì
- Please wait in the lobby — someone will assist you shortly.
- We waited over an hour for the delayed flight to depart.

wait + to + V → Chờ để làm gì
- She waited to hear the final decision before making any plans.
- He waited to be introduced before joining the conversation.

can't wait + to + V → Rất mong chờ làm gì
- I can't wait to see the results of the new marketing campaign.
- She can't wait to start her new role at the company.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- wait patiently, wait in line, wait one's turn, wait anxiously, keep someone waiting

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- wait on: phục vụ → *The waiter waited on the guests promptly.*
- wait up: thức chờ → *Don't wait up for me, I'll be late.*

### 64. serve *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** phục vụ

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| serve | served | served |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -d (regular)
- serve + [noun] (phục vụ cái gì) hoặc serve + as (đóng vai trò là)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

serve + [noun] → Phục vụ món ăn/dịch vụ
- This new policy will serve as a model for other departments to follow.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- serve customers, serve a purpose, serve as an example, serve the community, serve hot

### 65. die *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** chết, mất

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| die | died | died |

**Lưu ý:**
- y → ied (regular)
- die of (chết vì bệnh) vs. die from (chết vì chấn thương)
- die out = tuyệt chủng

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

die + of/from → Chết vì nguyên nhân gì
- He died peacefully surrounded by his family at the age of ninety.
- She died of a rare illness that doctors had never seen before.

die + [adverb] → Chết như thế nào
- Many species die out every year due to habitat destruction.
- The old traditions are slowly dying out in modern society.

die + young/old → Chết trẻ/già
- Without proper funding, this important research project will die before it can help anyone.
- It is tragic when talented artists die young.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- die suddenly, die young, die of cancer, die for a cause, die laughing (idiom)

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- die out: tuyệt chủng, biến mất → *Many traditions are dying out.*
- die down: lắng xuống → *The storm finally died down.*

### 66. send *(verb — irregular)*

|---|---|---|
| send | sent | sent |

**Lưu ý:**
- send + sth to sb hoặc send + sb + sth

### 67. expect *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** mong đợi, dự kiến

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| expect | expected | expected |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -ed (regular)
- expect + to V
- expect + sb + to V
- be expected to = được dự kiến

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

expect + to + V → Mong đợi/dự kiến làm gì
- I expect this project to take at least three months to complete.
- He didn't expect to win the award at such a young age.

expect + sb + to + V → Mong đợi ai làm gì
- She expected her team to deliver results on time without being reminded.
- The manager expects everyone to contribute actively in meetings.

be expected + to + V → Được dự kiến sẽ làm gì
- The new product is expected to launch in the second quarter of next year.
- Sales are expected to increase by 20% in the coming months.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- expect too much, fully expected, expect the unexpected, as expected, meet expectations
