## Từ học hôm nay

### 1. rate *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** tỷ lệ; tốc độ; mức giá

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a rate / rates

**Lưu ý:**
- "At any rate" = anyway, regardless
- "At this rate" = if things continue this way: "At this rate, we will never finish."
- "Rate" cũng là động từ: "to rate a film/restaurant" = đánh giá

**Ví dụ:**
- The interest rate on mortgages has risen significantly over the past year.
- At this rate, the project will not be finished until next year.
- At any rate, we should make a decision before the end of the week.
- The country has one of the highest birth rates in the region.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- interest rate, exchange rate
- birth/death rate, crime rate
- at any rate, at this rate
- heart rate, tax rate

### 2. percent *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** phần trăm

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** one percent / percent (không thêm -s sau số)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Percent" KHÔNG thêm "-s" sau số: "50 percent" (KHÔNG "50 percents")
- "Percentage" (danh từ không dùng với số): "a high percentage of students"
- "One hundred percent" (thông tục) = completely: "Are you sure?" "One hundred percent!"

**Ví dụ:**
- Prices have increased by almost fifteen percent over the last two years.
- A large percentage of the students in this class come from rural areas.
- "Are you ready for the presentation?" "One hundred percent!"
- The company reported a twelve percent rise in profits this quarter.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- 50 percent, percent of
- a high/low percentage, percentage point
- one hundred percent
- per capita / per annum

---

### 3. difference *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** sự khác biệt (C); điều khác nhau (C); sự thay đổi/tác động (U)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a difference / differences (C); difference (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Make a difference" = tạo ra sự thay đổi tích cực
- "A world of difference" = khác biệt rất lớn: "This new software makes a world of difference."
- "Split the difference" = thỏa hiệp ở điểm giữa

**Ví dụ:**
- Small daily habits can make a big difference in your overall health over time.
- The difference between success and failure is often just persistence and hard work.
- Learning in a real classroom makes a world of difference compared to studying alone.
- They could not agree on a price, so they decided to split the difference.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- make a difference, tell the difference
- the difference between
- a world of difference
- split the difference

### 4. change *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** sự thay đổi (C/U); tiền lẻ (U)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a change / changes (C); change (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "For a change" = để thay đổi không khí: "Let's try something new for a change."
- "A change of heart" = thay đổi ý kiến đột ngột
- "Keep the change" = giữ tiền thừa (khi trả tiền)

**Ví dụ:**
- The company needs to make some significant changes to its business strategy.
- We decided to go to a different restaurant for a change.
- She had a change of heart and decided to stay in her current job after all.
- He paid with a large bill and told the driver to keep the change.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- make a change, for a change
- a change of scenery, a change of heart
- spare change, keep the change
- climate change

### 5. development *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** sự phát triển (U); sự kiện/tin tức mới (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** development (U) / a development, developments (C)

**Lưu ý:**
- Uncountable: "Continuous development is important in any career."
- Countable: "There have been some unexpected developments in the case."
- "Professional development" = đào tạo liên tục nâng cao kỹ năng nghề nghiệp

**Ví dụ:**
- The country has experienced rapid economic development over the past decade.
- We will update you on any new developments as soon as we have more information.
- She regularly attends workshops and seminars for her professional development.
- The software is still under development and will be released next year.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- economic/personal development
- a recent/new development
- under development, sustainable development
- professional development

---

### 6. growth *(noun — uncountable)*
**Nghĩa:** sự tăng trưởng, phát triển

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** growth / (không có số nhiều)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Growth" thường uncountable; "a growth" (countable) = khối u (y học)
- "Personal growth" = phát triển bản thân
- "Growth mindset" = tư duy phát triển (tin rằng năng lực có thể được cải thiện)

**Ví dụ:**
- The economy experienced strong growth of six percent last year.
- Traveling alone is an incredible experience for personal growth.
- Having a growth mindset means believing that your abilities can be developed.
- The rapid population growth in this region is putting pressure on resources.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- economic growth, personal growth
- rapid/steady/slow growth, growth rate
- a period of growth, stunt growth
- population growth

---

### 7. increase *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** sự tăng lên, mức tăng

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** an increase / increases

**Lưu ý:**
- "Increase" (danh từ) /ˈɪnkriːs/ vs "increase" (động từ) /ɪnˈkriːs/ — trọng âm khác nhau
- "On the increase" = đang có xu hướng tăng
- Đối nghĩa: "increase" ↔ "decrease"

**Ví dụ:**
- There has been a significant increase in online shopping over the past few years.
- Online scams are on the increase, so it is important to stay vigilant.
- The employees were pleased with their five percent pay increase.
- Doctors have reported a sharp increase in cases of the flu this winter.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- an increase in, a sharp/gradual increase
- on the increase
- increase in price/temperature/crime
- pay increase

---

### 8. decrease *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** sự giảm xuống, mức giảm

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a decrease / decreases

**Lưu ý:**
- "Decrease" (danh từ) /ˈdiːkriːs/ vs (động từ) /dɪˈkriːs/ — trọng âm khác
- Cặp từ đối lập: "increase" ↔ "decrease"; "rise" ↔ "fall/drop"
- "Noticeable decrease" = sự giảm rõ rệt

**Ví dụ:**
- There has been a noticeable decrease in air pollution since the new laws were introduced.
- Sales figures show a steady decrease over the past three quarters.
- The number of smokers is on the decrease as more people become health-conscious.
- The government reported a decrease in unemployment this year.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- a decrease in, a sharp/gradual decrease
- on the decrease
- decrease in price/temperature/number

---

### 9. progress *(noun — uncountable)*
**Nghĩa:** tiến bộ, sự tiến triển

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** progress / (không có số nhiều — KHÔNG dùng "progresses")

**Lưu ý:**
- "Progress" luôn UNCOUNTABLE — không bao giờ nói "a progress" hay "progresses"
- "In progress" = đang diễn ra: "The meeting is in progress."
- "Work in progress" (WIP) = tác phẩm/dự án chưa hoàn thành

**Ví dụ:**
- She is making excellent progress in her English lessons — she improves every week.
- The construction is currently in progress and is expected to be completed by June.
- This presentation is still a work in progress — I have not finished the last section.
- The doctor was pleased with the patient's progress after the surgery.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- make progress, in progress
- slow/steady/rapid progress, progress report
- check/monitor progress
- a work in progress

---

### 10. success *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** thành công (U); người/thứ thành công (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** success (U) / a success, successes (C)

**Lưu ý:**
- Uncountable (khái niệm): "Success requires hard work." / "I wish you success."
- Countable (kết quả cụ thể): "The event was a great success." / "one of his successes"
- "Overnight success" = thành công nhanh chóng (thường trong thực tế là đã cố gắng lâu)

**Ví dụ:**
- Success in any field requires a combination of talent, hard work, and opportunity.
- The fundraising event was a great success, raising twice the target amount.
- She may look like an overnight success, but she worked hard for ten years to get here.
- The key to success is never giving up, even when things get difficult.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- achieve/find success
- a great/huge success
- key to success
- overnight success, success story

---

### 11. failure *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** thất bại (U); người/thứ thất bại (C); sự cố kỹ thuật (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** failure (U) / a failure, failures (C)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Failure" (U) = khái niệm thất bại; "a failure" (C) = người/thứ cụ thể thất bại
- "Fear of failure" = nỗi sợ thất bại (rào cản tâm lý lớn)
- "System/power failure" = sự cố kỹ thuật (countable)

**Ví dụ:**
- Fear of failure often stops people from even trying to pursue their dreams.
- The project ended in complete failure because of poor planning and communication.
- The network experienced a major system failure that affected thousands of users.
- Every great inventor has had many failures before achieving success.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- end in failure, a miserable failure
- system/power failure
- failure to do something, fear of failure
- learn from failure

---

### 12. opportunity *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** cơ hội

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** an opportunity / opportunities

**Lưu ý:**
- "Opportunity" thường đi với "take, seize, miss, create"
- "Window of opportunity" = khoảng thời gian ngắn thuận lợi để hành động
- "Opportunity cost" (kinh tế) = chi phí cơ hội

**Ví dụ:**
- This internship is a golden opportunity to gain real work experience.
- Do not miss this opportunity — it may not come again for a long time.
- We have a small window of opportunity to negotiate a better deal.
- The company gave all employees an equal opportunity to apply for the promotion.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- take/seize an opportunity
- an opportunity to, a golden opportunity
- miss an opportunity, equal opportunity
- window of opportunity

---

### 13. challenge *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** thách thức; lời thách đấu

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a challenge / challenges

**Lưu ý:**
- "Rise to the challenge" = vươn lên đáp ứng thách thức một cách xuất sắc
- "Challenge" cũng là động từ: "to challenge someone" = thách thức; "to challenge an idea" = phản biện
- "Challenging" (tính từ) = khó nhưng thú vị (tích cực hơn "difficult")

**Ví dụ:**
- Learning a new language is a significant challenge, but it is worth the effort.
- The young athlete rose to the challenge and won the championship against all odds.
- Climate change is the greatest challenge facing humanity in the 21st century.
- She finds her new job challenging but very rewarding.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- face/meet a challenge
- rise to the challenge
- a major/great challenge, a challenge to
- issue a challenge

---

### 14. risk *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** rủi ro (C/U); nguy cơ (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a risk / risks (C); risk (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "At risk" (không mạo từ) = đang gặp nguy hiểm: "Children are at risk."
- "At the risk of doing something" = dù có khả năng bị phê bình/nguy hiểm
- "Calculated risk" = rủi ro đã được cân nhắc kỹ

**Ví dụ:**
- Starting your own business always involves a certain amount of risk.
- She took a calculated risk by investing all her savings into the startup.
- At the risk of sounding critical, I think the proposal needs significant improvement.
- Children living in poverty are at risk of poor health and educational outcomes.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- take a risk, at risk
- a calculated risk, risk management
- at the risk of
- high/low risk, risk-taker

---

### 15. benefit *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** lợi ích (C/U); trợ cấp (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a benefit / benefits (C); benefit (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Give someone the benefit of the doubt" = tin tưởng ai dù chưa có bằng chứng
- "Benefit of hindsight" = lợi thế nhìn lại sau khi đã biết kết quả
- "Benefits package" = gói phúc lợi cho nhân viên

**Ví dụ:**
- Regular exercise has many benefits for both physical and mental health.
- I will give him the benefit of the doubt this time, but he needs to improve.
- With the benefit of hindsight, she realized she had made the wrong decision.
- The new job comes with an excellent benefits package including health insurance.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- the benefit of, benefit of the doubt
- health/financial benefits, employee benefits
- for the benefit of, mutual benefit
- be of benefit to

---

### 16. purpose *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** mục đích, mục tiêu

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a purpose / purposes

**Lưu ý:**
- "On purpose" (không mạo từ) = deliberately = cố ý
- "Sense of purpose" = cảm giác có mục tiêu sống — quan trọng cho sức khỏe tâm lý
- "All-purpose" = đa mục đích (tính từ ghép)

**Ví dụ:**
- The purpose of this meeting is to discuss the marketing strategy for the new product.
- Did you do that on purpose, or was it an accident?
- Having a clear sense of purpose helps people stay motivated through difficult times.
- This tool is all-purpose and can be used for many different tasks.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- the purpose of, on purpose
- serve a purpose, a sense of purpose
- for a specific purpose, all-purpose
- purposeful

## Ôn tập nhanh (Quick Review)
Điền từ thích hợp:

1. She is making excellent _______ in her English course. (progress / development)
2. Don't miss this golden _______. (opportunity / chance — cả hai đúng, nhưng opportunity hay hơn)
3. He rose to the _______ and delivered an outstanding performance. (challenge / risk)
4. I will give you the _______ of the doubt this time. (benefit / advantage)
5. The project ended in complete _______. (failure / success)
6. "Did you do that _______ purpose?" "No, it was an accident." (on / for)
7. "Progress" có thể dùng số nhiều không? (Không — uncountable)
8. "A large _______ of water was wasted." (amount / number)

**Đáp án:** 1. progress 2. opportunity 3. challenge 4. benefit 5. failure 6. on 7. Không 8. amount
