## Từ học hôm nay

### 1. translate *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** dịch

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| translate | translated | translated |

**Lưu ý:**
- Regular — thêm -d (vì tận cùng bằng -e)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

translate + noun + from/into → dịch cái gì từ/sang ngôn ngữ
- She translates medical documents from English into Vietnamese.
- The novel was translated into 30 languages.

translate + into action → chuyển thành hành động
- We need to translate this strategy into concrete actions.
- Good intentions must be translated into measurable outcomes.

be translated into → được dịch sang
- The instructions were translated into five languages for international users.
- The report was translated into a simplified version for the general public.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- translate a document, translate accurately, translate ideas into action
