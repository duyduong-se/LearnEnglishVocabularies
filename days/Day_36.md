## Từ học hôm nay

### 1. place *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** nơi, chỗ, địa điểm

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a place / places

**Lưu ý:**
- "Take place" = xảy ra (KHÔNG thay bằng "happen" trong ngữ cảnh có kế hoạch)
- "In place" = đúng chỗ, sẵn sàng: "Everything is in place for the event."
- "Place" cũng là động từ: "to place an order" (đặt hàng)

**Ví dụ:**
- This is a great place to have lunch — the food is delicious and affordable.
- The ceremony will take place on the main stage at 7 PM.
- She felt completely out of place at the formal dinner party.
- He is looking for a quiet place to study without any distractions.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- take place (xảy ra), in place (đúng chỗ), out of place (lạc lõng)
- place of birth (nơi sinh), place of work (nơi làm việc)
- a good/popular place, tourist place
- feel out of place (cảm thấy không phù hợp)

### 2. country *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** quốc gia, đất nước; nông thôn (the country)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a country / countries

**Lưu ý:**
- "Country" có hai nghĩa chính: quốc gia (C) và nông thôn (U, với "the")
- "The country" = the countryside (vùng nông thôn): "I prefer the city to the country."
- Một số quốc gia có mạo từ "the": the USA, the UK, the Netherlands

**Ví dụ:**
- Vietnam is a beautiful country with a rich culture and history.
- She grew up in the country and finds city life too noisy and fast.
- Developing countries need more investment in education and healthcare.
- The two countries signed a trade agreement to boost economic cooperation.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- developing country (nước đang phát triển), developed country
- live in the country (sống ở nông thôn)
- country music (nhạc đồng quê), country house (nhà ở nông thôn)
- cross-country (xuyên quốc gia/nông thôn)

---

### 3. city *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** thành phố (lớn)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a city / cities

**Lưu ý:**
- "City" lớn hơn "town"; trong tiếng Anh (Anh), "city" cần được vua/nữ hoàng công nhận
- "The City" (viết hoa) ở London = khu tài chính (Financial District)
- "City" và "town": "city" thường có hệ thống hành chính lớn hơn

**Ví dụ:**
- Ho Chi Minh City is the largest city in Vietnam by population.
- She prefers city life because of the many career opportunities it offers.
- The city center is always busy with traffic and tourists on weekends.
- The city plans to build a new metro line to ease traffic congestion.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- city center (trung tâm thành phố), city life (cuộc sống đô thị)
- inner city (nội ô thành phố), capital city (thủ đô)
- city hall (tòa thị chính), smart city (thành phố thông minh)
- in the city (ở thành phố)

---

### 4. town *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** thị trấn, thị xã

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a town / towns

**Lưu ý:**
- "In town" (không mạo từ) = đang ở khu vực trung tâm (trong tiếng Anh)
- "Out of town" = đi vắng hoặc xa trung tâm thành phố
- "It takes a village/town" = thành ngữ về sự hợp tác cộng đồng

**Ví dụ:**
- She grew up in a small town near the coast, far from the big city.
- He is out of town this week on a business trip.
- Let's go into town this afternoon to do some shopping.
- The town has a charming market square where locals gather on weekends.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- go into town (ra trung tâm), out of town (vắng mặt/xa trung tâm)
- a small/quiet/busy town
- town center (trung tâm thị trấn), hometown (quê hương)
- paint the town red (ăn chơi thâu đêm — thành ngữ)

---

### 5. village *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** làng, thôn

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a village / villages

**Lưu ý:**
- "Village" nhỏ hơn "town" nhỏ hơn "city"
- "Global village" = thế giới được kết nối như một làng (khái niệm của Marshall McLuhan)
- "It takes a village (to raise a child)" = thành ngữ về vai trò cộng đồng

**Ví dụ:**
- My grandparents live in a small fishing village by the sea.
- The village has a population of just 300 people and one small school.
- In today's world, social media has made us all part of a global village.
- The ancient village has preserved its traditional architecture for centuries.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- a small/remote/traditional village
- village life (cuộc sống làng quê), village hall (nhà văn hóa làng)
- fishing/farming village (làng chài/làng nông)
- global village (làng toàn cầu — McLuhan)

### 6. house *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** nhà (tòa nhà để ở)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a house / houses

**Lưu ý:**
- "House" (tòa nhà cụ thể) vs "home" (nơi cảm thấy thuộc về)
- "House music" (thể loại âm nhạc), "The House" = Quốc hội (the House of Representatives)
- "Playhouse", "storehouse", "greenhouse" — nhiều từ ghép quan trọng

**Ví dụ:**
- They spent three years saving money before they could buy a house.
- The house has three bedrooms, a living room, and a large garden.
- Come to my house for dinner on Saturday evening.
- The old house at the end of the street has been empty for many years.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- buy/sell/rent a house
- detached house (nhà độc lập), semi-detached house (nhà ghép đôi)
- house price (giá nhà), house hunting (tìm nhà)
- at my house / come to my house

### 7. home *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** nhà, tổ ấm (nơi thuộc về); (C) một ngôi nhà/nơi trú ẩn

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** home (U) / a home (C) / homes (C)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Home" cảm xúc hơn "house": "A house is not a home." (nhà không có hồn = không phải tổ ấm)
- "Go home" / "stay home" — KHÔNG dùng "to" trước "home" trong các cụm này
- "Nursing home" = nhà dưỡng lão; "children's home" = trại trẻ mồ côi

**Ví dụ:**
- After a long day, she was glad to finally be home.
- He has been working from home since the pandemic began.
- She made the small apartment feel like a real home with her decorations.
- Thousands of families lost their homes in the floods last year.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- at home (ở nhà — không mạo từ), go home, come home
- feel at home (cảm thấy thoải mái)
- home sweet home (nhà mình là nhất — thành ngữ)
- work from home (làm việc tại nhà)

---

### 8. room *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** phòng (C); không gian, chỗ (U)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a room (C) / room (U, không mạo từ) / rooms (C)

**Lưu ý:**
- Countable (phòng): "a hotel room", "the living room", "three rooms"
- Uncountable (không gian): "There is no room for your bag." / "Room for one more?"
- "There's no room for error" = không có chỗ cho sai lầm

**Ví dụ:**
- The hotel room was small but clean and comfortable.
- Can you move your car? There is no room for mine in the driveway.
- There is always room for improvement, no matter how good you are.
- She rented a room in a shared house while studying at university.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- bedroom, bathroom, living room, dining room (các loại phòng)
- room for improvement (còn chỗ để cải thiện — U)
- make room for (nhường chỗ cho), standing room only
- there is room (có chỗ — U)

---

### 9. office *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** văn phòng; chức vụ (in office)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** an office / offices

**Lưu ý:**
- "In office" = đang giữ chức vụ (không mạo từ): "The president is in office for four years."
- "Box office" = quầy vé, hoặc doanh thu phim: "a box office hit"
- "Office" cũng là phần mềm: Microsoft Office

**Ví dụ:**
- She left the office early because she had a doctor's appointment.
- The president has been in office for two years and is very popular.
- The new blockbuster film broke all box office records in its first week.
- The company's head office is located in the center of the capital city.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- head office (trụ sở chính), branch office (văn phòng chi nhánh)
- at the office (ở văn phòng), office hours (giờ làm việc)
- box office (phòng vé rạp chiếu phim)
- take/hold office (đảm nhận/giữ chức vụ)

### 10. school *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** trường học (C); hoạt động học/dạy (U)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a school (C) / school (U) / schools (C)

**Lưu ý:**
- Uncountable (hoạt động): "She goes to school every day." / "I learned this at school."
- Countable (cơ sở): "The school was built in 1970." / "There are three schools in this area."
- Tương tự: "hospital", "prison", "church", "university" — không mạo từ khi chỉ mục đích

**Ví dụ:**
- She goes to school by bicycle every morning.
- The school was founded in 1945 and has educated generations of students.
- There are many different schools of thought on how to learn a language.
- The children finish school at 4 PM and then go to after-school activities.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- go to school (đi học — U), at school (đang ở trường/đang học)
- primary/secondary school, school of thought (trường phái tư tưởng)
- after school (sau giờ học), school year (năm học)
- the school building (tòa nhà trường — C)

### 11. hospital *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** bệnh viện

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a hospital (C) / hospital (U, British) / hospitals (C)

**Lưu ý:**
- British: "She is in hospital." (đang điều trị, không mạo từ)
- American: "She is in the hospital." (dùng "the")
- "Hospital" tương tự "school" — không mạo từ khi chỉ mục đích/chức năng

**Ví dụ:**
- He was rushed to hospital after the accident and needed emergency surgery.
- The new hospital has the most advanced medical equipment in the region.
- She spent three days in hospital recovering from a minor operation.
- Volunteers regularly visit patients who have no family to come to the hospital.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- go to hospital (British = đi nhập viện)
- in hospital (British) / in the hospital (American)
- hospital ward (khu bệnh phòng), hospital staff
- leave hospital (xuất viện), admit to hospital (nhập viện)

### 12. market *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** chợ, thị trường

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a market / markets

**Lưu ý:**
- "Market" có hai nghĩa chính: chợ (nơi mua bán) và thị trường (kinh tế)
- "On the market" = đang rao bán: "The house has been on the market for months."
- "Black market" = chợ đen (buôn bán bất hợp pháp)

**Ví dụ:**
- She goes to the local market every Saturday to buy fresh vegetables.
- The stock market fell sharply after the news of the economic downturn.
- The company needs to do more market research before launching the product.
- Street food vendors set up their stalls at the night market every evening.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- stock market (thị trường chứng khoán)
- go to the market (đi chợ)
- market research (nghiên cứu thị trường)
- on the market (đang bán/rao bán)
- job market / housing market

---

### 13. store *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** cửa hàng (American English); kho dự trữ

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a store / stores

**Lưu ý:**
- American English: "store"; British English: "shop" (nhỏ) / "store" (lớn hơn)
- "In store" (không mạo từ) = sắp xảy ra: "What's in store for us?"
- "Store" cũng là động từ: "to store data/food" (lưu trữ)

**Ví dụ:**
- I need to stop by the grocery store on my way home from work.
- The department store is having a big sale this weekend.
- Nobody knows what the future has in store for this technology.
- The online store offers free delivery for orders over fifty dollars.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- department store (cửa hàng bách hóa), grocery store (cửa hàng tạp hóa)
- online store (cửa hàng trực tuyến)
- in store (đang chờ — thành ngữ: "What's in store?")
- store manager (quản lý cửa hàng)

### 14. restaurant *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** nhà hàng

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a restaurant / restaurants

**Lưu ý:**
- Phát âm: /ˈrɛstrɒnt/ (British) hoặc /ˈrɛstərɑːnt/ (American) — đừng phát âm tất cả các âm tiết
- "Dine out" = ăn ngoài nhà hàng (trang trọng hơn "eat out")
- "Restaurant" gốc Pháp nghĩa là nơi phục hồi/bổ dưỡng

**Ví dụ:**
- They went to a nice Italian restaurant to celebrate their promotion.
- The restaurant has excellent reviews online and is always fully booked.
- She has always dreamed of opening her own Vietnamese restaurant abroad.
- The new restaurant downtown serves traditional dishes with a modern twist.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- go to a restaurant (đến nhà hàng)
- a fancy/casual/local restaurant
- restaurant owner, restaurant review
- fast-food restaurant (nhà hàng thức ăn nhanh)

### 15. hotel *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** khách sạn

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a hotel / hotels

**Lưu ý:**
- "Hotel" bắt đầu bằng "H" nhưng có âm /h/ — nên dùng "a hotel" (không phải "an hotel")
- Trong số ít trường hợp British cũ, người ta nói "an hotel" vì "h" câm — nhưng ngày nay "a hotel" là chuẩn
- "Motel" = motor hotel (khách sạn ven đường cho xe hơi)

**Ví dụ:**
- They checked into a five-star hotel near the beach for their honeymoon.
- The hotel provides free shuttle service to and from the airport.
- Please check out of the hotel by 11 AM on the day of your departure.
- The hotel lobby was decorated with tropical plants and a large fountain.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- check in/out of a hotel (nhận/trả phòng)
- book/stay at a hotel (đặt/ở khách sạn)
- a five-star hotel (khách sạn 5 sao)
- hotel lobby (sảnh khách sạn), hotel room

---

### 16. airport *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** sân bay

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** an airport / airports

**Lưu ý:**
- "Airport" = "air" + "port" — cảng hàng không
- "Terminal" = nhà ga sân bay (có thể có nhiều terminal trong một airport)
- "Airfield" (nhỏ hơn) vs "airport" (có đầy đủ dịch vụ)

**Ví dụ:**
- We need to leave for the airport at least three hours before the flight.
- Noi Bai International Airport is the main gateway to Hanoi.
- His parents came to the airport to see him off before his flight to Japan.
- The new terminal at the airport can handle millions of passengers each year.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- go to the airport, pick someone up from the airport
- airport security (an ninh sân bay), airport lounge
- international airport (sân bay quốc tế)
- airport transfer (dịch vụ đưa đón sân bay)

### 17. station *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** ga (tàu/xe buýt); đồn (cảnh sát/cứu hỏa); kênh (TV/radio)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a station / stations

**Lưu ý:**
- "Station" có rất nhiều loại: train, bus, police, fire, petrol, TV, space station...
- "Space station" = trạm vũ trụ (ISS = International Space Station)
- "Station" cũng là động từ: "to be stationed somewhere" = đóng quân/làm việc ở đâu đó

**Ví dụ:**
- The train station is just a ten-minute walk from the hotel.
- He went to the police station to report the stolen bicycle.
- She was stationed in Singapore for two years before returning to Vietnam.
- The new bus station in the city center serves over 50 different routes.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- train/bus station (ga tàu/xe buýt)
- police station (đồn cảnh sát), fire station (trạm cứu hỏa)
- petrol/gas station (trạm xăng)
- TV/radio station (kênh truyền hình/đài phát thanh)

## Ôn tập nhanh (Quick Review)
Điền từ thích hợp vào chỗ trống:

1. The ceremony will _______ place on the main stage at 7 PM. (take / make)
2. She is in _______ recovering from an operation. (hospital / school)
3. They checked _______ of the hotel by 11 AM. (out / in)
4. There is no _______ for improvement — she is perfect! (room / place)
5. He went to the police _______ to report the theft. (station / office)
6. The stock _______ fell sharply after the bad news. (market / store)
7. Go _______ — it is getting late! (home / house)
8. The _______ has been on the market for three months and hasn't sold. (house / home)

**Đáp án:** 1. take 2. hospital 3. out 4. room 5. station 6. market 7. home 8. house
