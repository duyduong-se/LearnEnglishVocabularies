### 18. tell *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** kể, nói với ai, bảo

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| tell | told | told |
| /tɛl/ | /toʊld/ | /toʊld/ |

**Lưu ý:**
- tell luôn cần tân ngữ người — khác "say" (không cần tân ngữ người)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

**[Động từ chính]**

tell + sb + sth → Nói/kể cho ai điều gì
- She told me a funny story.

tell + sb + (that) + clause → Nói với ai rằng...
- He told me that the meeting was cancelled.

tell + sb + to + V → Bảo ai làm gì
- The doctor told him to rest.

**[Câu phủ định & câu hỏi]**

S + didn't + tell + sb + ... → Phủ định
- She didn't tell me about it.

Who told + S + ...? → Hỏi ai nói
- Who told you that?

### 19. ask *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** hỏi, yêu cầu

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| ask | asked | asked |
| /æsk/ | /æskt/ | /æskt/ |

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

**[Động từ chính]**

ask + sb + [question] → Hỏi ai điều gì
- She asked me where I was from.

ask + sb + to + V → Nhờ/yêu cầu ai làm gì
- He asked me to help him move.

ask + for + [noun] → Xin/yêu cầu cái gì

**[Câu phủ định & câu hỏi]**

S + didn't + ask + ... → Phủ định
- She didn't ask for help.

Why didn't + S + ask + ...? → Câu hỏi
- Why didn't you ask me earlier?

### 20. seem *(verb — regular)*

**[Động từ chính]**

### 22. try *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** cố gắng, thử

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| try | tried | tried |
| /traɪ/ | /traɪd/ | /traɪd/ |

**Lưu ý:**
- try + to V → cố gắng làm (chưa chắc thành công)
- try + V-ing → thử làm xem hiệu quả không

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

**[Động từ chính]**

try + to + V → Cố gắng làm gì
- I'll try to finish it today.

try + V-ing → Thử làm gì xem sao
- Try drinking more water if you feel tired.

try + [noun] → Thử cái gì
- Have you tried this café?

**[Câu phủ định & câu hỏi]**

S + didn't + try + ... → Phủ định
- I tried but it didn't work.

Did + S + try + ...? → Câu hỏi
- Did you try calling her?

### 23. leave *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** rời đi, để lại

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| leave | left | left |
| /liːv/ | /lɛft/ | /lɛft/ |

**Lưu ý:**
- leave có hai nghĩa: rời đi (leave the room) và để lại (leave a message)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

**[Động từ chính]**

leave + [place] → Rời khỏi nơi nào
- I left home at 7 this morning.

leave + [noun] → Để lại cái gì
- She left a note on the table.

leave + sb/sth + [adj] → Để ai/cái gì trong trạng thái nào
- Don't leave the door open.

**[Câu phủ định & câu hỏi]**

S + didn't + leave + ... → Phủ định
- He didn't leave a message.

When did + S + leave + ...? → Hỏi khi nào rời đi
- When did you leave the office?

### 26. let *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** để, cho phép

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| let | let | let |
| /lɛt/ | /lɛt/ | /lɛt/ |

**Lưu ý:**
- let + sb + V (bare infinitive, không có "to")

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

**[Động từ chính]**

let + sb + V → Cho phép ai làm gì
- She let me borrow her bike.

let + it + go → Buông bỏ, thả ra

S + won't + let + sb + V → Phủ định

Will + S + let + sb + V? → Câu hỏi

### 27. begin *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** bắt đầu

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| begin | began | begun |

**Lưu ý:**
- cả ba dạng đều khác nhau
- begin + to V hoặc begin + V-ing đều đúng

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

begin + to + V → Bắt đầu làm gì
- She began to understand the importance of time management.
- He began to realize he had made a mistake.

begin + V-ing → Bắt đầu làm gì (cùng nghĩa)
- The school year begins in September in most countries.
- It began raining just as we reached the park.

begin + [noun] → Bắt đầu cái gì
- The construction project will begin next Monday after the permits are approved.
- They began the meeting with a brief review of last week's decisions.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- begin a journey, begin work, begin a conversation, begin from scratch, begin afresh

### 28. show *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** chỉ, cho xem, trình bày

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| show | showed | shown |

**Lưu ý:**
- V3 là "shown" (cũng chấp nhận "showed" trong một số ngữ cảnh)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

show + sb + sth → Chỉ/cho ai đó xem cái gì
- I will show you how to operate the new software at the training session.
- She showed her students the correct way to format a report.

show + [noun] → Trình bày, thể hiện
- He showed great courage during the emergency.
- The team showed remarkable improvement over the past month.

show + that + clause → Chứng tỏ rằng...
- The data clearly shows an increase in productivity this quarter.
- Research shows that regular breaks improve focus.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- show interest, show respect, show results, show evidence, show off

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- show up: xuất hiện → *He finally showed up an hour late.*
- show off: khoe khoang → *She always shows off her new gadgets.*
- show around: dẫn tham quan → *Let me show you around the office.*

### 29. hear *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** nghe, nghe nói

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| hear | heard | heard |

**Lưu ý:**
- heard phát âm là /hɜːrd/
- "hear" là passive (nghe thấy không chủ ý), khác "listen" (lắng nghe chủ ý)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

hear + [noun] → Nghe thấy cái gì
- I can't hear you clearly — please speak up a bit.
- Did you hear that loud noise coming from outside?

hear + sb + V/V-ing → Nghe thấy ai làm gì
- She heard that the company was planning to expand overseas.
- I heard him talking on the phone late at night.

hear + (that) + clause → Nghe nói rằng...
- We will hear from the interview panel within two weeks.
- I heard that they are opening a new branch downtown.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- hear the news, hear a sound, hear from someone, hear about something, hear clearly

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- hear from: nhận tin từ → *I haven't heard from him in weeks.*
- hear out: nghe hết ý kiến → *Please hear me out before judging.*

### 30. play *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** chơi, diễn, biểu diễn

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| play | played | played |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -ed (regular)
- play + thể thao/game: không dùng mạo từ (play football, play chess)
- play + nhạc cụ: dùng "the" (play the guitar, play the piano)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

play + [sport/game] → Chơi thể thao/trò chơi (không mạo từ)
- The children play in the park every afternoon after school.
- He plays basketball with his colleagues every Friday evening.

play + the + [instrument] → Chơi nhạc cụ
- She played the piano at the school concert last Friday.
- My sister has been playing the violin since she was five.

play + a role/part → Đóng vai trò
- She played a key role in the success of the project.
- Technology plays an important role in modern education.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- play football/chess, play the guitar, play a role, play fair, play a trick

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- play around: đùa giỡn, chơi loanh quanh → *Stop playing around and get serious.*
- play down: giảm nhẹ tầm quan trọng → *He played down his achievements.*

### 31. run *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** chạy, điều hành, vận hành

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| run | ran | run |

**Lưu ý:**
- V3 cùng dạng V1
- run có nghĩa mở rộng: điều hành (run a company), vận hành (run a program)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

run + [distance/direction] → Chạy bao xa/theo hướng nào
- She runs 5 kilometers every morning to stay fit.
- The children ran across the field laughing.

run + [noun] → Điều hành, vận hành cái gì
- He ran his own restaurant for over fifteen years.
- She runs a successful online business from home.

run + out of → Hết, cạn kiệt
- The new software update will run automatically overnight.
- We ran out of coffee this morning before the meeting.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- run a business, run a risk, run out of time, run smoothly, run for office

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- run out of: cạn hết → *We ran out of coffee this morning.*
- run into: tình cờ gặp → *I ran into an old classmate downtown.*
- run away: chạy trốn → *The cat ran away when it heard the noise.*

### 32. move *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** di chuyển, dọn nhà, cảm động

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| move | moved | moved |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -d (regular)
- move cũng có nghĩa cảm xúc: "I was moved by the story" (cảm động)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

move + to/from + place → Chuyển đến/từ nơi nào
- They move frequently because of the father's job assignments.
- Our company will move to a larger office in the city center next year.

move + [noun] → Di chuyển cái gì
- Please move your car — it's blocking the entrance.
- He moved the boxes to the storage room.

be moved + by → Bị xúc động bởi
- The speech moved many audience members to tears.
- She was deeply moved by the kindness of strangers.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- move forward, move quickly, move house, make a move, move on

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- move on: tiếp tục, bỏ qua → *It's time to move on from the past.*
- move in: dọn vào → *They moved in to their new apartment last week.*
- move out: dọn ra → *She moved out when she started college.*

### 33. live *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** sống, sinh sống

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| live | lived | lived |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -d (regular)
- live /lɪv/ (động từ) khác live /laɪv/ (tính từ: trực tiếp)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

live + in/at + place → Sống ở đâu
- My grandparents live in a quiet village in the countryside.
- She has lived in this city for over ten years.

live + [adverb] → Sống như thế nào (live well, live simply)
- He lived in Paris for three years while studying architecture.
- They try to live simply and avoid unnecessary expenses.

live + to + [age] → Sống đến bao nhiêu tuổi
- I hope to live in a country with a clean environment in the future.
- Her grandmother lived to be ninety-two years old.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- live alone, live together, live in peace, live a healthy life, live up to expectations

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- live up to: sống xứng với, đáp ứng kỳ vọng → *He lived up to his reputation.*
- live on: sống nhờ vào → *She lives on a tight budget.*

### 34. believe *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** tin, tin tưởng

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| believe | believed | believed |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -d (regular)
- là stative verb, không dùng trong tiếp diễn
- believe in + noun (tin vào điều gì)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

believe + (that) + clause → Tin rằng...
- I believe that honesty is the best policy in all situations.
- Many experts believe that the economy will recover by next year.

believe + in + [noun] → Tin vào cái gì/ai
- She believed in her team's ability even when others doubted them.
- You need to believe in yourself to achieve your goals.

be believed + to + V → Được cho là...
- The ancient city is believed to be over 3,000 years old.
- He is widely believed to be one of the best in his field.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- believe strongly, believe firmly, believe in oneself, widely believed, hard to believe

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- believe in: tin tưởng vào → *You need to believe in yourself.*
