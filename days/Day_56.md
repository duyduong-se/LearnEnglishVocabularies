## Từ học hôm nay

### 1. film *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** phim (C); lớp màng mỏng / cuộn phim (U)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a film / films (C); film (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- BrE: "film" = bộ phim; AmE: thường dùng "movie"
- "film" (U) = lớp màng mỏng: "a film of grease on the surface"
- "be filmed" = được quay phim: "The documentary was filmed over three years."

**Ví dụ:**
- The film won three Academy Awards, including Best Picture and Best Director.
- She made her directorial debut with a low-budget film that became a surprise hit.
- There was a thin film of ice on the road, making driving extremely dangerous.
- The film festival attracts directors and actors from over fifty countries every year.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- make / direct / star in a film, film festival
- silent film, documentary film, film industry
- a film of dust/oil (lớp bụi/dầu mỏng)

---

### 2. show *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** chương trình (TV/sân khấu); buổi biểu diễn; cuộc triển lãm

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a show / shows

**Lưu ý:**
- "The show must go on." = Dù gì đi nữa cũng phải tiếp tục (câu nói nổi tiếng)
- "a show of hands" = giơ tay biểu quyết
- "show off" (động từ) = khoe khoang

**Ví dụ:**
- The cooking show has been running for fifteen seasons and remains hugely popular.
- The annual fashion show attracted designers and buyers from over thirty countries.
- Despite the lead actor falling ill, the director insisted the show must go on.
- Raise your hand if you agree — a show of hands, please.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- TV show, talk show, game show, road show
- put on a show, the show must go on
- fashion show, art show, show of hands

---

### 3. game *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** trò chơi; trận đấu; thú săn bắt (U)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a game / games (C); game (U, = thú rừng để săn/ăn)

**Lưu ý:**
- "game" (U) = thú rừng để săn bắt/ăn thịt: a game reserve (khu bảo tồn)
- "mind games" = trò chơi tâm lý (thao túng tinh thần)
- "fair game" = mục tiêu chính đáng để chỉ trích: "Politicians are fair game for satire."

**Ví dụ:**
- They spent the evening playing board games by candlelight during the power cut.
- The team had a clear game plan and executed it perfectly throughout the match.
- Video games have become one of the largest entertainment industries in the world.
- The restaurant specialises in game — dishes made from wild deer and pheasant.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- board game, video game, card game, mind game
- play a game, win / lose a game, game over
- fair game, game plan

### 4. concert *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** buổi hòa nhạc; buổi biểu diễn âm nhạc

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a concert / concerts

**Lưu ý:**
- "in concert (with)" = hợp tác, phối hợp (nghĩa bóng): "The departments are working in concert."
- "concert hall" = nhà hát lớn (cho hòa nhạc)
- Phân biệt: concert (buổi hòa nhạc) vs. gig (buổi diễn — informal, thường nhạc rock/pop)

**Ví dụ:**
- She attended a classical music concert at the famous Carnegie Hall in New York.
- The band announced a world concert tour celebrating their 30th anniversary.
- The government is working in concert with international organisations to address the crisis.
- The outdoor concert was enjoyed by thousands of people despite the cold weather.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- attend / go to a concert, rock concert, classical concert
- live concert, concert hall, concert tour
- in concert (with sb)

---

### 5. event *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** sự kiện; cuộc thi (thể thao)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** an event / events

**Lưu ý:**
- "in the event of" = trong trường hợp xảy ra: "In the event of fire, use the stairs."
- "in any event" = dù sao thì: "In any event, we need a backup plan."
- "current events" = thời sự hiện tại

**Ví dụ:**
- The Olympics is one of the world's most watched sporting events.
- In the event of an emergency, please remain calm and follow the instructions.
- She works in event management, organising conferences and corporate functions.
- The charity gala was a major fundraising event that raised over a million dollars.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- major / key event, sporting event, current events
- in the event of, in any event
- host / organise / attend an event, event management

---

### 6. festival *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** lễ hội; liên hoan

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a festival / festivals

**Lưu ý:**
- Phân biệt: festival (lễ hội — thường lặp đi lặp lại hàng năm) vs. event (sự kiện — có thể một lần)
- "festive" (adj) = mang không khí lễ hội: "the festive season" (mùa Giáng sinh)
- "festivity" (n) = sự vui mừng, hoạt động lễ hội

**Ví dụ:**
- The Cannes Film Festival is one of the most prestigious events in the film world.
- Millions of people visit Vietnam every year to experience its colourful festivals.
- The music festival was cancelled due to extreme weather conditions.
- The whole town was decorated and filled with festive spirit during the annual harvest festival.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- film festival, music festival, food festival
- attend / hold / celebrate a festival
- annual festival, cultural festival, traditional festival

---

### 7. ceremony *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** lễ; buổi lễ; nghi lễ

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a ceremony / ceremonies

**Lưu ý:**
- "without ceremony" = không cầu kỳ, không màu mè: "He left without ceremony."
- Phân biệt: ceremony (nghi lễ chính thức) vs. ritual (nghi thức — thường tôn giáo/văn hóa) vs. celebration (lễ kỷ niệm vui vẻ)
- "stand on ceremony" = quá cầu kỳ về nghi thức (thường dùng với "don't")

**Ví dụ:**
- The graduation ceremony was held in the university's grand auditorium.
- The opening ceremony of the Olympic Games is watched by billions of people worldwide.
- He packed up his belongings and left without ceremony, closing the door quietly behind him.
- The traditional tea ceremony is an important part of Japanese culture.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- wedding ceremony, graduation ceremony, opening ceremony
- hold / attend a ceremony, ceremony takes place
- without ceremony

---

### 8. speech *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** bài phát biểu (C); lời nói / khả năng nói (U)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a speech / speeches (C); speech (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "freedom of speech" = tự do ngôn luận (U) — không dùng "a freedom of speech"
- "parts of speech" = từ loại (noun, verb, adj...) — thuật ngữ ngữ pháp
- "acceptance speech" = bài phát biểu nhận giải

**Ví dụ:**
- The president gave a powerful speech that moved the entire nation to tears.
- Her acceptance speech at the awards ceremony was witty, warm, and deeply personal.
- Freedom of speech is a fundamental right in any truly democratic society.
- His speech was so inspiring that the entire audience gave him a standing ovation.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- give / make / deliver a speech, freedom of speech
- keynote speech, acceptance speech, best man speech
- speech therapy, speech impediment, parts of speech

---

### 9. interview *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** buổi phỏng vấn (xin việc/báo chí)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** an interview / interviews

**Lưu ý:**
- "interview" còn là động từ: "The police interviewed three suspects."
- "interviewee" = người được phỏng vấn; "interviewer" = người phỏng vấn
- "at interview" (BrE, không mạo từ): "Questions asked at interview..."

**Ví dụ:**
- She prepared extensively for the job interview by researching the company.
- The celebrity gave an exclusive interview to the magazine to promote her new book.
- The police interviewed all the witnesses before making any arrests.
- Good interview technique involves listening carefully and giving clear, structured answers.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- job interview, press interview, conduct an interview
- attend / give / do an interview, interview skills
- exclusive interview

---

### 10. debate *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** cuộc tranh luận (C); sự tranh cãi (U)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a debate / debates (C); debate (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "open to debate" = còn đang tranh cãi, chưa rõ ràng: "Whether he was right is still open to debate."
- "beyond debate" = không còn tranh cãi nữa: "The facts are beyond debate."
- "debate" còn là động từ: "I'm debating whether to go or not." (đang cân nhắc)

**Ví dụ:**
- The two candidates faced each other in a live televised debate before the election.
- The new law sparked a heated debate about individual rights and government control.
- Whether social media does more harm than good is still very much open to debate.
- She enjoys taking part in debates and has a talent for constructing persuasive arguments.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- have / hold / open a debate, presidential debate
- spark a debate, join the debate, heated debate
- open to debate, subject of debate, beyond debate

---

### 11. advertisement *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** quảng cáo; thông báo tuyển dụng

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** an advertisement / advertisements (thường viết tắt: ad/advert)

**Lưu ý:**
- Viết tắt: advertisement → ad (AmE) / advert (BrE) — cả hai rất phổ biến
- "advertising" (U) = ngành quảng cáo; "advertisement" (C) = một mẩu quảng cáo
- "a walking advertisement for sth" = người thể hiện rõ điều gì (sống động)

**Ví dụ:**
- The advertisement went viral because of its clever humour and emotional message.
- She placed a job advertisement in the local newspaper and received fifty applications.
- Her success story is a walking advertisement for the benefits of regular exercise.
- The company spent millions on TV advertisements to launch their new product range.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- place / run an advertisement, TV advertisement
- job advertisement, classified advertisement
- advertisement for sth, advertising campaign

---

### 12. channel *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** kênh (truyền hình/truyền thông); kênh đào; đường dẫn

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a channel / channels

**Lưu ý:**
- "channel surfing" = chuyển kênh liên tục (không biết xem gì)
- "through the proper channels" = qua các kênh chính thức, theo quy trình
- "channel" còn là động từ: "channel your energy into sth" (dồn sức vào)

**Ví dụ:**
- She started a YouTube channel about cooking and quickly gained millions of subscribers.
- If you have a complaint, please raise it through the proper channels.
- The English Channel separates England from the coast of France.
- He channelled all his frustration into his music, writing his best album yet.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- TV channel, YouTube channel, communication channel
- through official/proper channels
- the English Channel, channel surfing

---

### 13. episode *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** tập (phim/podcast); sự kiện; cơn bệnh

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** an episode / episodes

**Lưu ý:**
- Ba nghĩa: (1) tập phim/podcast, (2) một giai đoạn/sự kiện trong cuộc đời, (3) cơn bệnh: "an episode of depression"
- "binge-watch" = xem liên tục nhiều tập một lúc (Netflix marathons)
- "a dark episode" = một giai đoạn đen tối trong lịch sử/cuộc đời

**Ví dụ:**
- I stayed up until 3 a.m. binge-watching the entire first season in one night.
- This was the best episode of the series so far — full of unexpected twists.
- He suffered several episodes of severe anxiety before seeking professional help.
- The pilot episode of the new drama attracted record viewing figures for the channel.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- watch an episode, the latest episode, binge-watch episodes
- an episode of depression/illness
- pilot episode
