## Từ học hôm nay

### 1. understand *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** hiểu

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| understand | understood | understood |

**Lưu ý:**
- Irregular — V2 và V3 giống nhau: understood

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

understand + noun/pronoun → hiểu điều gì đó
- I understand your point, but I disagree.
- She finally understood the concept after reviewing the notes.

understand + that-clause → hiểu rằng...
- I understand that this is a difficult situation for everyone.
- He understood that the deadline could not be extended.

understand + how/why/what → hiểu cách/lý do/điều gì
- You will understand the rules better after practice.
- She struggled to understand why the project had failed.

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- understand from: hiểu từ (nguồn thông tin) → *I understand from the report that sales dropped.*

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- understand the situation, understand each other, understand the concept

### 11. wish *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** ước

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| wish | wished | wished |

**Lưu ý:**
- Regular — thêm -ed
- "wish" dùng với past subjunctive để diễn đạt điều không có thật (wish + V2)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

wish + that + past tense → ước điều gì đó khác hiện thực
- I wish I could speak French fluently.
- She wishes she had more time to spend with her family.

wish + to-infinitive → muốn làm gì (formal)
- He will wish he had studied harder when exam results come out.
- They wish to remain anonymous in the published report.

wish + someone + noun → chúc ai đó điều gì
- She wished him a happy birthday.
- The manager wished the team good luck before the presentation.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- wish good luck, wish someone well, wish for happiness

### 16. prefer *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** thích hơn

**Lưu ý:**

### 17. miss *(verb — regular)*

**Lưu ý:**
