## Từ học hôm nay

### 1. contract *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** hợp đồng; bản giao kèo

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a contract / contracts

**Lưu ý:**
- Phân biệt: contract (hợp đồng có ràng buộc pháp lý) vs. agreement (thỏa thuận — có thể là miệng)
- "break a contract" = vi phạm hợp đồng; "under contract" = đang theo hợp đồng
- "contract" còn là động từ: to contract a disease (mắc bệnh) / to contract (co lại)

**Ví dụ:**
- Both parties signed the contract before the project officially began.
- The company won a major contract to supply equipment to the government.
- If you break the contract, you may be liable for significant financial penalties.
- He is currently under contract with the club for another two years.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- sign / break / renew a contract, contract of employment
- under contract, contract terms, fixed-term contract
- win / award a contract

---

### 2. report *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** báo cáo; bài tường thuật

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a report / reports

**Lưu ý:**
- "report" còn là động từ: report a crime / report to sb (báo cáo cho ai)
- "report card" = phiếu điểm (ở trường)
- "a glowing report" = bản báo cáo/nhận xét rất tích cực

**Ví dụ:**
- The annual report showed a 20% increase in company profits over the past year.
- The journalist filed a detailed report from the scene of the disaster.
- According to reports, the government is planning to introduce new environmental laws.
- The committee published a report recommending major reforms to the education system.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- annual report, progress report, financial report
- write / submit / publish a report, report findings
- according to reports, news report, report card

---

### 3. meeting *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** cuộc họp; cuộc gặp gỡ

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a meeting / meetings

**Lưu ý:**
- "call a meeting" = triệu tập cuộc họp; "chair a meeting" = điều hành cuộc họp
- "in a meeting" = đang trong cuộc họp (không thể tiếp chuyện)
- "minutes" của cuộc họp = biên bản ghi chép (không phải "phút")

**Ví dụ:**
- The board meeting lasted three hours due to disagreements over the new policy.
- I'm afraid she's in a meeting right now — can I take a message?
- The manager called an emergency meeting to address the sudden drop in sales.
- The secretary was asked to take the minutes at the weekly staff meeting.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- hold / attend / chair / cancel a meeting
- board meeting, team meeting, annual general meeting (AGM)
- call a meeting, meeting agenda, minutes of a meeting

---

### 4. presentation *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** bài thuyết trình; sự trình bày

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a presentation / presentations

**Lưu ý:**
- "presentation" còn có nghĩa là lễ trao phần thưởng/quà: a presentation ceremony
- "presentation skills" = kỹ năng thuyết trình — rất quan trọng trong môi trường công việc
- Phân biệt: presentation (thuyết trình có cấu trúc) vs. speech (bài phát biểu — thường không dùng slide)

**Ví dụ:**
- She practiced her presentation for hours before the important board meeting.
- His presentation on market trends impressed the investors so much that they signed the deal immediately.
- Strong presentation skills can significantly advance your career in any profession.
- The sales team gave a polished presentation to potential clients at the conference.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- give / make / deliver a presentation
- PowerPoint presentation, sales presentation
- presentation skills, presentation on a topic

---

### 5. conference *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** hội nghị; hội thảo

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a conference / conferences

**Lưu ý:**
- "press conference" = họp báo (rất phổ biến trong tin tức)
- "video conference" / "conference call" = họp/gọi điện trực tuyến nhiều người
- Phân biệt: conference (hội nghị lớn — nhiều người, nhiều ngày) vs. meeting (cuộc họp nhỏ hơn)

**Ví dụ:**
- She presented her research at an international conference in Tokyo.
- The president held a press conference to address the nation after the disaster.
- Many companies now hold weekly video conferences instead of requiring everyone to be in the office.
- The annual conference attracts thousands of professionals from across the industry.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- attend / hold / organise a conference, conference room
- press conference, video conference, trade conference
- annual conference, international conference

---

### 6. team *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** đội; nhóm

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a team / teams

**Lưu ý:**
- BrE: "The team are playing well." (số nhiều — coi team là tập hợp cá nhân)
- AmE: "The team is playing well." (số ít — coi team là đơn vị)
- "team player" = người biết làm việc nhóm, không cá nhân

**Ví dụ:**
- The project was a success thanks to the dedication and teamwork of the entire team.
- She's known as a great team player who always puts the group's needs before her own.
- The manager assembled a team of specialists to handle the new product launch.
- Building a strong team requires trust, clear communication, and shared goals.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- team player, team spirit, team effort
- join / lead / build a team, teamwork
- a team of experts/doctors/engineers

---

### 7. department *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** phòng ban; bộ (chính phủ)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a department / departments

**Lưu ý:**
- "department store" = cửa hàng bách hóa nhiều tầng (không liên quan đến phòng ban công ty)
- Các phòng ban phổ biến: HR (Human Resources), R&D (Research and Development), PR (Public Relations)
- "head of department" = trưởng phòng; viết tắt: HoD

**Ví dụ:**
- She transferred to the marketing department after three years in finance.
- The Department of Health announced new guidelines for managing the outbreak.
- I spent the whole afternoon at the department store trying to find a birthday gift.
- The HR department is responsible for recruiting and training new employees.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- finance / marketing / HR department, head of department
- government department, police department
- department store, department head, department meeting

---

### 8. competition *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** sự cạnh tranh (U); cuộc thi (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a competition / competitions (C); competition (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- competition (U): sự cạnh tranh nói chung — "Competition in the tech industry is fierce."
- a competition (C): một cuộc thi cụ thể — "She entered a cooking competition."
- "the competition" = tập thể đối thủ: "We need to know what the competition is doing."

**Ví dụ:**
- There is fierce competition among smartphone companies for market share.
- She entered an international photography competition and won first prize.
- Our new product is cheaper and better quality than anything the competition offers.
- The school holds a writing competition every year to encourage creative skills.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- enter / win / lose a competition, competition between
- fierce / stiff competition
- face competition, the competition (= đối thủ)

---

### 9. supply *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** nguồn cung; cung cấp; kho dự trữ

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a supply / supplies (C); supply (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "supply and demand" = cung và cầu (quy luật kinh tế cơ bản)
- "supply chain" = chuỗi cung ứng (supply chain disruption = gián đoạn chuỗi cung ứng)
- "in short supply" = khan hiếm, thiếu: "Masks were in short supply at the start of the pandemic."

**Ví dụ:**
- The price of oil is determined by supply and demand in global markets.
- Medical supplies were in short supply during the first wave of the pandemic.
- The company restructured its supply chain to reduce delivery times.
- The hikers carried a three-day supply of food and water for the trek.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- supply and demand, supply chain, in short supply
- medical supplies, office supplies, food supplies
- supply a product / service, oversupply / undersupply

---

### 10. demand *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** nhu cầu (U); yêu cầu (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a demand / demands (C); demand (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "in demand" = được ưa chuộng: "Skilled programmers are in high demand."
- "on demand" = khi cần, theo yêu cầu: "streaming on demand"
- "demands of the job" = những yêu cầu/áp lực của công việc

**Ví dụ:**
- There is a high demand for qualified nurses in the healthcare sector.
- The strikers made several demands, including higher wages and better working conditions.
- Streaming services allow viewers to watch any show on demand, at any time.
- The growing demand for electric vehicles is driving investment in battery technology.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- supply and demand, in (high) demand, meet demand
- make demands (on sb), demand for sth
- on demand, demands of the job

---

### 11. stock *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** cổ phiếu (C); hàng tồn kho (U)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a stock / stocks (C); stock (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "out of stock" = hết hàng; "in stock" = còn hàng — rất phổ biến khi mua sắm
- "take stock (of)" = đánh giá lại tình hình một cách thận trọng
- "stock up on" = tích trữ, mua nhiều: "stock up on food before the storm"

**Ví dụ:**
- The company's stock price rose sharply after the announcement of record profits.
- I'm afraid this model is currently out of stock — we expect new deliveries next week.
- After the difficult year, she took stock of her life and decided to make some changes.
- Many investors lost money when the stock market crashed in 2008.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- stock market, stock exchange, stock price
- in stock / out of stock, take stock (of)
- livestock, stock up on

---

### 12. market *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** thị trường; chợ

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a market / markets

**Lưu ý:**
- "on the market" = đang được bán: "The house has been on the market for three months."
- "corner the market" = độc chiếm thị trường
- "black market" = chợ đen (buôn bán bất hợp pháp)

**Ví dụ:**
- The company entered the Asian market with its new product line last year.
- The stock market fell sharply in response to the disappointing economic data.
- I bought this vintage lamp at a flea market for only a few dollars.
- Their new drug has been on the market for less than a year and is already a bestseller.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- stock market, job market, housing market
- enter / corner / dominate a market, market share
- on the market, black market, flea market

---

### 13. brand *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** thương hiệu; nhãn hiệu

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a brand / brands

**Lưu ý:**
- "brand new" = hoàn toàn mới tinh (không qua sử dụng): "a brand new car"
- "brand loyalty" = lòng trung thành thương hiệu
- "own-brand" / "store brand" = hàng nhãn hiệu riêng của siêu thị

**Ví dụ:**
- Apple is one of the most recognised and valuable brands in the world.
- She received a brand new laptop as a birthday gift from her parents.
- Building brand loyalty takes years of consistent quality and good customer service.
- The company spent millions on advertising to raise brand awareness in new markets.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- luxury brand, brand new, brand awareness, brand loyalty
- build / launch / promote a brand
- brand image, brand name, brand identity
