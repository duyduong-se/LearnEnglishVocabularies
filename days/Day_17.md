## Từ học hôm nay

### 1. pour *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** đổ

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| pour | poured | poured |

**Lưu ý:**
- Regular — thêm -ed

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

pour + noun + into/over → đổ cái gì vào/lên
- Could you pour me a glass of water, please?
- He poured the sauce over the pasta.

pour out → đổ ra / trút bày (cảm xúc)
- She poured out all her worries to her best friend.
- He poured out the remaining liquid from the container.

it's pouring (with rain) → trời đang đổ mưa
- It will pour with rain this afternoon, so bring an umbrella.
- It was pouring when we left the office, so we waited inside.

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- pour out: đổ ra / trút bày tâm sự → *She poured out all her feelings to her friend.*

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- pour water, pour concrete, pour your heart out

### 11. tear *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** xé

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| tear | tore | torn |

**Lưu ý:**
- Irregular — V2: tore, V3: torn
- Lưu ý phát âm: tear (xé) đọc là /tɛr/, khác với tear (nước mắt) đọc là /tɪr/

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

tear + noun → xé cái gì
- Please don't tear pages out of library books.
- He tore the letter in half and threw it away.

tear up → xé nhỏ / bị cảm xúc làm xúc động
- She tore up the contract after the negotiation broke down.
- The farewell speech tore him up inside — he could barely hold back tears.

tear apart → xé tan / chia rẽ
- They will tear down the old stadium to build a new one.
- The conflict threatened to tear the team apart.

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- tear up: xé nhỏ / xé vụn → *She tore up the letter without reading it.*
- tear down: phá bỏ → *They tore down the old building.*

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- tear paper, tear apart, tear into pieces
