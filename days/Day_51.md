## Từ học hôm nay

### 1. storm *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** bão; cơn giận dữ (nghĩa bóng)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a storm / storms

**Lưu ý:**
- "Weather a storm" = vượt qua giai đoạn khó khăn (thành ngữ)
- "A storm in a teacup" (BrE) = "a tempest in a teapot" (AmE) = chuyện nhỏ nhặt bị thổi phồng
- "Brainstorm" = động não, phát triển ý tưởng

**Ví dụ:**
- A violent storm swept through the coastal town, damaging hundreds of homes.
- The company managed to weather the financial storm and emerged stronger.
- The media controversy turned out to be a storm in a teacup — it was forgotten within a week.
- The team held a brainstorming session to generate new marketing ideas.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- thunderstorm, snowstorm, tropical storm
- weather a storm, brainstorm
- storm in a teacup, a storm of protest

### 2. snow *(noun — uncountable)*
**Nghĩa:** tuyết

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** snow / (không có số nhiều; "the snows" = những cơn tuyết — văn thơ)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Be snowed under (with work)" = bị ngập trong công việc: "I'm completely snowed under this week."
- "Snowball effect" = hiệu ứng quả cầu tuyết (tích lũy và phát triển nhanh)
- "Snowed in" = bị tuyết phong tỏa, không ra ngoài được

**Ví dụ:**
- Heavy snow fell overnight and covered the streets in a thick white blanket.
- I'm completely snowed under with work this month and can't take any time off.
- The children rushed outside to build a snowman as soon as the snow stopped.
- The village was snowed in for three days after the blizzard.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- heavy snow, fresh snow, snow falls / melts
- snowstorm, snowflake, snowman, snowball
- be snowed under

### 3. ice *(noun — uncountable)*
**Nghĩa:** băng; đá lạnh

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** ice / (không có số nhiều)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Break the ice" = phá vỡ bầu không khí ban đầu (giúp mọi người cảm thấy thoải mái)
- "On thin ice" = đang ở tình huống nguy hiểm/rủi ro
- "Black ice" = lớp băng mỏng trong suốt rất nguy hiểm trên đường

**Ví dụ:**
- The lake froze over completely, and children skated on the ice all winter.
- He told a funny joke to break the ice at the beginning of the meeting.
- You're on thin ice with the manager — one more mistake and you might lose your job.
- Drivers were warned about black ice on the roads after the overnight freeze.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- black ice, ice cap, ice cream
- break the ice
- on thin ice, cut no ice (with sb)

### 4. heat *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** nhiệt độ cao; sức nóng (U); vòng đấu loại (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a heat / heats (C); heat (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "If you can't stand the heat, get out of the kitchen." = Nếu không chịu được áp lực thì đừng làm (tục ngữ)
- "A dead heat" = cuộc đua/thi kết quả hòa hoàn toàn
- "Heat wave" = đợt nắng nóng kéo dài

**Ví dụ:**
- The heat wave last summer broke all previous temperature records in the region.
- In the heat of the argument, she said things she later regretted.
- She won her qualifying heat and advanced to the finals of the 100-meter race.
- The desert heat was so intense that the road appeared to shimmer.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- in the heat, heat wave, body heat, heat stroke
- turn up the heat, if you can't stand the heat...
- dead heat

### 5. light *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** ánh sáng (U); đèn (C); góc nhìn

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a light / lights (C); light (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Shed light on" = làm sáng tỏ, giải thích: "Research shed light on the mystery."
- "In a new light" = theo cách nhìn mới: "See sb in a new light."
- "Green light" = sự cho phép: "We got the green light for the project."

**Ví dụ:**
- Natural light is better for reading than artificial light from screens.
- The new evidence shed light on what really happened the night of the crime.
- We finally got the green light to proceed with the construction project.
- She saw her old friend in a new light after learning about his struggles.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- natural light, daylight, sunlight, traffic lights
- in a new light, green light
- shed / throw light on, come to light

### 6. energy *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** năng lượng (U); sức sống (U); loại năng lượng (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** an energy / energies (C); energy (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Renewable energy" = năng lượng tái tạo (gió, mặt trời...)
- "Energy drink" = đồ uống tăng lực
- "Channel your energies into" = dồn tâm sức vào: "She channeled her energies into her career."

**Ví dụ:**
- Solar energy is becoming cheaper and more widely used around the world.
- He was full of energy in the morning but completely exhausted by the evening.
- After the breakup, she channeled all her energies into building her own business.
- The government invested heavily in renewable energies to reduce carbon emissions.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- renewable energy, solar energy, nuclear energy
- have energy, lack energy, save / waste energy
- full of energy, energy crisis, energy drink

### 7. force *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** lực; sức mạnh (U); lực lượng (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a force / forces (C); force (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "By force" = bằng vũ lực (không có mạo từ)
- "A force of nature" = người cực kỳ mạnh mẽ/năng động
- "Force of habit" = thói quen đã ăn sâu: "I checked my phone by force of habit."

**Ví dụ:**
- The gravitational force of the Earth keeps the Moon in its orbit.
- She is a force of nature — unstoppable, passionate, and full of ideas.
- He reached for his phone by force of habit, even though he had decided to reduce screen time.
- The armed forces were deployed to maintain order during the crisis.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- armed forces, police force, workforce
- by force, force of habit, force of gravity
- driving force, force of nature

### 8. pressure *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** áp suất; áp lực (U); nguồn áp lực (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a pressure / pressures (C); pressure (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Peer pressure" = áp lực từ bạn bè cùng lứa
- "Under pressure" = dưới áp lực (không mạo từ)
- "Put pressure on sb" = gây áp lực lên ai

**Ví dụ:**
- High blood pressure is a silent condition that can lead to heart attacks.
- She performs best under pressure and thrives in competitive environments.
- Peer pressure pushed many teenagers into making choices they later regretted.
- The pressures of modern life can be overwhelming without proper support.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- blood pressure, air pressure, peer pressure
- under pressure, put pressure on, pressure to do sth
- high / low pressure, work under pressure

### 9. temperature *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** nhiệt độ; thân nhiệt

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a temperature / temperatures (C); temperature (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Have a temperature" = bị sốt (nhiệt độ cao hơn bình thường): "The child has a temperature."
- "Take sb's temperature" = đo nhiệt độ của ai
- "At room temperature" = ở nhiệt độ phòng

**Ví dụ:**
- The temperature dropped to minus 10 degrees Celsius overnight in the mountains.
- The nurse took the patient's temperature and found it was higher than normal.
- Store the medication at room temperature, away from direct sunlight.
- Rising global temperatures are linked to increased frequency of extreme weather.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- room temperature, body temperature, high/low temperature
- take sb's temperature, have a temperature
- temperature rises / drops / reaches

### 10. environment *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** môi trường tự nhiên (U, with "the"); môi trường cụ thể (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** an environment / environments (C); the environment (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "The environment" = môi trường tự nhiên (luôn có "the")
- "An environment" = môi trường làm việc/học tập: "a positive learning environment"
- "Environmentally friendly" = thân thiện với môi trường

**Ví dụ:**
- We all have a responsibility to protect the environment for future generations.
- A positive working environment greatly improves employee productivity.
- The factory was fined for releasing toxic waste that damaged the local environment.
- Children learn best in a safe and supportive environment.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- protect the environment, damage the environment
- working environment, learning environment, home environment
- environmental impact, environmental policy

### 11. climate *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** khí hậu (C/U); bầu không khí (C, nghĩa bóng)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a climate / climates (C); climate (U in "climate change")

**Lưu ý:**
- "Climate change" (không mạo từ) = biến đổi khí hậu — cụm danh từ cố định quan trọng
- Nghĩa bóng: "a climate of fear" = bầu không khí sợ hãi
- "Climate" vs. "weather": climate = xu hướng thời tiết dài hạn; weather = thời tiết hôm nay

**Ví dụ:**
- Climate change is causing more extreme weather events around the world.
- Vietnam has a tropical climate with distinct wet and dry seasons.
- The new management created a climate of fear that damaged staff morale.
- Countries with different climates have developed very different cuisines.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- climate change, climate crisis, climate action
- tropical climate, Mediterranean climate, harsh climate
- business climate, political climate, climate of fear

### 12. weather *(noun — uncountable)*
**Nghĩa:** thời tiết

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** weather / (không có số nhiều)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Under the weather" = không khỏe: "I'm feeling a bit under the weather today."
- "Weather permitting" = nếu thời tiết thuận lợi
- "Weather the storm" = vượt qua giai đoạn khó khăn

**Ví dụ:**
- The weather forecast says there will be thunderstorms this afternoon.
- I'm feeling a bit under the weather today, so I think I'll stay home.
- The outdoor event will be held weather permitting.
- The unpredictable weather made planning the outdoor wedding very stressful.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- good / bad / extreme weather, weather forecast
- weather permitting, in all weathers
- under the weather, weather the storm

### 13. pollution *(noun — uncountable)*
**Nghĩa:** ô nhiễm

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** pollution / (không có số nhiều)

**Lưu ý:**
- Phân biệt: pollution (ô nhiễm — danh từ trừu tượng U) vs. pollutant (chất gây ô nhiễm — C)
- "Pollute" (v), "pollutant" (n — C), "pollution" (n — U)
- Không dùng "a pollution" — sai

**Ví dụ:**
- Air pollution in major cities has reached dangerous levels in recent years.
- The government introduced strict laws to reduce water pollution from factories.
- Noise pollution from constant traffic makes it difficult for residents to sleep.
- Light pollution in cities prevents people from seeing the stars at night.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- air pollution, water pollution, noise pollution, light pollution
- cause / reduce / tackle pollution
- pollution levels, pollution control, environmental pollution

### 14. carbon *(noun — uncountable)*
**Nghĩa:** carbon; khí thải carbon

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** carbon / (không có số nhiều)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Carbon footprint" = lượng khí thải carbon cá nhân/tổ chức tạo ra
- "Carbon neutral" = không tạo ra thêm khí thải carbon ròng
- "Carbon dioxide" (CO₂) = khí carbon dioxide — nguyên nhân chính của biến đổi khí hậu

**Ví dụ:**
- Reducing carbon emissions is essential to slowing the effects of climate change.
- Many companies are trying to reduce their carbon footprint by using renewable energy.
- The country aims to become carbon neutral by 2050.
- Cars and factories are the biggest sources of carbon emissions in most countries.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- carbon dioxide (CO₂), carbon emissions, carbon footprint
- carbon neutral, carbon tax, carbon capture
- reduce your carbon footprint

### 15. oxygen *(noun — uncountable)*
**Nghĩa:** oxy

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** oxygen / (không có số nhiều)

**Lưu ý:**
- Ký hiệu hóa học: O₂ (phân tử oxy) / O (nguyên tử oxy)
- "Give sb oxygen" (nghĩa bóng) = tiếp thêm sức sống, cơ hội: "Media coverage gave oxygen to the campaign."
- Trees produce oxygen through photosynthesis

**Ví dụ:**
- The brain begins to die within minutes if deprived of oxygen.
- The patient was given an oxygen mask to help him breathe after the accident.
- Trees and plants produce oxygen, making forests vital for life on Earth.
- Climbers at high altitudes often need supplemental oxygen to breathe properly.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- oxygen supply, oxygen tank, oxygen level
- oxygen mask, oxygen therapy
- need oxygen to survive, low oxygen levels

### 16. plant *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** cây (thực vật); nhà máy; bằng chứng giả

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a plant / plants

**Lưu ý:**
- Ba nghĩa: (1) cây (thực vật), (2) nhà máy: "a power plant", (3) đặt thứ gì bí mật: "The police planted evidence."
- "Plant-based" = có nguồn gốc thực vật (xu hướng ăn uống hiện đại)
- "Plant" cũng là động từ: "to plant seeds/trees"

**Ví dụ:**
- She filled her apartment with indoor plants to improve air quality and mood.
- The chemical plant was shut down after it was found to be polluting the river.
- A plant-based diet has been linked to lower rates of heart disease.
- The gardener planted a row of flowers along the garden path.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- power plant, manufacturing plant, indoor plant
- water / grow / plant a plant, plant life
- plant-based (diet)

### 17. tree *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** cây (cây gỗ lớn)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a tree / trees

**Lưu ý:**
- "Family tree" = cây gia phả (sơ đồ phả hệ gia đình)
- "Up a tree" (AmE) = trong tình huống khó xử
- "Can't see the wood/forest for the trees" = không thấy bức tranh lớn vì tập trung vào chi tiết nhỏ

**Ví dụ:**
- A large oak tree stood in the centre of the garden for over three hundred years.
- She drew her family tree for a school project and discovered ancestors from different countries.
- We need to plant more trees in cities to combat air pollution and rising temperatures.
- Sometimes we can't see the wood for the trees when we get too focused on details.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- family tree, tree trunk, tree branch
- plant a tree, climb a tree, chop down a tree
- decision tree, tree house
