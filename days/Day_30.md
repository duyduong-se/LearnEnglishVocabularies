### 1. broadcast *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** phát sóng (radio, TV)

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| broadcast | broadcast | broadcast |

**Lưu ý:**
- Irregular — V1/V2/V3 giống nhau: broadcast

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

broadcast + program/news/live → phát sóng chương trình/tin tức/trực tiếp
- The news is broadcast every evening at seven o'clock.
- The match was broadcast live to over 50 countries.

be broadcast on + channel → được phát trên kênh nào
- The station will broadcast the concert for free this weekend.
- The documentary was broadcast on national television last night.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- broadcast live, broadcast nationwide, broadcast a program
