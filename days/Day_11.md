### 168. transfer *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** chuyển, chuyển giao

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| transfer | transferred | transferred |

**Lưu ý:**
- gấp đôi r trước -ed (regular)
- transfer + [noun] + to (chuyển cái gì đến)
- /ˈtrænsfər/ (noun) vs /trænsˈfər/ (verb)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

transfer + [noun] + to → Chuyển cái gì đến
- He transferred all his files to the new computer before the old one crashed.
- She transferred the funds to the supplier's account before the deadline.

be transferred + to → Được chuyển đến
- She was transferred to the Singapore office to lead the regional expansion.
- He was transferred to the night shift due to staffing needs.

transfer + funds/data → Chuyển tiền/dữ liệu
- We will transfer the remaining project budget to the new account next week.
- Data is transferred securely between servers using encryption protocols.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- transfer data, transfer money, transfer knowledge, transfer ownership, bank transfer

### 173. contract *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** thu hẹp, co lại, ký hợp đồng

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| contract | contracted | contracted |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -ed (regular)
- contract /kənˈtrækt/ (verb: co rút, ký) vs /ˈkɒntrækt/ (noun: hợp đồng)
- contract + a disease (mắc bệnh)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

contract + [disease] → Mắc bệnh
- He contracted a serious illness while working in the tropics.
- She contracted food poisoning after eating at the restaurant.

contract + with/to → Ký hợp đồng với
- The company contracted with a local supplier to reduce delivery costs.
- They contracted with a well-known design firm to rebrand their products.

[muscles/materials] + contract → Co lại
- Metal expands in heat and contracts in cold temperatures.
- During the recession, the retail market contracted by approximately 15%.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- contract a disease, contract a muscle, contract the market, contract work, awarded a contract

### 174. decrease *(verb — regular)*

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| decrease | decreased | decreased |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -d (regular)
- /dɪˈkriːs/ (verb) vs /ˈdiːkriːs/ (noun)
- decrease + in

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

decrease + by + [amount] → Giảm xuống bao nhiêu
- Crime rates in the city have decreased by 20% since the new measures were introduced.

- Absenteeism decreased from 10% to just 4% following the wellness program.

decrease + in + [noun] → Giảm về mặt nào
- Renewable energy adoption will help decrease dependence on fossil fuels.
- There has been a noticeable decrease in customer complaints this quarter.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- decrease significantly, decrease gradually, decrease in value, decrease spending, decrease pollution
