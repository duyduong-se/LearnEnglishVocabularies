## Từ học hôm nay

### 1. hire *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** thuê (nhân viên)

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| hire | hired | hired |

**Lưu ý:**
- Regular — thêm -d (vì tận cùng bằng -e)
- Phân biệt: hire = thuê người; rent = thuê đồ vật/bất động sản

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

hire + someone → thuê ai
- We hire new staff every quarter to meet demand.
- The startup hired experienced developers to build their product.

hire + someone + to-infinitive → thuê ai làm gì
- They will hire ten engineers for the upcoming project.
- She hired a professional photographer to document the event.

be hired as → được thuê với vị trí
- The company hired her as their new marketing director.
- He was hired as a consultant to oversee the digital transformation.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- hire staff, hire a consultant, hire and fire

### 2. fire *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** sa thải

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| fire | fired | fired |

**Lưu ý:**
- Regular — thêm -d (vì tận cùng bằng -e)
- "Fire" (informal) = "dismiss" (formal) = sa thải

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

fire + someone → sa thải ai
- The company fires employees who repeatedly break the rules.
- He was fired after it was discovered he had been falsifying reports.

fire someone for + V-ing → sa thải ai vì làm gì
- She was fired for disclosing confidential information.
- The manager was fired for consistently missing his performance targets.

get fired → bị sa thải
- If performance doesn't improve, they will fire several managers.
- He was afraid of getting fired after the project failed.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- fire an employee, fire someone on the spot, risk being fired

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| borrow | borrowed | borrowed |

**Lưu ý:**
- Regular — thêm -ed
- Phân biệt: borrow = mượn (nhận từ ai); lend = cho mượn (đưa cho ai)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

borrow + noun + from → mượn cái gì từ ai

### 10. afford *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** đủ tiền mua

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| afford | afforded | afforded |

**Lưu ý:**
- Regular — thêm -ed
- Thường dùng với "can/can't afford"

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

can afford + noun → đủ khả năng mua cái gì

- After saving for three years, she could finally afford a new car.

can't afford + to-infinitive → không đủ khả năng làm gì
- She couldn't afford the rent in the city center.
- The small company can't afford to lose any more clients.

afford to ignore → có thể bỏ qua (không nên bỏ qua)
- We can't afford to make another mistake this quarter.
- No business can afford to ignore feedback from its customers.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- afford a house, barely afford, afford the luxury

### 14. sign *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** ký

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| sign | signed | signed |

**Lưu ý:**
- Regular — thêm -ed

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

sign + noun → ký cái gì

**Phrasal Verbs:**

**Collocation phổ biến:**

### 16. reject *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** từ chối (đơn/đề nghị)

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| reject | rejected | rejected |

**Lưu ý:**
- Regular — thêm -ed
- Phân biệt với "refuse": reject dùng trong ngữ cảnh chính thức (đơn xin việc, đề nghị, kế hoạch); refuse = từ chối làm gì

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

reject + noun → từ chối cái gì
- The university rejects applicants who don't meet the minimum requirements.

be rejected by → bị từ chối bởi
- His proposal was rejected because it lacked supporting data.
- She was rejected by three universities before being accepted by her top choice.

reject an application/proposal → từ chối đơn/đề xuất
- The bank will reject the loan if your credit score is too low.
- The board rejected the proposal outright without discussion.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- reject an application, reject a proposal, reject outright
