## Từ học hôm nay

### 1. stress *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** căng thẳng; áp lực (U); nguồn căng thẳng (C); trọng âm (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a stress / stresses (C); stress (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- Ba nghĩa: (1) căng thẳng tâm lý, (2) nhấn mạnh ("lay stress on"), (3) trọng âm trong ngôn ngữ học
- "Under stress" = đang căng thẳng (phổ biến hơn "under pressure" trong bối cảnh y tế)
- "Post-traumatic stress" = căng thẳng sau sang chấn tâm lý

**Ví dụ:**
- Chronic stress can weaken the immune system and cause serious health problems.
- She does yoga every evening to reduce stress after a long day at work.
- The stresses of modern city life can take a toll on mental health.
- He placed great stress on the importance of good communication in a team.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- under stress, under pressure, work-related stress
- cause / reduce / manage stress, cope with stress
- stress levels, stress relief, post-traumatic stress

### 2. therapy *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** liệu pháp (C); trị liệu (U)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a therapy / therapies (C); therapy (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "In therapy" (không mạo từ) = đang trị liệu tâm lý: "She's been in therapy for a year."
- "Therapy" ngày càng phổ biến theo nghĩa tâm lý trị liệu: "I need therapy!" (tiếng lóng)
- Liên quan: therapist (n), therapeutic (adj)

**Ví dụ:**
- She started therapy after experiencing severe anxiety following the accident.
- Cognitive behavioral therapy has proven very effective in treating depression.
- The child made significant progress through speech therapy over six months.
- Music therapy is often used to help patients manage chronic pain.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- speech therapy, physical therapy, occupational therapy
- cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT), group therapy
- be in therapy, go to therapy, seek therapy

### 3. pill *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** viên thuốc; thuốc tránh thai (the Pill)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a pill / pills

**Lưu ý:**
- "A bitter pill to swallow" = điều khó chấp nhận nhưng cần thiết (thành ngữ)
- "The Pill" (viết hoa, không mạo từ "a") = thuốc tránh thai: "She's on the Pill."
- Phân biệt: pill (viên thuốc tròn nhỏ) vs. tablet (viên thuốc dẹt) vs. capsule (viên nang)

**Ví dụ:**
- The doctor prescribed sleeping pills to help her manage her insomnia.
- Losing the championship was a bitter pill to swallow for the whole team.
- Don't forget to take your vitamin pills with breakfast every morning.
- He found it difficult to swallow the large pill without water.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- sleeping pill, vitamin pill, birth control pill
- take a pill, swallow a pill
- a bitter pill to swallow

### 4. dose *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** liều (thuốc); lượng

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a dose / doses

**Lưu ý:**
- "A dose of reality" = cú sốc thực tế (nghĩa bóng)
- "Overdose" (n/v) = quá liều thuốc; "OD" là viết tắt thông dụng
- "In small doses" = ở lượng nhỏ: "He's fun in small doses."

**Ví dụ:**
- Take one dose of this medicine three times a day after meals.
- The trip to the refugee camp gave the students a dose of reality about poverty.
- The patient accidentally took a double dose and had to be rushed to hospital.
- Humor, in small doses, can make difficult conversations much easier.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- daily dose, recommended dose, lethal dose
- take / give / administer a dose
- overdose, dose of reality

### 5. clinic *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** phòng khám; cơ sở y tế

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a clinic / clinics

**Lưu ý:**
- Phân biệt: clinic (phòng khám nhỏ) vs. hospital (bệnh viện lớn) vs. surgery (BrE, phòng khám bác sĩ gia đình)
- "Walk-in clinic" = phòng khám không cần đặt lịch trước
- "Outpatient clinic" = phòng khám ngoại trú

**Ví dụ:**
- She made an appointment at the dental clinic for a routine check-up.
- The free health clinic provides basic medical services to those without insurance.
- There is a walk-in clinic nearby where you can see a doctor without an appointment.
- The fertility clinic helped dozens of couples start their families.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- dental clinic, health clinic, private clinic, fertility clinic
- go to a clinic, visit a clinic, run a clinic
- outpatient clinic, walk-in clinic

### 6. pharmacy *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** nhà thuốc; khoa dược

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a pharmacy / pharmacies

**Lưu ý:**

- AmE: "drugstore" = nhà thuốc (thường kiêm bán nhiều thứ hơn)
- "Pharmacist" = dược sĩ (người làm việc tại pharmacy)

**Ví dụ:**
- I need to stop by the pharmacy to pick up my prescription.
- You can buy most cold medicines over the counter at any pharmacy.
- She studied pharmacy at university and now works as a hospital pharmacist.
- The pharmacy is open 24 hours a day for emergency medication needs.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- go to the pharmacy, pick up a prescription at the pharmacy
- hospital pharmacy, community pharmacy
- pharmacy technician, pharmacy counter

### 7. muscle *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** cơ (C); sức mạnh cơ bắp (U); quyền lực (U, nghĩa bóng)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a muscle / muscles (C); muscle (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Pull a muscle" = bị kéo căng cơ (thường gặp khi tập thể thao)
- "Flex your muscles" = (1) căng cơ cho người thấy (2) thể hiện sức mạnh/quyền lực
- "Muscle memory" = trí nhớ cơ bắp (kỹ năng đã được rèn luyện tự động)

**Ví dụ:**
- He pulled a muscle in his calf while running and had to stop the race.
- Regular weight training helps build muscle and increase metabolism.
- The country flexed its military muscles by conducting large-scale naval exercises.
- After years of practice, his fingers moved with perfect muscle memory.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- build muscle, pull / strain a muscle, muscle tone
- muscle pain, muscle cramp, core muscles
- flex your muscles

### 9. stomach *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** dạ dày; bụng; sự chịu đựng (nghĩa bóng)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a stomach / stomachs

**Lưu ý:**
- "On an empty stomach" = khi bụng đói: "Don't take this medicine on an empty stomach."
- "Have a strong stomach" = chịu được cảnh ghê sợ/khó chịu
- "Stomach" (informal) = phần bụng nói chung (không chính xác về giải phẫu)

**Ví dụ:**
- He had a stomach ache all day after eating the undercooked chicken.
- Don't take aspirin on an empty stomach — it can cause irritation.
- The graphic scenes in the documentary were enough to turn anyone's stomach.
- She had butterflies in her stomach before the big presentation.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- stomach ache, upset stomach, empty stomach
- have a strong stomach
- turn sb's stomach

### 10. nerve *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** dây thần kinh (C); can đảm/liều lĩnh (U); lo lắng (nerves, plural)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a nerve / nerves (C); nerve (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Get on sb's nerves" = làm ai khó chịu, bực bội
- "Have nerves of steel" = rất bình tĩnh dưới áp lực
- "A bundle of nerves" = rất lo lắng, hồi hộp

**Ví dụ:**
- The dentist accidentally touched a nerve, causing a sharp jolt of pain.
- Before her performance, she was a bundle of nerves but calmed down once she started.
- His constant humming in the office was beginning to get on everyone's nerves.
- It took a lot of nerve to stand up and speak in front of such a large audience.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- hit/touch a nerve, get on sb's nerves
- have the nerve to do sth, bundle of nerves
- nerves of steel

### 11. nature *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** thiên nhiên (U); bản chất (U/C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** nature (U); a nature (rare, C)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Second nature" = như bản năng tự nhiên: "Driving became second nature to her."
- "Nature vs. nurture" = bản chất vs. nuôi dưỡng (tranh luận về gen và môi trường)
- "By nature" = về bản chất: "She is shy by nature."

**Ví dụ:**
- Spending time in nature can reduce stress and improve mental wellbeing.
- It is human nature to seek connection and belonging.
- After years of practice, cooking became second nature to him.
- The violent nature of the attack shocked the whole community.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- in nature, by nature, human nature, second nature
- the nature of (sth), nature and nurture
- nature reserve, nature documentary, close to nature

### 12. world *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** thế giới; lĩnh vực; cõi đời

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** the world / worlds (hiếm)

**Lưu ý:**
- Hầu hết dùng "the world" (danh từ riêng)
- "A world of difference" = khác biệt rất lớn
- "In the world" (trong thành ngữ nghi vấn): "What in the world is happening?" = Trời ơi điều gì đang xảy ra?

**Ví dụ:**

- There's a world of difference between knowing something and truly understanding it.
- He is considered one of the best chess players in the world.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- around the world, all over the world, world record
- the world of (business/art/...), worldview
- a world of difference, do sb the world of good

### 13. earth *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** Trái Đất (proper noun); đất (chất liệu, U)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** Earth / the Earth (proper); earth (U, chất liệu)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Earth" hoặc "the Earth" (viết hoa) = hành tinh; "earth" (U, chữ thường) = đất, chất đất
- "On earth" dùng để nhấn mạnh: "What on earth are you doing?"
- "Down-to-earth" (adj) = thực tế, không khoa trương

**Ví dụ:**
- The Earth orbits the Sun once every 365 days.
- What on earth were you thinking when you made that decision?
- Despite being a famous chef, she's very down-to-earth and approachable.
- The gardener turned the rich dark earth before planting the seeds.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- on earth (trên trái đất / nhấn mạnh), down to earth
- earth-shattering, earthly
- the most beautiful place on earth

### 14. sun *(noun — singular)*
**Nghĩa:** Mặt Trời; ánh nắng (U)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** the Sun / the sun (singular; "suns" chỉ dùng trong thiên văn học)

**Lưu ý:**
- "There's nothing new under the sun." = Không có gì là mới mẻ trên đời (tục ngữ)
- "Make hay while the sun shines" = tranh thủ lúc thuận lợi (tục ngữ)
- "In the sun" = dưới nắng; "sunlight" = ánh nắng mặt trời

**Ví dụ:**
- The sun rises in the east and sets in the west every day.
- She sat in the sun for too long and got a bad sunburn.
- Every invention you can think of — there's nothing new under the sun.
- Children need sunlight to help their bodies produce vitamin D.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- in the sun, under the sun, sunlight, sunrise, sunset
- catch some sun, sun protection
- there's nothing new under the sun

- full moon, new moon, half moon, moonlight
- over the moon, once in a blue moon
- ask for the moon

### 18. cloud *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** đám mây (C); đám khói/bụi (C); điện toán đám mây (U)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a cloud / clouds (C); cloud (U, in tech)

**Lưu ý:**
- "On cloud nine" = cực kỳ hạnh phúc (thành ngữ)
- "Every cloud has a silver lining." = Trong cái rủi có cái may (tục ngữ)
- "Cloud" (tech) = lưu trữ dữ liệu trực tuyến (điện toán đám mây)

**Ví dụ:**
- Dark clouds gathered on the horizon, signalling an approaching storm.
- She was on cloud nine after receiving the job offer from her dream company.
- I store all my photos on the cloud so I never lose them.
- Every cloud has a silver lining — losing that job led him to a much better one.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- dark cloud, storm cloud, on cloud nine
- cloud storage, cloud computing, every cloud has a silver lining
- a cloud of smoke/dust

### 19. rain *(noun — uncountable)*
**Nghĩa:** mưa

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** rain / (không có số nhiều; "the rains" = mùa mưa ở một số vùng nhiệt đới)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Right as rain" = hoàn toàn khỏe mạnh/tốt đẹp (thành ngữ)
- "Rain or shine" = dù mưa hay nắng, dù thế nào đi nữa
- "It never rains but it pours." = Họa vô đơn chí (tục ngữ)

**Ví dụ:**
- Heavy rain flooded the streets and caused traffic delays across the city.
- She walked home in the rain without an umbrella and got completely soaked.
- The outdoor concert will take place rain or shine.
- After a week of illness, she felt right as rain once she recovered.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- heavy rain, light rain, acid rain, rain forest
- in the rain, rain falls, be caught in the rain
- right as rain, rain or shine

### 20. wind *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** gió (U/C); hơi (trong cơ thể, U)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a wind / winds (C); wind (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Get wind of" = nghe phong thanh, biết tin đồn: "She got wind of the plan."
- "In the wind" = sắp xảy ra nhưng chưa chắc: "Changes are in the wind."
- "Second wind" = lấy lại sức, tìm được động lực mới

**Ví dụ:**
- Strong winds knocked down several trees and damaged power lines overnight.
- The company got wind of a competitor's plan and quickly changed its strategy.
- He was exhausted at the halfway mark but found his second wind and finished strong.
- The sails caught the wind and the boat glided smoothly across the bay.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- strong wind, cold wind, trade winds, wind energy
- in the wind, get wind of
- windmill, wind power, wind direction
