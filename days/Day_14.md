## Từ học hôm nay

### 1. care *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** quan tâm

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| care | cared | cared |

**Lưu ý:**
- Regular — thêm -d (vì tận cùng bằng -e)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

care about + noun → quan tâm đến điều gì
- I care about the quality of my work.
- She cares deeply about the well-being of her students.

care for + noun → chăm sóc ai/điều gì
- She cared for her sick mother all winter.
- He volunteers at the hospital to care for elderly patients.

don't care → không quan tâm
- Will you care enough to make a change?
- He doesn't care about others' opinions as long as he does his best.

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- care for: chăm sóc → *She cares for her elderly parents.*
- care about: quan tâm đến → *He doesn't care about money.*

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- care about others, care for the elderly, care deeply

### 2. mind *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** bận tâm, phiền

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| mind | minded | minded |

**Lưu ý:**
- Regular — thêm -ed
- Thường dùng trong câu hỏi lịch sự: "Do you mind...?" và câu phủ định: "I don't mind."

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

mind + V-ing → phiền khi làm gì
- Do you mind closing the window?
- Would you mind speaking a little louder?

do you mind if...? → bạn có phiền nếu...?
- He didn't mind waiting for an hour.
- Do you mind if I join your table?

never mind → thôi được rồi, không sao
- I won't mind if you come a bit late.
- Never mind the small details — focus on the bigger picture.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- mind the gap, mind your own business, never mind

### 7. argue *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** tranh luận

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| argue | argued | argued |

**Lưu ý:**
- Regular — thêm -d (vì tận cùng bằng -e)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

argue + that-clause → lập luận rằng...
- She argued that the decision was unfair.
- Critics argue that the new policy will harm small businesses.

argue with + person → tranh cãi với ai

**Phrasal Verbs:**

- argue for: lập luận ủng hộ → *He argued for a pay raise.*

**Collocation phổ biến:**

### 8. discuss *(verb — regular)*

|---|---|---|

**Lưu ý:**

discuss + wh-clause → thảo luận về việc...

- The team discussed how to handle the customer complaint effectively.

discuss with + person → thảo luận với ai
- We will need to discuss this with the entire team.
- She discussed her career goals with her mentor.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- discuss a problem, discuss options, discuss the matter

### 9. debate *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** tranh luận (có tổ chức)

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| debate | debated | debated |

**Lưu ý:**
- Regular — thêm -d (vì tận cùng bằng -e)
- Khác với "argue": debate mang tính trang trọng, có cấu trúc hơn

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

debate + noun → tranh luận về vấn đề gì

- Academics have debated this theory for decades.

debate + whether/wh-clause → tranh luận về việc liệu...
- Politicians debated the new healthcare bill for weeks.
- The committee debated whether to approve the funding request.

hotly debated → được tranh luận sôi nổi
- They will debate whether to expand the business overseas.
- The environmental impact of the project is still hotly debated.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- debate an issue, debate a topic, vigorously debate

| V1 | V2 | V3 |

| advise | advised | advised |

**Lưu ý:**

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

- The financial advisor advised against investing in volatile markets.

advise + that-clause → khuyên rằng...
- The lawyer will advise us on the best course of action.
- Experts advise that people should exercise regularly to maintain good health.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- advise caution, advise strongly, advise professionally

### 17. suspect *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** nghi ngờ

|---|---|---|
| suspect | suspected | suspected |

**Lưu ý:**
- Regular — thêm -ed

suspect + that-clause → nghi ngờ rằng...
- I suspected that something was wrong when she didn't reply.
- She suspected that her colleague had taken credit for her work.

be suspected of → bị nghi ngờ về
- They will suspect a cover-up if no explanation is given.
- The official is suspected of accepting bribes from local contractors.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- suspect foul play, suspect a crime, highly suspected
