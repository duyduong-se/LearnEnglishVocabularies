## Từ học hôm nay

### 1. satisfy *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** thỏa mãn

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| satisfy | satisfied | satisfied |

**Lưu ý:**
- Regular — y → i + ed (vì trước y là phụ âm)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

satisfy + someone → làm ai thỏa mãn
- Good food and good company satisfy me completely.
- The new menu satisfied even the most demanding customers.

be satisfied with → hài lòng với
- The results satisfied everyone on the team.
- She was satisfied with the final outcome of the project.

satisfy a requirement/condition → đáp ứng yêu cầu/điều kiện
- This solution will not satisfy the client's demands.
- Applicants must satisfy all the eligibility requirements before applying.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- satisfy customer needs, satisfy curiosity, satisfy requirements

### 2. disappoint *(verb — regular)*

**Lưu ý:**

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

disappoint + someone → làm ai thất vọng

### 5. interest *(verb — regular)*

- She became interested in robotics after attending a science fair.

it may interest you to know → có thể bạn muốn biết
- I think this opportunity will interest you.
- It may interest you to know that the company is expanding into new markets.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- interest someone in something, deeply interested, keen interest

### 8. convince *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** thuyết phục (bằng lý luận)

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| convince | convinced | convinced |

**Lưu ý:**
- Regular — thêm -d (vì tận cùng bằng -e)
- Phân biệt với "persuade": convince nhấn mạnh vào lý luận/bằng chứng

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

convince + someone + that-clause → thuyết phục ai rằng...
- I can't convince him that his plan won't work.
- The data convinced the board that a change in strategy was necessary.

convince + someone + to-infinitive → thuyết phục ai làm gì
- The evidence convinced the jury of his guilt.
- She convinced her manager to approve the budget for the new project.

be convinced of → bị thuyết phục về
- A good presentation will convince investors to fund the project.
- He was convinced of the need for more staff after reviewing the workload.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- convince someone of the truth, fully convinced, hard to convince

### 9. persuade *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** thuyết phục (bằng cảm xúc/lý lẽ)

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|

**Lưu ý:**

- Phân biệt với "convince": persuade nhấn mạnh vào hành động thuyết phục và kết quả là hành động

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

persuade + someone + to-infinitive → thuyết phục ai làm gì

persuade + someone + into + V-ing → thuyết phục ai làm gì
- He persuaded his father to buy him a new laptop.
- She persuaded her colleague into attending the training session.

be persuaded by → bị thuyết phục bởi
- With enough patience, you can persuade anyone to change their mind.
- He was persuaded by the compelling evidence presented at the meeting.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- persuade someone to act, easily persuaded, persuade gently

### 11. permit *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** cho phép (formal)

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| permit | permitted | permitted |

**Lưu ý:**
- Regular — nhân đôi phụ âm cuối t trước khi thêm -ted (vì âm tiết cuối được nhấn: per-MIT)
- Formal hơn "allow" và "let"

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

permit + someone + to-infinitive → cho phép ai làm gì
- The manager permitted him to leave early.
- The visa permits the holder to stay for up to 90 days.

permit + V-ing/noun → cho phép điều gì
- Smoking is not permitted inside the building.
- Photography is not permitted in the museum's exhibition halls.

if time permits → nếu thời gian cho phép
- We will explore more topics if time permits.
- If time permits, we will also cover some advanced grammar points.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- permit access, permit entry, weather permitting
