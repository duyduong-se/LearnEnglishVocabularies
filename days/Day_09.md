### 135. improve *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** cải thiện, cải tiến

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| improve | improved | improved |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -d (regular)
- improve + [noun] (cải thiện cái gì)
- improve + on (làm tốt hơn)
- constantly improving

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

improve + [noun] → Cải thiện cái gì
- She practices speaking English daily to improve her fluency and confidence.
- The new management improved employee satisfaction by 25% in six months.

improve + on → Làm tốt hơn điều gì đó
- The updated software will improve the system's stability and response time.
- This version improves on the previous one in terms of speed and accuracy.

improve + over time → Cải thiện theo thời gian
- His writing skills improved significantly over the course of the year.
- The relationship between the two teams improved over time with better communication.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- improve performance, improve skills, improve significantly, room for improvement, constantly improving

### 136. record *(verb — regular)*

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -ed (regular)
- /rɪˈkɔːd/ (verb) vs /ˈrekɔːd/ (noun)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

record + [noun] → Ghi lại/thu âm cái gì
- The teacher records all sessions so students can review them later.

- Staff are required to record all client interactions in the system.

be on record → Được ghi chép chính thức
- The device will record all measurements automatically every hour.
- This is the worst drought on record in the past fifty years.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- record data, record a conversation, set a record, off the record, break a record

### 137. connect *(verb — regular)*

**Lưu ý:**

- be connected = có mối liên hệ
- well-connected = có nhiều mối quan hệ

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**
