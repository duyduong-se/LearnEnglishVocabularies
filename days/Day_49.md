## Từ học hôm nay

### 1. disease *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** bệnh (thường là bệnh cụ thể, có tên gọi)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a disease / diseases (C); disease (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- Phân biệt: disease (bệnh có tên/nguyên nhân cụ thể) vs. illness (trạng thái ốm yếu, bao gồm cả tinh thần) vs. sickness (cảm giác buồn nôn, không khỏe)
- "Infectious disease" (lây qua tiếp xúc) vs. "contagious disease" (lây qua không khí) — thường dùng thay nhau
- "Disease" (U): "Poor sanitation is the main cause of disease in developing countries."

**Ví dụ:**
- Heart disease is one of the leading causes of death worldwide.
- Scientists are working to develop a vaccine for the new infectious disease.
- Poor sanitation is the main cause of disease in many developing countries.
- Early detection is key to successfully treating most serious diseases.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- infectious disease, heart disease, rare disease
- spread / prevent / treat a disease, disease outbreak
- cause of disease, symptoms of a disease

### 2. pain *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** cơn đau (C/U); sự đau khổ (U)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a pain / pains (C); pain (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "A pain in the neck" (hoặc "a pain in the backside") = người/thứ gây phiền toái
- "Take pains to do sth" = cẩn thận kỹ lưỡng trong việc gì
- "Growing pains" = đau khi lớn (cả nghĩa đen và bóng)

**Ví dụ:**
- She felt a sharp pain in her lower back after lifting the heavy box.
- He was in tremendous pain after the operation but refused strong medication.
- My little brother can be a real pain in the neck sometimes.
- She took great pains to make sure every detail of the event was perfect.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- sharp pain, chronic pain, back pain, chest pain
- feel / suffer / cause pain, be in pain
- pain relief, painkiller, pain threshold

### 3. injury *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** thương tích; chấn thương

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** an injury / injuries (C); injury (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Add insult to injury" = thêm sỉ nhục vào đau đớn, làm tình trạng tệ hơn
- Phân biệt: injury (thương tích vật lý) vs. wound (vết thương hở, thường do vũ khí) vs. damage (thiệt hại cho vật)
- "Sustain an injury" = bị thương (formal)

**Ví dụ:**
- The player suffered a knee injury and will miss the rest of the season.
- She recovered fully from her injuries after three months of physical therapy.
- To add insult to injury, he lost his job the same week his car broke down.
- Wearing the right protective gear can help prevent serious injuries.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- serious / minor / fatal injury, sports injury
- suffer / sustain an injury, recover from an injury
- add insult to injury

### 4. treatment *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** điều trị (C/U); sự đối xử (U)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a treatment / treatments (C); treatment (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- Hai nghĩa quan trọng: (1) điều trị y tế: "cancer treatment" / (2) cách đối xử: "equal treatment"
- "Give sb the silent treatment" = cố tình im lặng, không nói chuyện với ai đó
- "Undergo treatment" = trải qua quá trình điều trị (formal)

**Ví dụ:**
- She underwent six months of cancer treatment and is now in remission.
- All employees deserve fair treatment regardless of their background.
- A new treatment for Alzheimer's disease is currently being tested in clinical trials.
- He gave her the silent treatment for days after their argument.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- medical treatment, cancer treatment, drug treatment
- receive / undergo / provide treatment
- equal treatment, unfair treatment, VIP treatment

### 5. surgery *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** phẫu thuật (U); ca mổ (C); phòng khám (BrE, C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a surgery / surgeries (C); surgery (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Have surgery" (không mạo từ) = làm phẫu thuật: "She had surgery on her knee."
- "Rocket surgery" KHÔNG tồn tại — lỗi phổ biến (nhầm giữa "rocket science" và "brain surgery")
- BrE: "the doctor's surgery" = phòng khám bác sĩ

**Ví dụ:**
- He had surgery to remove a tumour from his spine last month.
- The surgery lasted eight hours and the patient recovered well.
- Cosmetic surgery has become increasingly popular in recent decades.
- The surgeon performed three surgeries in a single day.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- have surgery, undergo surgery, perform surgery
- open-heart surgery, brain surgery, cosmetic surgery
- surgery was successful, recovery from surgery

### 6. vaccine *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** vaccine; vắc-xin

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a vaccine / vaccines

**Lưu ý:**
- "Vaccination" (n) = hành động tiêm phòng; "vaccinate" (v) = tiêm phòng
- "Vaccine hesitancy" = sự do dự không muốn tiêm vaccine (thuật ngữ y tế công cộng)
- "Vaccine rollout" = triển khai tiêm chủng đại trà

**Ví dụ:**
- Scientists developed a COVID-19 vaccine in record time during the pandemic.
- The government launched a nationwide campaign to encourage people to get the vaccine.
- The flu vaccine needs to be updated every year because the virus mutates.
- The successful vaccine rollout helped bring the pandemic under control.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- develop / produce / approve a vaccine
- get / receive a vaccine, vaccine rollout
- COVID-19 vaccine, flu vaccine, vaccine hesitancy

### 7. symptom *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** triệu chứng (của bệnh); dấu hiệu (của vấn đề lớn hơn)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a symptom / symptoms

**Lưu ý:**
- Dùng cả nghĩa đen và bóng: "Corruption is a symptom of a deeper problem."
- "Asymptomatic" = không có triệu chứng (thuật ngữ y tế)
- Thường dùng ở số nhiều: "symptoms of flu/depression"

**Ví dụ:**
- The most common symptoms of the flu are fever, cough, and body aches.
- If you experience any symptoms of a heart attack, call emergency services immediately.
- High unemployment is often a symptom of deeper economic problems.
- Some people carry the virus without showing any symptoms at all.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- common symptom, early symptom, warning symptom
- show / display / develop symptoms
- symptoms of flu/depression/a problem

### 8. fever *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** sốt; cơn sốt (cả nghĩa đen và bóng)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a fever / fevers (C); fever (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Run a fever" = bị sốt (thường dùng hơn "have a fever" trong văn nói)
- "Hay fever" = bệnh sốt cỏ khô (dị ứng phấn hoa)
- "Fever pitch" = trạng thái hưng phấn cao độ: "excitement reached fever pitch"

**Ví dụ:**
- The child had a high fever all night, so her parents took her to the hospital.
- The fever finally broke in the morning and he started feeling better.
- The whole city was gripped by World Cup fever during the tournament.
- She was running a fever so she stayed home from work.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- high fever, mild fever, run a fever
- have a fever, develop a fever, fever breaks
- gold fever, hay fever, spring fever

### 9. cold *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** cảm lạnh; bệnh cảm

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a cold / colds

**Lưu ý:**
- Luôn dùng "a cold": "have a cold", "catch a cold" — KHÔNG nói "have cold" (thiếu mạo từ là sai)
- Phân biệt: a cold (bệnh cảm — danh từ C) vs. cold (lạnh — tính từ/danh từ U)
- "The common cold" = cảm lạnh thông thường (không có thuốc chữa khỏi hoàn toàn)

**Ví dụ:**
- She caught a cold after walking in the rain without an umbrella.
- I've had this terrible cold for a week and I can't seem to shake it.
- Children often get several colds a year because their immune systems are still developing.
- The best remedy for a cold is rest, fluids, and time.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- catch a cold, have a cold, get over a cold
- common cold, bad cold, chest cold
- cold and flu season

### 10. virus *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** vi-rút; virus máy tính

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a virus / viruses

**Lưu ý:**
- Hai nghĩa: (1) vi-rút sinh học: "a flu virus" / (2) virus máy tính: "a computer virus"
- "Go viral" (adj) = lan truyền nhanh trên mạng xã hội — từ "viral" từ "virus"
- "Antivirus software" = phần mềm diệt virus

**Ví dụ:**
- The virus spread rapidly across the country before any measures were taken.
- His computer was infected with a virus that stole his personal information.
- The video went viral overnight, reaching millions of viewers around the world.
- Scientists are still working to find an effective treatment for the virus.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- computer virus, flu virus, spread a virus
- catch / contract / carry a virus
- virus spreads, virus mutates, antivirus software

### 11. bacteria *(noun — plural)*
**Nghĩa:** vi khuẩn

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** bacterium (hiếm dùng) / bacteria (đây là dạng số nhiều của "bacterium")

**Lưu ý:**
- Lỗi phổ biến: "bacteria is" là SAI — phải dùng "bacteria are" (vì bacteria là số nhiều)
- Số ít đúng là "bacterium" nhưng ít dùng trong văn thường
- Không phải tất cả bacteria đều có hại — nhiều loại có lợi (e.g., gut bacteria)

**Ví dụ:**
- Some bacteria are harmful to humans, while others are essential for digestion.
- The bacteria multiply quickly in warm and moist conditions.
- Antibiotic-resistant bacteria pose a serious threat to global public health.
- Washing your hands regularly helps prevent the spread of harmful bacteria.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- harmful / beneficial bacteria, bacteria grow / multiply
- kill / destroy bacteria, bacteria cause infection
- gut bacteria, soil bacteria, antibiotic-resistant bacteria

### 12. cancer *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** ung thư (U); loại ung thư cụ thể (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a cancer / cancers (C); cancer (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- Uncountable (bệnh chung): "Cancer is a disease that affects millions worldwide."
- Countable (một loại/nghĩa bóng): "Corruption is a cancer eating away at society."
- "Cancer" (viết hoa) = cung Cự Giải trong chiêm tinh học

**Ví dụ:**
- Breast cancer is one of the most common cancers affecting women globally.
- Thanks to early detection, she was able to beat cancer and make a full recovery.
- Corruption is a cancer that destroys the foundations of a fair society.
- Smoking significantly increases the risk of developing lung cancer.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- breast cancer, lung cancer, skin cancer, colon cancer
- develop / have / survive cancer, fight cancer
- cancer treatment, cancer research, cancer cell

### 13. diet *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** chế độ ăn uống (U); chế độ ăn kiêng cụ thể (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a diet / diets (C); diet (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Be on a diet" = đang ăn kiêng (để giảm cân)
- "Diet" cũng là tính từ: "diet Coke" (ít đường); "diet food"
- Phân biệt: diet (chế độ ăn) vs. meal (bữa ăn) vs. food (thức ăn)

**Ví dụ:**
- A balanced diet rich in vegetables and fruits helps prevent many diseases.
- She's been on a strict diet for three months and has lost ten kilograms.
- A poor diet combined with a sedentary lifestyle increases the risk of heart disease.
- The doctor advised him to change his diet and exercise more regularly.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- balanced diet, healthy diet, low-fat diet, Mediterranean diet
- be on a diet, go on a diet, follow a diet
- diet and exercise, dietary needs, diet plan
