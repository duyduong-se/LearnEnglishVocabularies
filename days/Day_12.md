### 185. predict *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** dự đoán

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| predict | predicted | predicted |

**Lưu ý:**
- Regular — thêm -ed
- predict + [noun/that clause]
- prediction (danh từ); predictable (adj)
- hard to predict

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

predict + [noun] → Dự đoán cái gì
- Meteorologists can now predict weather patterns with greater accuracy.
- Scientists use historical data to predict future climate trends.

predict + (that) + clause → Dự đoán rằng...
- Analysts predicted that the market would recover within six months — and it did.
- Experts predict that electric vehicles will account for 50% of all sales by 2035.

be predicted + to → Được dự đoán sẽ
- The population is predicted to grow significantly over the next decade.
- The new policy is predicted to reduce unemployment rates by next year.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- predict accurately, predict outcomes, predict trends, hard to predict, widely predicted

### 186. recommend *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** đề xuất, khuyên

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| recommend | recommended | recommended |

**Lưu ý:**
- Regular — thêm -ed
- recommend + V-ing (không dùng to V)
- recommend + (that) + S + (should) + V
- recommendation (danh từ)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

recommend + V-ing → Khuyên nên làm gì
- The doctor recommends exercising at least 30 minutes every day.
- Nutritionists recommend eating more vegetables and reducing sugar intake.

recommend + (that) + clause → Đề xuất rằng...
- She recommended that the team take a different approach to the problem.
- The consultant recommended that the company restructure its operations.

recommend + [noun] → Đề xuất/giới thiệu cái gì
- I would highly recommend this course to anyone looking to improve their English.
- He recommended the new restaurant to all his colleagues.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- strongly recommend, highly recommended, recommend to others, recommend a solution, personal recommendation

### 190. plan *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** lên kế hoạch

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| plan | planned | planned |

**Lưu ý:**
- Regular — gấp đôi n trước -ed
- plan + to V (có kế hoạch làm gì)
- plan + [noun] (lên kế hoạch cho cái gì)
- planner (danh từ)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

plan + to + V → Có kế hoạch làm gì
- We are planning to open two new branches in the northern region next year.
- She plans to pursue a master's degree after graduating from university.

plan + [noun] → Lên kế hoạch cho cái gì
- The team planned the product launch strategy three months in advance.
- They planned the entire event without any outside assistance.

plan + for → Lên kế hoạch cho
- She plans her weekly meals on Sundays to eat healthily and save money.
- Smart investors plan for unexpected expenses and market downturns.

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- plan out: lên kế hoạch chi tiết → *She planned out every step of the project.*

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- plan carefully, plan ahead, plan for the future, as planned, plan a trip

### 196. adapt *(verb — regular)*

| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|

**Lưu ý:**
- Regular — thêm -ed
- adapt + to (thích nghi với)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

### 198. balance *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** cân bằng

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| balance | balanced | balanced |

**Lưu ý:**
- Regular — thêm -d
- balance + A + with/against + B
- balance (danh từ)
- strike a balance = tìm sự cân bằng

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

balance + A + with + B → Cân bằng A với B
- Successful leaders balance the needs of the business with the welfare of their team.
- He balances his passion for art with a practical career in design.

balance + [budget/time] → Cân đối ngân sách/thời gian
- She balanced her full-time job with part-time studies by managing her time wisely.
- The finance department balanced the budget carefully before the new fiscal year.

strike a balance → Tìm/đạt được sự cân bằng
- The new policy will help employees better balance professional and personal responsibilities.
- Good managers strike a balance between flexibility and accountability.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- balance work and life, balance the budget, strike a balance, maintain balance, well-balanced
