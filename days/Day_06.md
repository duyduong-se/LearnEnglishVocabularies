### 85. agree *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** đồng ý

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| agree | agreed | agreed |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -d (regular)
- agree + to V (đồng ý làm gì)
- agree + with sb (đồng ý với ai)
- agree + on sth (đồng thuận về điều gì)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

agree + with + sb → Đồng ý với ai
- Most participants in the survey agree that work-life balance is essential.
- I completely agree with your analysis of the situation.

agree + to + V → Đồng ý làm gì
- I believe all stakeholders will agree to the revised terms of the contract.
- She agreed to take on the additional responsibilities.

agree + on + [noun] → Đồng thuận về vấn đề gì
- Both teams agreed on the timeline after a productive discussion.
- The two companies finally agreed on a fair price.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- agree to disagree, strongly agree, agree in principle, agree unanimously, agree wholeheartedly

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- agree with: phù hợp, đồng ý → *Spicy food doesn't agree with me.*

### 86. support *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** hỗ trợ, ủng hộ

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| support | supported | supported |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -ed (regular)
- support + [noun/sb]
- support cũng là danh từ (financial support)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

support + sb/sth → Hỗ trợ/ủng hộ ai/cái gì
- Thousands of fans support the local football club every weekend.
- She always supports her colleagues when they face challenges.

support + a cause → Ủng hộ một mục đích
- The organization supported the refugees with food, shelter, and legal aid.
- He has long supported environmental causes and clean energy initiatives.

support + V-ing → Ủng hộ việc làm gì
- The new government policy will support small businesses through tax incentives.
- The management team fully supports investing in employee development.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- support strongly, support financially, support a team, support the community, technical support

### 87. hit *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** đánh, đập, đạt đến

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| hit | hit | hit |

**Lưu ý:**
- V1/V2/V3 đều là "hit"
- hit có nghĩa rộng: đạt mục tiêu (hit a target), ảnh hưởng (the storm hit)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

hit + [noun] → Đánh/đập cái gì
- The typhoon hit the coastal region with winds of 150 km/h.
- The ball hit the post and bounced back into play.

hit + [target/milestone] → Đạt mốc/mục tiêu
- Our sales team will hit the one million dollar target by the end of the quarter.
- The app hit one million downloads in its first week.

hit + [place] → Tấn công, ảnh hưởng đến
- The new product hit the market and became an instant bestseller.
- The economic downturn hit the manufacturing sector especially hard.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- hit a target, hit a record, hit the road, hit the market, hit rock bottom

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- hit on/upon: tình cờ nghĩ ra → *She hit upon a brilliant solution.*
- hit back: phản công → *The company hit back against the criticism.*

### 88. produce *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** sản xuất, tạo ra

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| produce | produced | produced |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -d (regular)
- produce cũng là danh từ /ˈprɒdjuːs/ (nông sản)
- produce + results/effects

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

produce + [noun] → Sản xuất/tạo ra cái gì
- This factory produces over 10,000 units of electronics per day.
- The farm produces organic vegetables sold at local markets.

produce + results/evidence → Đưa ra kết quả/bằng chứng
- The research team produced groundbreaking results on cancer treatment.
- He was asked to produce evidence to support his claims.

be produced + by/in → Được sản xuất bởi/tại
- The new solar panels will produce enough electricity for the entire building.
- This documentary was produced by an award-winning filmmaker.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- produce results, produce goods, mass-produce, locally produced, produce evidence

### 89. eat *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** ăn

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| eat | ate | eaten |

**Lưu ý:**
- cả ba dạng đều khác nhau
- eat out = ăn ngoài
- eat up = ăn hết

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

eat + [noun] → Ăn cái gì
- She eats a balanced diet rich in vegetables, protein, and whole grains.
- I will eat something light before the interview to stay focused and energized.

eat + at/in → Ăn ở đâu
- The whole family ate together at the restaurant to celebrate her promotion.
- He prefers to eat at home rather than going out on weekdays.

eat + [adverb] → Ăn như thế nào (eat healthily)
- She has been trying to eat more healthily since joining the wellness program.
- They eat together as a family every Sunday evening.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- eat breakfast/lunch/dinner, eat healthily, eat out, eat together, eat in moderation

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- eat out: ăn ở nhà hàng → *Let's eat out tonight instead of cooking.*
- eat up: ăn hết → *Eat up your vegetables!*

### 90. cover *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** bao phủ, đề cập, tường thuật

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| cover | covered | covered |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -ed (regular)
- cover có nhiều nghĩa: che phủ, đề cập chủ đề, bảo hiểm chi trả, tường thuật

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

cover + [noun] → Phủ/che cái gì
- The health insurance plan will cover all medical expenses including hospitalization.
- Snow covered the entire city overnight.

cover + a topic → Đề cập đến chủ đề
- The course covers grammar, vocabulary, and professional writing skills.
- The training session will cover the basics of project management.

cover + [distance] → Đi quãng đường
- She covered the award ceremony for the national newspaper.
- He covered 20 kilometers on his bicycle before noon.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- cover a topic, cover ground, cover expenses, cover a story, cover all bases

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- cover up: che giấu → *He tried to cover up his mistake.*
- cover for: làm thay, bao che → *Can you cover for me on Friday?*

### 91. catch *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** bắt, nắm được, kịp

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| catch | caught | caught |

**Lưu ý:**
- catch có nhiều nghĩa: bắt được, kịp (catch the bus), bị (catch a cold), hiểu (catch the meaning)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

catch + [noun] → Bắt/nắm cái gì
- He caught a cold after walking in the rain without an umbrella.
- The goalkeeper caught the ball just before it crossed the line.

catch + [transport] → Kịp tàu/xe
- She managed to catch the last train home just before midnight.
- I need to leave now to catch the 8 o'clock bus.

catch + [illness] → Bị mắc bệnh
- I will catch you up on everything that happened at the conference.
- Many students catch flu during the cold season.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- catch a bus, catch a cold, catch someone's eye, catch fire, catch up

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- catch up: theo kịp → *I need to catch up on my reading.*
- catch on: nắm bắt được, trở nên phổ biến → *The new trend caught on quickly.*

### 92. draw *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** vẽ, kéo, thu hút

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| draw | drew | drawn |

**Lưu ý:**
- draw có nghĩa rộng: vẽ tranh, kéo ra (draw a sword), thu hút (draw attention), kết luận (draw conclusions)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

draw + [noun] → Vẽ cái gì
- The artist draws detailed portraits of local people from memory.
- She drew a simple map to help visitors find the venue.

draw + [conclusion/comparison] → Rút ra kết luận/so sánh
- The unique architecture drew thousands of tourists to the city.
- We should draw lessons from what happened last quarter.

draw + [attention/crowd] → Thu hút sự chú ý/đám đông
- We will draw up a comprehensive action plan before the next board meeting.
- The colorful display drew a large crowd at the trade fair.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- draw attention, draw conclusions, draw inspiration, draw a line, draw near

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- draw up: soạn thảo → *The lawyers drew up the contract.*
- draw on: dựa vào, sử dụng → *She drew on her experience.*

### 93. choose *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** chọn, lựa chọn

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| choose | chose | chosen |

**Lưu ý:**
- ba dạng đều khác nhau
- choose + to V (chọn làm gì) vs. choose + [noun] (chọn cái gì)
- choose between

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

choose + [noun] → Chọn cái gì
- She chooses her words carefully in every professional communication.
- He chose the simplest solution to avoid unnecessary complications.

choose + to + V → Chọn làm gì
- He chose to study abroad despite the challenges it presented.
- She chose to speak up rather than stay silent.

choose + between/from → Chọn giữa/từ
- We will choose the best supplier based on quality, price, and reliability.
- It was difficult to choose between the two equally strong candidates.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- choose carefully, choose freely, choose between options, handpicked/carefully chosen

### 94. cause *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** gây ra

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| cause | caused | caused |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -d (regular)
- cause + [noun] (gây ra cái gì)
- cause + sb + to V (khiến ai làm gì)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

cause + [noun] → Gây ra cái gì
- Poor communication often causes misunderstandings in the workplace.
- The heavy rainfall caused significant flooding in the low-lying areas.

cause + sb + to + V → Khiến ai làm/trở nên gì
- The factory's emissions will cause serious air pollution if not regulated.
- The sudden noise caused everyone in the room to jump.

be caused + by → Được gây ra bởi
- The delay was caused by unexpected technical issues.
- Most accidents are caused by human error rather than equipment failure.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- cause damage, cause problems, cause confusion, cause concern, cause harm

### 95. point *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** chỉ, trỏ, nêu ra

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| point | pointed | pointed |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -ed (regular)
- point + at/to (chỉ vào)
- point out (nêu ra)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

point + at/to + [noun] → Chỉ vào cái gì
- The tour guide pointed to the historical landmark and explained its significance.
- She pointed at the chart to highlight the key trends.

point + out → Nêu ra, chỉ ra
- He pointed out that the budget proposal had several calculation errors.
- She pointed out a significant flaw in the original plan.

point + in a direction → Chỉ hướng
- I will point you to the resources you need to complete this task.
- The sign pointed in the direction of the nearest exit.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- point out a mistake, point in the right direction, point the way, make a point

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- point out: chỉ ra, nêu ra → *She pointed out several errors in the report.*
- point to: ám chỉ, cho thấy → *The evidence points to a clear pattern.*

### 96. listen *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** lắng nghe

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| listen | listened | listened |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -ed (regular)
- listen (chủ ý lắng nghe) khác "hear" (nghe thấy không chủ ý)
- listen + to

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

listen + to + [noun/sb] → Lắng nghe cái gì/ai
- Good leaders always listen to their team's concerns and suggestions.
- She listened to the entire podcast during her morning run.

listen + carefully/attentively → Lắng nghe chú ý
- Please listen carefully to the instructions before starting the assessment.
- The students listened attentively as the professor explained the concept.

listen + for → Nghe để tìm/phát hiện
- She listened for any sign that he was not telling the truth.
- We listened for the sound of footsteps in the hallway.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- listen carefully, listen to music, listen attentively, listen to advice, listen actively

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- listen in on: nghe lén → *Someone was listening in on our conversation.*

### 97. explain *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** giải thích

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| explain | explained | explained |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -ed (regular)
- explain + [noun] + to + sb
- không dùng "explain sb" mà phải dùng "explain to sb"

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

explain + [noun] + to + sb → Giải thích cái gì cho ai
- The teacher explains complex concepts with simple, relatable examples.
- Can you explain the new process to the team during the briefing?

explain + why/how/what + clause → Giải thích tại sao/như thế nào/cái gì
- He explained why the project had been delayed by two weeks.
- She explained how the software works in simple terms.

explain + (that) + clause → Giải thích rằng...
- I will explain the entire process to the new team members during orientation.
- He explained that he had misunderstood the original instructions.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- explain clearly, explain in detail, explain the reason, explain briefly, explain step by step

### 98. control *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** kiểm soát

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| control | controlled | controlled |

**Lưu ý:**
- gấp đôi l trước -ed (regular)
- control + [noun]
- be in control
- out of control

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

control + [noun] → Kiểm soát cái gì
- She controls her emotions very well even in stressful situations.
- The manager carefully controls the project budget to avoid overruns.

be in control + of → Kiểm soát, nắm quyền
- The government introduced new measures to control rising inflation.
- She felt fully in control of the situation despite the pressure.

be out of control → Mất kiểm soát
- The automated system will control the temperature and humidity in the facility.
- The situation quickly got out of control during the negotiation.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- control costs, control quality, control emotions, remain in control, remote control

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- control over: có quyền kiểm soát → *She has full control over the budget.*

### 100. carry *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** mang, vận chuyển

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| carry | carried | carried |

**Lưu ý:**
- y → ied (regular)
- carry + [noun] (mang vật)
- carry on (tiếp tục)
- carry out (thực hiện)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

carry + [noun] → Mang/vận chuyển cái gì
- She always carries a notebook to write down her ideas on the go.
- He carries his lunch to work every day to save money.

carry + out → Thực hiện (thường dùng với nghiên cứu, kế hoạch)
- The research team carried out extensive tests before launching the drug.
- The inspection was carried out by an independent authority.

carry + on → Tiếp tục
- The logistics company will carry the goods safely to all destinations.
- Despite the difficulties, she carried on with her studies.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- carry out a plan, carry weight, carry on with, carry responsibility, carry a risk

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- carry out: thực hiện → *The team carried out a thorough investigation.*
- carry on: tiếp tục → *Carry on with your work.*
- carry away: cuốn trôi, làm mất lý trí → *Don't get carried away with emotions.*
