## Từ học hôm nay

### 1. heal *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** chữa lành, lành lại

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| heal | healed | healed |

**Lưu ý:**
- Regular — thêm -ed

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

heal + noun (wound/injury/pain) → chữa lành một vết thương/chấn thương/nỗi đau
- Time heals all wounds.
- The medicine healed her skin condition within a few weeks.

heal (intransitive) → tự lành (không cần tân ngữ)
- The doctor said the broken bone healed faster than expected.
- The cut was minor and healed on its own overnight.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- heal a wound, heal the body, heal naturally

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- heal over: (vết thương) lành lại, đóng vảy → *The cut healed over in a week.*

### 2. cure *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** chữa khỏi (bệnh)

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| cure | cured | cured |

**Lưu ý:**
- Regular — thêm -d (đã có -e)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

cure + person + of + disease → chữa khỏi bệnh cho ai
- The new medication cured him of the infection within days.
- Doctors hope the new therapy will cure patients of the disease.

cure + disease → chữa khỏi một bệnh
- Scientists are still trying to cure cancer.
- No drug has been found to cure this rare condition yet.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- cure a disease, cure cancer, cure an infection

be diagnosed with + disease → được/bị chẩn đoán mắc bệnh

**Collocation phổ biến:**

### 11. sleep *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** ngủ

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| sleep | slept | slept |

**Lưu ý:**
- Irregular — V2 và V3 là slept

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

sleep + time expression → ngủ bao lâu
- Adults should sleep at least seven to eight hours per night.
- She slept for twelve hours after the long flight.

sleep well/poorly → ngủ ngon/không ngon
- If you don't sleep enough, your immune system will weaken.
- He slept poorly the night before the surgery due to anxiety.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- sleep well, sleep deeply, sleep eight hours

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- sleep in: ngủ muộn → *I slept in until 10 a.m. on Sunday.*
- sleep off: ngủ cho hết (say, mệt) → *He slept off his headache.*

**Phrasal Verbs:**

## Ôn tập nhanh (Quick Review)
Dịch sang tiếng Anh:
1. Vết thương của anh ấy đã lành sau hai tuần.

5. Hàng triệu người chịu đựng chứng trầm cảm mỗi năm.

**Đáp án:**
1. His wound healed after two weeks.
2. The doctor diagnosed her with diabetes.
3. You should sleep at least seven hours a night.
4. She hurt her back after lifting a heavy box.
5. Millions of people suffer from depression every year.
