## Từ học hôm nay

### 1. government *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** chính phủ

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a government / governments (collective noun)

**Lưu ý:**
- "The government" có thể đi với động từ số ít (Mỹ) hoặc số nhiều (Anh): "The government is/are..."
- "Government" ≠ "state": "state" (nhà nước) bao hàm toàn bộ thể chế; "government" là nội các cụ thể
- "Govern" (động từ) = cai quản; "governance" (uncountable) = quản trị

**Ví dụ:**
- The government has announced a new stimulus package to support small businesses.
- Democratic governments are accountable to the people through free elections.
- Local government is responsible for waste collection, roads, and planning permissions.
- The new government introduced several reforms in its first year in office.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- the government announced/introduced...
- form a government
- government policy, government spending
- local/central/federal government

---

### 2. law *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** luật, pháp luật (U); một đạo luật/quy tắc (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** law (U) / a law, laws (C)

**Lưu ý:**
- Uncountable (pháp luật nói chung): "She studies law." / "The law applies to everyone."
- Countable (một đạo luật): "They passed a new law banning plastic bags."
- "Lay down the law" (thành ngữ) = ra lệnh, áp đặt quy tắc

**Ví dụ:**
- Everyone is equal before the law, regardless of their social status or wealth.
- The parliament passed a new law requiring all cyclists to wear helmets.
- By law, employers must provide a safe working environment for all employees.
- She decided to study law because she wanted to fight for justice.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- break the law, obey/follow the law
- pass/enforce a law
- by law
- law and order, rule of law

---

### 3. right *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** quyền (pháp lý/đạo đức); phía bên phải (hướng)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a right / rights

**Lưu ý:**
- "Human rights" (số nhiều, không mạo từ) — cụm rất quan trọng
- "In your own right" = tự thân bạn, không cần nhờ ai: "She is famous in her own right."
- "Right" cũng là tính từ (đúng), trạng từ (bên phải), và thán từ (okay!)

**Ví dụ:**
- Every person has the right to freedom of speech and expression.
- The organization works to protect human rights around the world.
- She is a successful artist in her own right, not just because of her famous parents.
- Children have the right to a safe and quality education.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- human rights, the right to
- civil/equal rights
- have the right to, rights and responsibilities
- in your own right

---

### 4. rule *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** quy tắc, quy định; sự cai trị

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a rule / rules

**Lưu ý:**
- "As a rule" = generally, usually = thường thì
- "Rule of thumb" = nguyên tắc thực tế chung (không hoàn toàn chính xác)
- "Rule out" = loại trừ khả năng: "We cannot rule out any options."

**Ví dụ:**
- As a rule, the library is closed on public holidays.
- A good rule of thumb is to save at least ten percent of your monthly income.
- The police cannot rule out the possibility that the fire was started deliberately.
- Students are expected to follow the rules of the school at all times.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- follow/break the rules
- as a rule, rule of thumb
- ground rules, rule out
- majority rule

---

### 5. policy *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** chính sách; hợp đồng bảo hiểm

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a policy / policies

**Lưu ý:**
- "Policy" (chính sách) khác "politics" (chính trị) và "politician" (chính trị gia)
- "An insurance policy" = hợp đồng bảo hiểm (nghĩa khác)
- "Zero-tolerance policy" = chính sách không khoan nhượng

**Ví dụ:**
- The new government policy aims to reduce unemployment among young people.
- It is our company policy that all employees work from home at least two days a week.
- She took out an insurance policy to cover her car against theft and damage.
- The school has a strict zero-tolerance policy on bullying.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- government/foreign/economic policy
- implement/introduce a policy
- policy maker/policymaking, an insurance policy
- company policy

### 6. program *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** chương trình (kế hoạch/TV/máy tính)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a program / programs (American); programme / programmes (British)

**Lưu ý:**
- American: "program"; British: "programme" (ngoại trừ "computer program" — cả hai dùng "program")
- "Program" cũng là động từ: "to program a computer" = lập trình
- "Program" = kế hoạch có cấu trúc; "project" = dự án cụ thể

**Ví dụ:**
- She enrolled in a training program to improve her project management skills.
- The exchange program allows students to study abroad for one semester.
- What is your favorite TV program? Mine is a nature documentary series.
- The government launched a new program to support unemployed workers.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- training/exchange program
- TV program/programme
- computer program, run a program
- government program

### 7. plan *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** kế hoạch; bản vẽ thiết kế

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a plan / plans

**Lưu ý:**
- "Go according to plan" = diễn ra đúng như kế hoạch
- "Plan B" = kế hoạch dự phòng khi kế hoạch A không thành
- "Best-laid plans" (thành ngữ): "The best-laid plans of mice and men often go awry."

**Ví dụ:**
- We made a detailed plan for the camping trip to make sure everything went smoothly.
- Everything went according to plan, and the event was a great success.
- We always need a backup plan in case the original idea does not work out.
- The architect showed us the floor plan of the new building.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- make/draw up a plan, carry out/implement a plan
- a backup plan / plan B
- go according to plan
- floor plan

### 8. action *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** hành động (U); vụ kiện (C); cảnh hành động (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** action (U) / an action, actions (C)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Take action" (không mạo từ) = hành động, làm điều gì đó
- "Out of action" = không hoạt động được (do bị thương, hỏng hóc...)
- "Actions speak louder than words" = hành động chứng tỏ hơn lời nói

**Ví dụ:**
- We need to take immediate action to address the problem before it gets worse.
- He injured his knee and has been out of action for three weeks.
- Actions speak louder than words — show your commitment through your hard work.
- The government must decide on a course of action to tackle the housing crisis.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- take action, out of action
- in action, action movie
- a course of action
- actions speak louder than words

---

### 9. decision *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** quyết định

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a decision / decisions

**Lưu ý:**
- "Make a decision" = decide = đưa ra quyết định
- "A snap decision" = quyết định vội vàng không suy nghĩ kỹ
- "Decision fatigue" = mệt mỏi do phải đưa ra quá nhiều quyết định

**Ví dụ:**
- She took her time before making the difficult decision to change careers.
- The manager made a snap decision that later turned out to be a mistake.
- It is important to involve all team members in the decision-making process.
- Reaching a decision that everyone agrees on can take a very long time.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- make a decision, come to/reach a decision
- a difficult/tough decision, a snap decision
- decision-making, the final decision
- a life-changing decision

---

### 10. choice *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** sự lựa chọn (U); lựa chọn cụ thể (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a choice / choices (C); choice (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Have no choice but to" = buộc phải: "I had no choice but to accept."
- "By choice" = tự nguyện, không bị ép buộc
- "Spoilt for choice" (British) = có quá nhiều lựa chọn tốt

**Ví dụ:**
- She made the right choice by accepting the job offer in Singapore.
- He became a teacher by choice, not because he could not find another job.
- With so many restaurants on this street, we were completely spoilt for choice.
- Having too many choices can sometimes make it harder to decide.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- make a choice, have no choice
- by choice, a wise/poor choice
- freedom of choice, a matter of choice
- spoilt for choice

### 11. power *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** quyền lực (U); điện (U); quyền năng/cường quốc (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** power (U) / a power, powers (C)

**Lưu ý:**
- "In power" (không mạo từ) = đang nắm chính quyền
- "Soft power" = sức mạnh mềm (ảnh hưởng văn hóa/ngoại giao)
- "Power-sharing" = chia sẻ quyền lực

**Ví dụ:**
- The ruling party has been in power for over fifteen years.
- The United States and China are the two dominant world powers of the 21st century.
- Any abuse of power should be investigated and punished appropriately.
- The balance of power between the two political parties has shifted dramatically.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- come to/be in power
- world power / superpower
- power of attorney, abuse of power
- balance of power

### 12. authority *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** thẩm quyền, quyền hạn (U); nhà chức trách (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** authority (U) / an authority, authorities (C)

**Lưu ý:**
- Uncountable (thẩm quyền): "She has the authority to make this decision."
- Countable (tổ chức có thẩm quyền): "The local authorities have been informed."
- "An authority on" (C) = chuyên gia được công nhận: "She is an authority on ancient history."

**Ví dụ:**
- The manager does not have the authority to approve expenses over $10,000.
- The local authorities have introduced new measures to reduce traffic congestion.
- Professor Chen is a leading authority on ancient Chinese philosophy.
- Parents have the authority to make decisions on behalf of their young children.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- the authorities, in authority
- have authority over, abuse authority
- an authority on
- parental authority

### 13. organization *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** tổ chức; sự tổ chức/sắp xếp (uncountable)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** an organization / organizations (American); organisation / organisations (British)

**Lưu ý:**
- "NGO" = Non-Governmental Organization = tổ chức phi chính phủ
- "Organization" (C) = tổ chức cụ thể; "organization" (U) = khả năng tổ chức/sắp xếp
- "Organizational skills" = kỹ năng tổ chức công việc (rất được coi trọng)

**Ví dụ:**
- She works for a non-governmental organization that helps refugees in Southeast Asia.
- The World Health Organization plays a crucial role in global health emergencies.
- Good organization of your time and tasks is essential for productivity.
- He has excellent organizational skills and keeps the whole team on track.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- non-profit/non-governmental organization (NGO)
- join/lead an organization
- organizational skills
- the World Health Organization / WHO

### 14. institution *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** tổ chức/thể chế lớn; phong tục lâu đời

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** an institution / institutions

**Lưu ý:**
- "Institution" thường chỉ tổ chức phục vụ công cộng (ngân hàng, trường học, bệnh viện...)
- "Be an institution" (nghĩa bóng) = ai/thứ gì đó đã là biểu tượng không thể thiếu
- "Institutionalized" = được thể chế hóa (tích cực) hoặc bị ảnh hưởng lâu dài bởi thể chế (tiêu cực)

**Ví dụ:**
- Banks and universities are important financial and educational institutions in society.
- That tea shop on the corner has been here for fifty years — it's a local institution.
- The institution of democracy requires an informed and engaged citizenry.
- Marriage is one of the oldest social institutions in human history.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- financial/educational institution
- the institution of marriage
- be an institution
- mental institution (tránh dùng, thay bằng "psychiatric hospital")

### 15. community *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** cộng đồng

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a community / communities (collective noun)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Community" là collective noun — dùng "the community is/are" tùy văn phong
- "Community spirit" = tinh thần hỗ trợ lẫn nhau trong cộng đồng
- "Online community" = cộng đồng trực tuyến (ngày càng phổ biến)

**Ví dụ:**
- The local community came together to clean up the park after the festival.
- She volunteers regularly because she believes in giving back to the community.
- There is a strong sense of community spirit in this small town.
- Online communities allow people with shared interests to connect from around the world.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- local community, community center
- community service, community spirit
- the business/academic community
- gated community

### 16. society *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** xã hội (U); hội/tổ chức (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** society (U) / a society, societies (C)

**Lưu ý:**
- Uncountable (xã hội nói chung): "Corruption is a problem in society."
- Countable (tổ chức/hội): "She joined the local history society."
- "High society" = giới thượng lưu, xã hội thượng lưu

**Ví dụ:**
- Education plays a vital role in building a fair and equal society.
- He joined the university debating society and became an excellent public speaker.
- We live in an increasingly multicultural society where diversity should be celebrated.
- Every member of society has both rights and responsibilities.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- in society, civil society
- a debating society / drama society
- high society, society values
- multicultural society

### 17. culture *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** văn hóa (U); một nền văn hóa (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** culture (U) / a culture, cultures (C)

**Lưu ý:**
- Uncountable (văn hóa nói chung): "Culture is an important part of identity." / "popular culture"
- Countable (một nền văn hóa): "Eastern and Western cultures differ in many ways."
- "Culture shock" = cảm giác bỡ ngỡ khi tiếp xúc với văn hóa mới

**Ví dụ:**
- Vietnamese culture places great importance on respect for elders and family ties.
- The company has created a culture of innovation where new ideas are welcomed.
- Moving to a new country can cause culture shock, but it also broadens your perspective.
- Learning about different cultures helps us become more open-minded and tolerant.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- popular culture, company culture
- a culture of
- cross-cultural, culture shock
- cultural exchange

## Ôn tập nhanh (Quick Review)
Điền từ thích hợp vào chỗ trống:

1. The _______ passed a new law requiring all cyclists to wear helmets. (government / authority)
2. As _______ rule, the library is closed on public holidays. (a / the)
3. She is an _______ on ancient Chinese philosophy. (authority / expert — cả hai đúng)
4. We need to take immediate _______ to address the problem. (action / decision)
5. She became a teacher _______ choice, not necessity. (by / on)
6. There is a strong sense of community _______ in this town. (spirit / culture)
7. Living abroad can cause culture _______ at first. (shock / change)
8. "Actions speak louder than _______." (words / decisions)

**Đáp án:** 1. government 2. a 3. authority/expert 4. action 5. by 6. spirit 7. shock 8. words
