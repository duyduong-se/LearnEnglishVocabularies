## Từ học hôm nay

### 1. study *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** học, nghiên cứu

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| study | studied | studied |

**Lưu ý:**
- Regular — y → i + ed (vì trước y là phụ âm)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

study + noun → học/nghiên cứu điều gì
- She studies English for two hours every evening.
- He studied medicine at the University of Hanoi.

study for → học cho kỳ thi
- He stayed up late studying for the chemistry exam.
- She studied hard for the final exams and achieved excellent results.

study under + someone → học dưới sự hướng dẫn của ai
- He studied under one of the most respected professors in the country.
- Many successful scientists studied under influential mentors early in their careers.

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- study up on: ôn tập kỹ về → *You should study up on the history before the test.*

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- study hard, study abroad, study a subject

### 2. research *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** nghiên cứu

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| research | researched | researched |

**Lưu ý:**
- Regular — thêm -ed
- "research" KHÔNG dùng với "about" (research a topic, KHÔNG phải research about a topic)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

research + noun → nghiên cứu về điều gì
- I always research thoroughly before buying expensive products.
- The team researched consumer behavior for six months.

research into → nghiên cứu sâu vào
- Scientists will research the long-term effects of climate change.
- The university is currently researching into new renewable energy sources.

well-researched → được nghiên cứu kỹ
- A well-researched report builds credibility with the audience.
- Her presentation was well-researched and backed by solid data.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- research a topic, conduct research, research thoroughly

### 4. discover *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** khám phá

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| discover | discovered | discovered |

**Lưu ý:**
- Regular — thêm -ed
- Phân biệt: discover = tìm ra điều đã tồn tại; invent = tạo ra điều chưa từng có

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

- Children discover the world through play and exploration.
- Scientists discovered a new species of fish in the Amazon.

discover + that-clause → phát hiện rằng...
- Researchers will discover new treatments for cancer in the future.
- She discovered that the two samples had entirely different properties.

newly discovered → vừa được phát hiện
- The newly discovered planet has conditions similar to Earth.
- The museum unveiled a newly discovered painting attributed to a famous artist.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- discover a cure, discover the truth, make a discovery
