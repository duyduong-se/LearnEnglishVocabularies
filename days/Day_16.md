## Từ học hôm nay

### 1. push *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** đẩy

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| push | pushed | pushed |

**Lưu ý:**
- Regular — thêm -ed

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

push + noun → đẩy cái gì
- Please push the door to open it.
- She pushed her suitcase through the airport corridor.

push + someone + to-infinitive → thúc đẩy ai làm gì
- The manager pushed the team to finish the project early.
- His coach pushed him to train harder every day.

push for + noun → thúc đẩy để có điều gì
- We will push for better working conditions next year.
- The union is pushing for higher wages and improved benefits.

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- push back: trì hoãn / phản đối → *They pushed back the deadline by two weeks.*
- push through: cố vượt qua / thúc đẩy thông qua → *She pushed through the crowd.*

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- push a button, push the limits, push for change

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- drop off: thả ai xuống / ngủ gật → *Can you drop me off at the station?*

**Collocation phổ biến:**
