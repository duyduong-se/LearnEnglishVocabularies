## Từ học hôm nay

### 1. class *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** lớp học / buổi học (C); tầng lớp xã hội (C); đẳng cấp / phong cách (U)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a class / classes (C); class (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "in a class of your own" = xuất sắc đến mức không ai so sánh được
- "class" (U): "She has real class — elegant and gracious in everything she does."
- "class of 2024" = khóa tốt nghiệp năm 2024

**Ví dụ:**
- She never misses a class — she has a perfect attendance record this semester.
- The gap between the working class and the upper class has widened in recent decades.
- As a tennis player, she is in a class of her own — no one comes close to her level.
- The new teacher made the class engaging by mixing games with serious discussion.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- attend / take / miss a class, class of (2024)
- working class, middle class, upper class
- in a class of your own, have class

### 2. lesson *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** bài học; tiết học

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a lesson / lessons

**Lưu ý:**
- "teach sb a lesson" = dạy cho ai một bài học (thường nghĩa bóng — trừng phạt hoặc răn dạy)
- "let this be a lesson to you" = hãy rút ra bài học từ điều này
- Phân biệt: lesson (bài học — cụ thể) vs. class (lớp học — rộng hơn)

**Ví dụ:**
- She takes piano lessons every Saturday morning at the music school.
- Let this be a lesson to you — always back up your data before updating your software.
- Failing the exam was a valuable lesson that motivated him to study more effectively.
- The best lessons in life are not learned from books but from experience.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- learn a lesson, teach sb a lesson, piano/language lesson
- let this be a lesson to you, lesson plan
- take lessons (in/from), private lesson

### 3. course *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** khóa học; môn học; đường đi; món ăn

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a course / courses

**Lưu ý:**
- "of course" = tất nhiên rồi (KHÔNG phải "course" thôi — cần có "of")
- "a course of action" = phương án hành động: "What is the best course of action?"
- Bốn nghĩa: (1) khóa học, (2) đường đi của thuyền/bão, (3) sân golf, (4) món ăn trong bữa

**Ví dụ:**
- She enrolled in an online course to improve her data analysis skills.
- Of course you can borrow my notes — just make sure to return them before the exam.
- The best course of action is to speak directly to the manager about the problem.
- The dinner consisted of three courses: soup, roast lamb, and a chocolate dessert.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- take / do / complete a course, online course
- course of action, of course
- golf course, race course, starter/main/dessert course

### 4. subject *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** môn học; chủ đề; đối tượng (ngữ pháp/khoa học)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a subject / subjects

**Lưu ý:**
- "change the subject" = đổi sang chủ đề khác (tránh nói về điều gì)
- "subject to" = phụ thuộc vào, có điều kiện: "Prices are subject to change."
- Ba nghĩa: (1) môn học (school subjects), (2) chủ đề (the subject of the book), (3) chủ ngữ (grammar)

**Ví dụ:**
- Her favourite subjects at school were History and Literature.
- Whenever someone mentions money, he quickly changes the subject.
- The offer is subject to approval from the board of directors.
- The subject of climate change comes up in almost every political discussion these days.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- school subject, change the subject
- on the subject of, subject to (approval/change)
- a sensitive/taboo subject, drop the subject

### 5. test *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** bài kiểm tra; thử nghiệm; thách thức

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a test / tests

**Lưu ý:**
- Phân biệt: test (kiểm tra ngắn, thường trong lớp) vs. exam/examination (thi cử quan trọng hơn, thường cuối kỳ)
- "stand the test of time" = vẫn còn giá trị/phổ biến sau nhiều năm
- "the acid test" = thử thách quyết định, phép thử cuối cùng

**Ví dụ:**
- She passed the driving test on her third attempt and was overjoyed.
- The new product will face the acid test when it launches in the competitive market next month.
- Their friendship has stood the test of time — they have been close for over thirty years.
- Blood tests revealed that he had a vitamin deficiency that explained his fatigue.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- take / pass / fail a test, test results
- driving test, blood test, drug test
- stand the test of time, acid test

### 6. exam *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** kỳ thi; bài thi

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** an exam / exams

**Lưu ý:**
- BrE: "sit an exam" / AmE: "take an exam" — cả hai đều đúng
- "mock exam" = kỳ thi thử (luyện tập trước khi thi thật)
- "cram for an exam" = nhồi nhét kiến thức ngay trước kỳ thi

**Ví dụ:**
- He stayed up until midnight cramming for the history exam the following morning.
- She passed all her final exams with flying colours and graduated with honours.
- The entrance exam for this medical school is extremely competitive.
- Mock exams are useful because they give students practice under real exam conditions.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- sit / take / pass / fail an exam, exam results
- entrance exam, final exam, mock exam
- exam pressure, exam season, cram for an exam

### 7. grade *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** điểm số; lớp học (AmE); loại/cấp độ

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a grade / grades

**Lưu ý:**
- AmE: "grade" = lớp học: "first grade" (lớp 1), "fifth grade" (lớp 5)
- BrE: "year": "Year 1", "Year 5" (tương đương)
- "make the grade" = đủ tiêu chuẩn, đạt yêu cầu

**Ví dụ:**
- She worked hard all semester and finally got the grade she deserved.
- Not everyone makes the grade in this highly competitive profession.
- In the American school system, children start first grade at around six years old.
- His grades improved dramatically after he started attending the after-school tutoring programme.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- get / receive a grade, good / poor grade
- make the grade, grade school (AmE)
- grade point average (GPA)

### 8. degree *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** bằng (đại học); mức độ; độ (đo lường)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a degree / degrees

**Lưu ý:**
- Ba nghĩa: (1) bằng đại học, (2) mức độ: "to a certain degree", (3) đơn vị đo: "30 degrees Celsius"
- "to some degree" = ở một mức độ nào đó: "To some degree, I agree with you."
- "by degrees" = dần dần, từng bước một

**Ví dụ:**
- She earned her bachelor's degree in Economics from a top university in the UK.
- To some degree, success depends on luck as well as hard work.
- The temperature dropped to minus five degrees Celsius overnight.
- He is studying for a master's degree in Environmental Science while working full-time.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- get / earn a degree, bachelor's / master's / PhD degree
- to some degree / to a degree, degree of difficulty
- 360 degrees, temperature in degrees Celsius

### 9. university *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** trường đại học

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a university / universities

**Lưu ý:**
- BrE: "at university" (không mạo từ): "She's at university in London."
- AmE: "in college" hoặc "at university" — cả hai đều được
- "a university" — PHẢI nói "a" (không phải "an") vì "university" bắt đầu bằng âm /j/

**Ví dụ:**
- She was the first person in her family to go to university.
- The university offers scholarships to students from low-income backgrounds.
- While at university, she balanced her studies with part-time work at a local café.
- Getting into a top university requires not just high grades but also strong personal statements.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- go to / attend / apply to university
- university degree, university campus, university fees
- at university (BrE), in college (AmE)

### 10. college *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** trường đại học/cao đẳng (AmE); trường chuyên/cao đẳng (BrE)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a college / colleges (C); college (U, trong "at college")

**Lưu ý:**
- AmE: "college" thường đồng nghĩa với "university" (học 4 năm lấy bằng đại học)
- BrE: "college" thường là trường dạy nghề, cao đẳng, hoặc trường chuyên trong một đại học lớn
- "the Electoral College" = cử tri đoàn Mỹ — danh từ riêng

**Ví dụ:**
- He moved to Boston to attend college and study computer engineering.
- College life can be both exciting and challenging for first-year students.
- The college offers both full-time and part-time programmes for adult learners.
- She applied to several colleges and was offered a place at her first choice.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- go to college, college degree, college campus
- college student, college life, college application
- electoral college

### 11. library *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** thư viện

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a library / libraries

**Lưu ý:**
- "a library of" (nghĩa bóng) = kho lớn: "a library of films" (kho phim lớn)
- "library card" = thẻ thư viện (để mượn sách)
- Phát âm chú ý: /ˈlaɪbrəri/ (không bỏ âm "r" đầu)

**Ví dụ:**
- She spent three hours at the library researching for her history assignment.
- The new public library has a digital collection of over 50,000 e-books.
- The streaming service boasts a library of over ten thousand films and TV shows.
- The university library is open until midnight during the exam period.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- go to the library, public library, school library
- borrow from / return to the library, library card
- digital library, a library of information

### 12. laboratory *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** phòng thí nghiệm

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a laboratory / laboratories (viết tắt: lab / labs)

**Lưu ý:**
- Viết tắt phổ biến: "lab" — cả hai đều dùng trong mọi bối cảnh
- BrE phát âm: /ləˈbɒrətri/; AmE: /ˈlæbrətɔːri/
- "a language lab" = phòng luyện ngôn ngữ (với tai nghe và máy)

**Ví dụ:**
- The scientists spent months working in the laboratory to develop the new compound.
- The hospital laboratory processed over five hundred blood samples that day.
- Students use the language laboratory to practise their listening and speaking skills.
- Safety rules must always be followed in the laboratory to prevent accidents.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- work in a laboratory, laboratory test / experiment
- science laboratory, language laboratory
- state-of-the-art laboratory, laboratory results

### 13. lecture *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** bài giảng; bài thuyết trình học thuật; bài mắng (informal)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a lecture / lectures

**Lưu ý:**
- "get a lecture (from sb)" = bị ai đó giảng đạo/mắng (informal): "I got a lecture from my mum about being late."
- Phân biệt: lecture (bài giảng đại học — một chiều) vs. seminar (buổi thảo luận nhỏ — tương tác) vs. tutorial (hướng dẫn nhỏ 1-5 người)
- "a lecture theatre" / "a lecture hall" = giảng đường

**Ví dụ:**
- She attended a fascinating lecture on the history of artificial intelligence.
- The professor delivers his lectures with such enthusiasm that students never fall asleep.
- When I came home late, my father gave me a long lecture about responsibility.
- The lecture was recorded so that students who missed it could watch it online.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- give / deliver / attend a lecture, lecture theatre
- lecture on / about a topic, keynote lecture
- get a lecture from sb

### 14. essay *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** bài luận; bài tiểu luận

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** an essay / essays

**Lưu ý:**
- Phân biệt: essay (bài luận — thường cho học sinh/sinh viên) vs. article (bài báo/tạp chí) vs. paper (bài nghiên cứu học thuật)
- Cấu trúc essay: introduction → body paragraphs → conclusion
- "essay" còn là động từ (formal): "to essay sth" = cố gắng làm điều gì đó (rất hiếm dùng)

**Ví dụ:**
- She submitted her philosophy essay two days before the deadline.
- The teacher asked the students to write a 500-word essay on climate change.
- His personal essay for the university application was honest, thoughtful, and compelling.
- Writing a good essay requires planning, clear argument, and careful editing.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- write / submit an essay, essay question
- personal essay, academic essay, argumentative essay
- essay deadline, essay plan, essay marking

---

### 15. thesis *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** luận văn; luận điểm

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a thesis / theses (phát âm: /ˈθiːsiːz/)

**Lưu ý:**
- Phân biệt: thesis (luận văn thạc sĩ = Master's thesis; luận án tiến sĩ = doctoral thesis/dissertation)
- "thesis statement" = câu chủ đề/luận điểm chính của bài luận
- Số nhiều bất quy tắc: thesis → theses

**Ví dụ:**
- She spent two years writing and researching her doctoral thesis on environmental policy.
- The central thesis of the book is that technology improves human relationships rather than weakening them.
- He successfully defended his thesis and was awarded his doctorate last spring.
- A strong thesis statement is essential to guide the reader through your argument.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- write / submit / defend a thesis
- master's thesis, doctoral thesis, thesis statement
- the central thesis

---

### 16. curriculum *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** chương trình học; giáo trình

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a curriculum / curricula (hoặc curriculums)

**Lưu ý:**
- "extra-curricular activities" = hoạt động ngoại khóa (rất quan trọng trong đơn xin học)
- "curriculum vitae" (CV) = lý lịch khoa học/sơ yếu lý lịch (bắt nguồn từ Latin)
- Số nhiều: "curricula" (Latin) / "curriculums" (English) — cả hai đúng

**Ví dụ:**
- The national curriculum in England includes compulsory subjects such as English, Maths, and Science.
- The school is introducing coding into the curriculum from next year.
- Her extracurricular activities, including debate and volunteering, strengthened her university application.
- A well-designed curriculum balances academic content with practical, real-world skills.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- national curriculum, school curriculum, curriculum design
- add sth to / remove sth from the curriculum
- extra-curricular activities
