> **Lưu ý đặc biệt về bộ phận cơ thể:**
> - Hầu hết bộ phận cơ thể là **countable** (đếm được)
> - Khi nói chung về chức năng: dùng **"the"** → "The heart pumps blood."
> - Khi nói về bộ phận của ai đó cụ thể: dùng **possessive** (my/his/her) → "He broke **his** leg." (KHÔNG dùng "the leg")
> - Khi nói chung có nghĩa toàn bộ loài người: dùng không mạo từ → "Humans use language to communicate."

## Từ học hôm nay

### 1. body *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** cơ thể; thân thể; thi thể

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a body / bodies

**Lưu ý:**
- "Body language" = ngôn ngữ cơ thể (U) — không dùng mạo từ "a" trước khi nói chung
- "A governing body" = cơ quan quản lý (nghĩa mở rộng quan trọng)
- "Body of water" = khối nước; "body of work" = khối lượng tác phẩm

**Ví dụ:**
- The human body is made up of trillions of cells.
- Regular exercise helps keep your body healthy and strong.
- Her body language showed that she was nervous during the interview.
- The university has a governing body responsible for major decisions.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- human body, body language, body temperature
- keep fit / take care of your body
- body of water, body of work

---

### 2. head *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** đầu; người đứng đầu

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a head / heads

**Lưu ý:**
- "Use your head!" = Hãy suy nghĩ đi! (thành ngữ)
- "Head" còn có nghĩa là người đứng đầu: "the head of the company"
- "Per head" = mỗi người: "The meal costs $20 per head."

**Ví dụ:**
- She shook her head to show that she disagreed with the proposal.
- The head of the department called an urgent meeting this morning.
- Use your head before making such an important decision.
- The dinner costs $30 per head, including drinks.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- head of state, head of department, headache
- shake / nod your head, use your head
- head start, head office, per head

---

### 3. face *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** khuôn mặt; bề mặt

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a face / faces

**Lưu ý:**
- "Face to face" = gặp mặt trực tiếp (gạch nối khi là adj: "a face-to-face meeting")
- "Save face" = giữ thể diện
- "Face" còn là động từ: "face a problem" = đối mặt với vấn đề

**Ví dụ:**
- Her face lit up with joy when she heard the good news.
- It's better to discuss serious issues face to face rather than over text.
- He tried to save face after making the embarrassing mistake in public.
- The clock face was damaged when it fell off the wall.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- face to face, save face, face value
- pull a face, make a face
- the face of (= đại diện cho)

### 4. eye *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** mắt

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** an eye / eyes

**Lưu ý:**
- "Keep an eye on" = trông chừng: "Keep an eye on the children."
- "See eye to eye" = đồng ý với nhau: "We don't always see eye to eye."
- "The naked eye" = mắt thường (không có kính)

**Ví dụ:**
- She has beautiful green eyes that catch everyone's attention.
- Could you keep an eye on my bag while I go to the restroom?
- Stars are too far away to see with the naked eye.
- We don't always see eye to eye, but we respect each other's opinions.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- keep an eye on, catch sb's eye
- eye contact, naked eye
- in the eye of (= quan điểm của)

---

### 5. ear *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** tai

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** an ear / ears

**Lưu ý:**
- "Play it by ear" = ứng biến, tùy cơ ứng biến (thành ngữ phổ biến)
- "Turn a deaf ear to" = làm ngơ, giả vờ không nghe
- "Have an ear for" = có tai thẩm âm tốt cho âm nhạc/ngôn ngữ

**Ví dụ:**
- She has a wonderful ear for languages and picks them up quickly.
- He played the melody by ear without looking at any sheet music.
- The manager turned a deaf ear to the employees' complaints about the policy.
- I don't have a firm plan yet — let's just play it by ear.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- have a good ear (for music/language), turn a deaf ear
- earache, eardrum, earring
- play by ear

### 6. nose *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** mũi

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a nose / noses

**Lưu ý:**
- "Turn up your nose at" = chê bai, coi thường
- "Have a nose for" = có khiếu/nhạy cảm với: "She has a nose for a good story."
- "Follow your nose" = đi thẳng / làm theo bản năng

**Ví dụ:**
- He had a runny nose and kept sneezing throughout the meeting.
- A good journalist needs to have a nose for interesting stories.
- She turned up her nose at the simple meal, expecting something more elaborate.
- Just follow your nose and you will find the market easily.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- runny nose, stuffy nose, nosebleed
- blow your nose, turn up your nose (at)
- have a nose for

### 7. mouth *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** miệng; cửa (sông, hang)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a mouth / mouths

**Lưu ý:**
- "Word of mouth" = truyền miệng: "The restaurant became famous by word of mouth."
- "Keep your mouth shut" = im lặng, không tiết lộ
- "The mouth of the river" = cửa sông (chỗ đổ ra biển)

**Ví dụ:**
- Don't talk with your mouth full — it's considered rude.
- The restaurant built its reputation through word of mouth alone.
- The city is located near the mouth of the Mekong River.
- She kept her mouth shut about what she had seen.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- open / close your mouth, keep your mouth shut
- word of mouth, mouth-watering
- the mouth of a river/cave

### 8. hand *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** bàn tay; kim đồng hồ; người giúp việc

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a hand / hands

**Lưu ý:**
- "Give sb a hand" = (1) giúp đỡ: "Can you give me a hand?" / (2) vỗ tay: "Give her a hand!"
- "On the other hand" = mặt khác (linking phrase)
- "Out of hand" = mất kiểm soát

**Ví dụ:**
- Could you give me a hand moving these boxes to the storage room?
- The situation got out of hand when the crowd started pushing.
- She always works hand in hand with her colleagues to achieve the best results.
- On the one hand, the salary is good; on the other hand, the hours are long.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- give sb a hand, shake hands
- hand in hand, out of hand
- firsthand / secondhand

### 9. arm *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** cánh tay; vũ khí (arms, số nhiều)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** an arm / arms

**Lưu ý:**
- "Arms" (plural) = vũ khí: "arms deal", "arms race" — nghĩa hoàn toàn khác với cánh tay
- "Welcome sb with open arms" = đón tiếp ai đó nồng nhiệt
- "Up in arms" = giận dữ, phản đối mạnh mẽ

**Ví dụ:**
- He broke his arm while playing football and had to wear a cast for six weeks.
- The couple walked arm in arm along the riverside in the evening.
- The local residents were up in arms about the plan to build a factory nearby.
- The new teacher was welcomed with open arms by the students.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- arm in arm, open arms
- arms race, arms deal
- with open arms, up in arms (about sth)

### 10. leg *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** chân (từ hông đến bàn chân); giai đoạn (của hành trình)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a leg / legs

**Lưu ý:**
- "Break a leg!" = Chúc may mắn! (thành ngữ dùng trong biểu diễn sân khấu)
- "He broke his leg" — dùng possessive, KHÔNG phải "He broke the leg"
- "Leg" của bàn/ghế = chân bàn/ghế

**Ví dụ:**
- She fractured her leg during the skiing trip and needed surgery.
- After sitting for hours, he stood up to stretch his legs.
- "Break a leg!" said her friend before she walked onto the stage.
- The first leg of the journey takes us from Hanoi to Da Nang.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- break a leg, leg room
- stretch your legs
- the first/last leg of a journey

### 11. foot *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** bàn chân; đơn vị đo (feet = 30.48 cm)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a foot / feet (bất quy tắc)

**Lưu ý:**
- "On foot" = đi bộ (không mạo từ)
- "Foot the bill" = trả hóa đơn (thường nghĩa bóng: chịu chi phí)
- Số nhiều bất quy tắc: foot → feet (không phải "foots")

**Ví dụ:**
- He injured his foot while jogging and couldn't walk properly for a week.
- The village is accessible only on foot — there's no road to it.
- The answers are at the foot of the page.
- The company always foots the bill for the team's annual dinner.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- on foot, set foot (in/on), foot the bill
- the foot of the mountain/stairs/page
- put your foot in it

### 12. heart *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** tim (C); trái tim/tình cảm (U); trung tâm (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a heart / hearts (C); heart (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Learn sth by heart" = học thuộc lòng
- "At heart" = về bản chất: "She's a kind person at heart."
- "The heart of the city" = trung tâm thành phố (nghĩa bóng)

**Ví dụ:**
- The heart pumps blood to every part of the body continuously.
- She learned all the poems by heart before the performance.
- Despite his tough appearance, he is a gentle person at heart.
- The old market is located in the heart of the city.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- heart attack, heart disease, heartbeat
- by heart, at heart, from the heart
- the heart of (= trung tâm của)

### 13. brain *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** não (C); trí tuệ (U)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a brain / brains (C); brains (U, không mạo từ)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Brains" (U, không mạo từ) = trí tuệ: "She has both beauty and brains."
- "Rack your brains" = vắt óc suy nghĩ
- "The brains of the operation" = bộ óc của chiến dịch

**Ví dụ:**
- The human brain is the most complex organ in the known universe.
- She's the brains behind all the company's most successful marketing campaigns.
- I racked my brains all night trying to remember where I had left my keys.
- He has the brains to succeed but needs more discipline.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- use your brain, brain damage, brain tumor
- have brains, rack your brains
- the brains behind sth

### 14. blood *(noun — uncountable)*
**Nghĩa:** máu; dòng máu/huyết thống

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** blood / (không có số nhiều)

**Lưu ý:**
- "In cold blood" = máu lạnh, có chủ đích: "He killed in cold blood."
- "Blood is thicker than water." = Máu mủ ruột thịt quan trọng hơn quan hệ bạn bè (tục ngữ)
- "Bad blood" (between sb) = mối thù/căng thẳng tích tụ

**Ví dụ:**
- He lost a lot of blood in the accident and needed an emergency transfusion.
- Regular blood tests can detect many health problems early.
- Despite their disagreements, she helped her brother — blood is thicker than water.
- There has always been bad blood between the two rival families.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- blood pressure, blood type, blood test, blood donation
- in cold blood, blood is thicker than water
- shed blood, lose blood

### 15. bone *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** xương

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a bone / bones

**Lưu ý:**
- "Bone dry" = hoàn toàn khô kiệt
- "Chilled to the bone" = lạnh thấu xương
- "Have a bone to pick with sb" = có chuyện muốn nói thẳng/phàn nàn với ai đó

**Ví dụ:**
- She broke a bone in her wrist when she fell off the bicycle.
- After standing in the rain for hours, he was chilled to the bone.
- I have a bone to pick with you — why didn't you tell me about the meeting?
- The archaeologists found ancient human bones at the excavation site.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- break a bone, bone fracture, backbone
- bone dry, chilled to the bone
- have a bone to pick with sb

### 16. skin *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** da (U); vỏ trái cây (C); tấm da thuộc (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a skin / skins (C); skin (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "By the skin of your teeth" = thoát hiểm trong gang tấc
- "Get under sb's skin" = (1) làm ai khó chịu / (2) chạm đến cảm xúc sâu thẳm của ai
- "Thick-skinned" = dày mặt, không bị tổn thương dễ dàng

**Ví dụ:**
- It's important to protect your skin from the sun by wearing sunscreen.
- He passed the exam by the skin of his teeth — he got exactly the minimum score.
- Her enthusiasm for teaching really got under his skin and inspired him.
- The recipe calls for peeling off the skin of the tomatoes before cooking.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- skin care, skin type, skin cancer
- by the skin of your teeth
- get under sb's skin

---

### 17. health *(noun — uncountable)*
**Nghĩa:** sức khỏe

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** health / (không có số nhiều)

**Lưu ý:**
- Thường không có mạo từ: "Health is wealth."
- "Health" vs. "healthcare": health = trạng thái sức khỏe; healthcare = hệ thống y tế
- "To your health!" = Chúc sức khỏe! (lúc nâng ly)

**Ví dụ:**
- Good health is the most valuable asset a person can have.
- Mental health is just as important as physical health.
- The government invested heavily in public health to prevent disease outbreaks.
- She drinks green tea every morning for the benefit of her health.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- good / poor health, mental health, public health
- health care, health system, health risk
- be in good/bad health, improve / damage your health
