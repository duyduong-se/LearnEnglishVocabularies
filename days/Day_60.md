## Từ học hôm nay

### 1. skill *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** kỹ năng; kỹ xảo

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a skill / skills

**Lưu ý:**
- "soft skills" = kỹ năng mềm (giao tiếp, làm việc nhóm, lãnh đạo)
- "hard skills" = kỹ năng cứng (lập trình, kế toán, ngoại ngữ — đo được cụ thể)
- "transferable skills" = kỹ năng có thể áp dụng ở nhiều lĩnh vực

**Ví dụ:**
- Communication skills are among the most valued skills in any workplace.
- She developed her graphic design skills through online courses and practice.
- The years he spent teaching gave him transferable skills useful in management.
- Employers increasingly value soft skills such as teamwork, adaptability, and problem-solving.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- develop / acquire / master a skill, transferable skills
- communication skills, social skills, life skills
- soft skills, hard skills

---

### 2. ability *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** khả năng; năng lực

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** an ability / abilities (C); ability (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "to the best of your ability" = hết sức mình, theo khả năng tốt nhất
- Phân biệt: ability (năng lực — thường có thể đo được) vs. talent (thiên tài — thiên bẩm) vs. skill (kỹ năng — học được)
- "mixed ability" (class) = lớp học với học sinh ở nhiều trình độ khác nhau

**Ví dụ:**
- She has an amazing ability to remain calm under the most intense pressure.
- Please complete the form to the best of your ability and return it by Friday.
- The school runs mixed-ability classes to ensure all students are included.
- His natural ability in mathematics was noticed by his teachers from an early age.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- the ability to do sth, mixed ability (classes)
- natural ability, ability to communicate/learn
- to the best of your ability

---

### 3. talent *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** tài năng (U); người tài (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a talent / talents (C); talent (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- Phân biệt: talent (thiên bẩm tự nhiên) vs. skill (kỹ năng học được) vs. ability (năng lực — có thể thiên bẩm hoặc học)
- "talent show" = cuộc thi tìm kiếm tài năng (America's Got Talent...)
- "a talent for" = khiếu về: "She has a real talent for languages."

**Ví dụ:**
- She has a natural talent for music — she could play the violin perfectly at the age of five.
- The talent scout watched the local football match looking for promising young players.
- Hard work can compensate for a lack of natural talent in most fields.
- The company actively recruits local talent rather than hiring from abroad.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- natural talent, talent for (music/art), hidden talent
- nurture / develop talent, talent show
- a talent scout, local talent

---

### 4. concept *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** khái niệm; ý tưởng trừu tượng

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a concept / concepts

**Lưu ý:**
- "proof of concept" = bằng chứng khả thi (kiểm tra ý tưởng trước khi thực hiện đầy đủ)
- "concept car" = xe concept (mẫu xe thử nghiệm không bán đại trà)
- Phân biệt: concept (khái niệm trừu tượng) vs. idea (ý tưởng — rộng hơn, có thể cụ thể)

**Ví dụ:**
- The concept of democracy is based on the idea that all citizens have equal political rights.
- Some students struggle to grasp abstract concepts without concrete examples.
- The startup created a proof of concept before seeking major investment.
- Quantum physics deals with concepts that challenge our everyday understanding of the world.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- understand / grasp / introduce a concept
- the concept of (justice/freedom), abstract concept
- concept car, concept album, proof of concept

---

### 5. model *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** mô hình; hình mẫu; người mẫu; phiên bản

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a model / models

**Lưu ý:**
- "role model" = người mà mình noi theo, học tập
- "a model of sth" = mẫu mực về điều gì: "She is a model of patience."
- "model" còn là động từ: "model clothes" (người mẫu trình diễn quần áo)

**Ví dụ:**
- Her mother was her greatest role model, inspiring her to pursue a career in medicine.
- Scientists use computer models to predict the effects of climate change.
- The new business model proved so successful that competitors quickly copied it.
- She started her career as a fashion model before transitioning to acting.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- role model, fashion model
- a model of (efficiency), business model, economic model
- model behaviour, the latest model (of a car/phone)

---

### 6. structure *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** cấu trúc (U); công trình kiến trúc (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a structure / structures (C); structure (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- structure (U): "The essay lacks structure." (cấu trúc bài viết)
- a structure (C): "The bridge is an impressive steel structure." (công trình)
- "structured" (adj) = có cấu trúc: "a structured approach"; "unstructured" = không có cấu trúc

**Ví dụ:**
- The essay needs more structure — each paragraph should focus on one clear idea.
- The ancient pyramid is one of the most remarkable structures ever built by humans.
- Understanding the social structure of a society helps explain many of its problems.
- A clear structure is essential in both writing and in building a successful organisation.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- organisational structure, sentence structure, price structure
- the structure of (DNA/society), impose a structure
- a solid / rigid structure, power structure

---

### 7. pattern *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** mô hình; khuôn mẫu; họa tiết; quy luật

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a pattern / patterns

**Lưu ý:**
- Hai nghĩa chính: (1) quy luật/mẫu hành vi: "behavior patterns" / (2) họa tiết trang trí: "a floral pattern"
- "a recurring pattern" = quy luật lặp đi lặp lại
- "break the pattern" = phá vỡ quy luật

**Ví dụ:**
- Scientists identified a clear pattern in the way the disease spread through the population.
- Her sleep patterns changed dramatically after she started working night shifts.
- The carpet has a beautiful floral pattern in shades of blue and gold.
- If you can recognise the pattern, solving the problem becomes much easier.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- behaviour pattern, sleep pattern, weather pattern
- follow / see / detect a pattern, set a pattern
- a pattern of (behaviour/events), recurring pattern

---

### 8. evidence *(noun — uncountable)*
**Nghĩa:** bằng chứng; chứng cứ

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** evidence (không có số nhiều — không nói "evidences" hay "an evidence")

**Lưu ý:**
- "an evidence" là SAI — phải nói "a piece of evidence"
- "in evidence" = rõ ràng có mặt, thấy rõ: "His talent is much in evidence in this work."
- "circumstantial evidence" = bằng chứng gián tiếp (không trực tiếp chứng minh)

**Ví dụ:**
- The prosecution presented compelling evidence that placed the suspect at the crime scene.
- There is growing evidence that regular meditation improves mental health and focus.
- His dedication and passion were much in evidence throughout the entire project.
- Without solid evidence, the investigation could not proceed to a formal charge.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- provide / present / gather evidence, hard evidence
- lack of evidence, evidence that..., in evidence
- circumstantial evidence, forensic evidence

---

### 9. conclusion *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** kết luận; kết thúc

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a conclusion / conclusions

**Lưu ý:**
- "jump to conclusions" = vội vàng kết luận khi chưa có đủ bằng chứng
- "a foregone conclusion" = kết luận đã được định sẵn, không có gì bất ngờ: "His victory was a foregone conclusion."
- "in conclusion" = để kết luận (thường ở đoạn văn cuối bài luận)

**Ví dụ:**
- The study reached the conclusion that diet and exercise are equally important for health.
- Don't jump to conclusions before you have heard the full story from both sides.
- In conclusion, the evidence strongly supports the need for immediate action on climate change.
- Her surprising conclusion overturned everything researchers had previously believed.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- reach / draw / come to a conclusion, in conclusion
- jump to conclusions, foregone conclusion
- the conclusion of (the story/meeting)

---

### 10. argument *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** lý lẽ/luận điểm (học thuật); cuộc tranh luận/cãi vã

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** an argument / arguments

**Lưu ý:**
- Hai nghĩa: (1) luận điểm logic trong bài viết/thảo luận: "a compelling argument", (2) cuộc cãi vã: "a heated argument"
- "a strong argument for/against" = luận điểm mạnh mẽ ủng hộ/phản đối
- "argument" ≠ "discussion" — argument thường có tính đối lập/tranh cãi hơn

**Ví dụ:**
- She made a compelling argument for investing in renewable energy rather than fossil fuels.
- They had a heated argument over whose turn it was to do the dishes.
- There is a strong argument to be made for introducing financial education in schools.
- His argument was well structured and impossible to dismiss without careful thought.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- have / win / lose an argument, heated argument
- make / put forward an argument
- a strong argument (for/against), line of argument

---

### 11. logic *(noun — uncountable)*
**Nghĩa:** logic; tư duy lý luận

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** logic (không có số nhiều)

**Lưu ý:**
- "the logic of" = cái lý của: "I don't understand the logic of this decision."
- "faulty/twisted logic" = lý luận sai lệch/méo mó
- "There's no logic in..." = Không có gì logic trong: "There's no logic in getting angry about it."

**Ví dụ:**
- The logic of the argument is clear, but the evidence doesn't fully support it.
- It defies all logic to spend more money than you earn every month.
- She applied logic and careful analysis to solve the complex mathematical problem.
- Sometimes people act against their own logic because emotions override reason.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- follow the logic, logic suggests / dictates
- the logic behind sth, twisted logic
- logical (adj), logically (adv), illogical (adj)

---

### 12. analysis *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** phân tích (U); bản phân tích (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** an analysis / analyses (phát âm: /əˈnæləsiːz/)

**Lưu ý:**
- Số nhiều bất quy tắc: analysis → analyses
- "in the final analysis" = xét cho cùng, cuối cùng mà nói: "In the final analysis, it's your decision."
- "analyse" (BrE) / "analyze" (AmE) là động từ tương ứng

**Ví dụ:**
- The team conducted a detailed analysis of the market before launching the new product.
- In the final analysis, what matters most is whether the patients are getting better.
- She specialises in data analysis and helps companies make sense of large datasets.
- A thorough analysis of the survey results revealed some surprising trends.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- conduct / carry out an analysis, data analysis
- cost-benefit analysis, in-depth analysis
- in the final analysis, under analysis

---

### 13. summary *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** tóm tắt; bản tóm lược

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a summary / summaries

**Lưu ý:**
- "executive summary" = tóm tắt điều hành (ở đầu báo cáo/đề xuất kinh doanh)
- "in summary" = tóm lại (dùng để kết thúc một lập luận)
- "summarise" (BrE) / "summarize" (AmE) = tóm tắt (động từ)

**Ví dụ:**
- Please provide a brief summary of the meeting for those who were unable to attend.
- In summary, the report recommends three key changes to the company's strategy.
- The executive summary allowed senior managers to grasp the key findings in five minutes.
- A good summary captures the main points without including unnecessary detail.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- write / provide a summary, brief summary
- executive summary, in summary
- a summary of events/findings

---

### 14. vocabulary *(noun — uncountable)*
**Nghĩa:** từ vựng; vốn từ

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** vocabulary (không có số nhiều — không nói "vocabularies" hay "a vocabulary" trong nghĩa thông thường)

**Lưu ý:**
- "active vocabulary" = từ vựng tích cực (từ bạn có thể dùng trong nói/viết)
- "passive vocabulary" = từ vựng thụ động (từ bạn hiểu khi đọc/nghe nhưng chưa dùng được)
- "have a wide/large vocabulary" = có vốn từ phong phú

**Ví dụ:**
- Reading widely is one of the most effective ways to expand your vocabulary.
- She has an impressive vocabulary for someone who has only been learning English for two years.
- Building vocabulary is a daily habit — learning just ten new words a day adds up quickly.
- Technical vocabulary varies greatly between different professions and fields of study.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- build / expand / improve vocabulary, active / passive vocabulary
- a wide / large / rich vocabulary, vocabulary list
- technical vocabulary, vocabulary building
