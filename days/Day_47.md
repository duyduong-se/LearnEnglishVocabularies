## Từ học hôm nay

### 1. vote *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** lá phiếu (C); cuộc bỏ phiếu (C); quyền bầu cử (U)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a vote / votes (C); the vote (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Vote" còn là động từ: "to vote for/against sth"
- "The popular vote" = tổng số phiếu của cử tri (khác với Electoral College ở Mỹ)
- "Get the vote" = giành được quyền bầu cử

**Ví dụ:**
- Each citizen has the right to cast one vote in the election.
- The proposal was passed by a vote of 15 to 3.
- Women in many countries fought for decades to get the vote.
- The winning candidate received over 60% of the popular vote.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- cast a vote, win votes, count the votes
- vote of confidence, popular vote, majority vote
- get the vote

### 2. party *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** đảng (chính trị); bữa tiệc; bên (trong hợp đồng)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a party / parties

**Lưu ý:**
- Ba nghĩa chính: (1) đảng chính trị, (2) bữa tiệc, (3) một bên trong thỏa thuận/hợp đồng
- "The third party" = bên thứ ba (trong hợp đồng hoặc bảo hiểm)
- Context quyết định nghĩa: political context = đảng; social context = tiệc

**Ví dụ:**
- The ruling party lost the election for the first time in twenty years.
- We're throwing a party to celebrate her promotion.
- Both parties must sign the contract for it to be valid.
- She joined a political party because she wanted to make a difference.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- political party, ruling party, opposition party
- join / leave / support a party, party leader, party member
- birthday party, dinner party, house party

### 3. democracy *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** nền dân chủ (C); chế độ dân chủ (U)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a democracy / democracies (C); democracy (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- Uncountable (nguyên tắc): "Democracy requires active citizen participation."
- Countable (nhà nước): "Sweden is one of the world's strongest democracies."
- Liên quan: democratic (adj), democrat (n)

**Ví dụ:**
- Democracy gives citizens the power to choose their own leaders.
- The country transitioned to a democracy after years of authoritarian rule.
- Freedom of speech is a cornerstone of any functioning democracy.
- Protecting democratic values requires the active participation of citizens.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- liberal democracy, direct democracy, representative democracy
- promote / defend / threaten democracy
- democracy works / fails, democratic values

---

### 4. freedom *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** tự do

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a freedom / freedoms (C); freedom (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Freedom from" = tự do khỏi điều gì đó tiêu cực: "freedom from fear/poverty"
- "Freedom to" = tự do làm gì: "the freedom to choose"
- "Freedoms" (plural C) = các quyền tự do cụ thể

**Ví dụ:**
- Freedom of speech is protected by the constitution in most democracies.
- Moving abroad gave her the freedom to start a new life.
- They fought for their political freedoms for generations.
- Every person deserves freedom from fear and oppression.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- freedom of speech/press/religion, personal freedom
- fight for / win / lose freedom
- give sb the freedom to do sth, freedom from (sth bad)

### 5. justice *(noun — uncountable)*
**Nghĩa:** công lý; sự công bằng

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** justice / (không có số nhiều trong nghĩa "công lý")

**Lưu ý:**
- Thường không có mạo từ: "Justice must be served."
- "A Justice" (C, viết hoa) = một thẩm phán tòa tối cao
- "Justice" vs. "fairness": justice = công lý (theo luật), fairness = sự công bằng (cảm giác)

**Ví dụ:**
- The victims demanded justice after years of waiting for a verdict.
- The justice system must treat all people equally, regardless of wealth.
- The criminal was finally brought to justice after a ten-year investigation.
- She dedicated her life to fighting for social justice.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- criminal justice, social justice, justice system
- seek / demand / deliver justice, bring sb to justice
- justice is served, in the name of justice

### 6. crime *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** tội phạm (U); hành vi phạm tội (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a crime / crimes (C); crime (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- Uncountable (tội phạm nói chung): "Crime is a serious problem in many cities."
- Countable (một vụ phạm tội cụ thể): "He committed a serious crime."
- "It's a crime!" = Thật là uổng phí! (nghĩa bóng)

**Ví dụ:**
- Theft is a crime that can result in imprisonment.
- The government launched a campaign to reduce violent crime in the city.
- It would be a crime to waste this opportunity.
- The detective spent years investigating organized crime in the region.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- violent crime, organized crime, petty crime, hate crime
- commit a crime, fight crime, crime rate
- crime scene, crime wave, crime novel

### 7. punishment *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** hình phạt; sự trừng phạt

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a punishment / punishments (C); punishment (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Capital punishment" = án tử hình
- "Corporal punishment" = trừng phạt thể xác (đòn roi)
- Liên quan: punish (v), punitive (adj)

**Ví dụ:**
- The punishment for drug trafficking in some countries is life imprisonment.
- Many schools have banned corporal punishment for students.
- He was given community service as a punishment for the minor offence.
- The severity of the punishment should match the seriousness of the crime.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- capital punishment, corporal punishment
- give / face / receive punishment, escape punishment
- punishment for (doing) sth, as a punishment

### 8. prison *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** nhà tù (C); tù giam (U)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a prison / prisons (C); prison (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "In prison" (không mạo từ) = đang bị giam: "He is in prison." (giống "at school", "in hospital")
- "The prison" = tòa nhà nhà tù: "The prison was built in 1890."
- Phân biệt: prison (nhà tù, tội nặng) vs. jail (trại giam tạm, tội nhẹ — dùng nhiều ở AmE)

**Ví dụ:**
- He was sent to prison for five years for fraud.
- The new prison was built to hold up to 500 inmates.
- After ten years in prison, she was finally released on good behaviour.
- The conditions inside the prison were reported to be very poor.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- go to prison, be in prison, send sb to prison, release from prison
- prison sentence, prison cell, prison guard
- maximum-security prison

### 9. court *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** tòa án; sân (thể thao)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a court / courts

**Lưu ý:**
- "In court" = đang ở tòa xét xử (không mạo từ)
- "A basketball court" / "a tennis court" = sân bóng rổ / sân tennis
- "Food court" = khu ẩm thực trong trung tâm thương mại

**Ví dụ:**
- The case was brought to court after months of failed negotiations.
- The Supreme Court made a landmark ruling on freedom of speech.
- They played tennis on the outdoor court every weekend.
- The witness was asked to tell the truth in court.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- go to court, take sb to court, appear in court
- court of law, Supreme Court, High Court
- tennis court, basketball court, food court

### 10. judge *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** thẩm phán; giám khảo

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a judge / judges

**Lưu ý:**
- "Judge" còn là động từ: "Don't judge a book by its cover."
- "A good judge of character" = người biết đánh giá tính cách người khác
- "Panel of judges" = ban giám khảo

**Ví dụ:**
- The judge sentenced the defendant to three years in prison.
- She served as a judge on the panel for the international film festival.
- He is a good judge of character and rarely trusts the wrong people.
- The judge asked both sides to present their evidence clearly.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- federal judge, senior judge, chief judge
- judge rules / decides / sentences
- panel of judges

---

### 11. tax *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** thuế

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a tax / taxes (C); tax (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- Uncountable (thuế nói chung): "Tax is used to fund public services."
- Countable (một loại thuế cụ thể): "A new tax on sugary drinks was introduced."
- "Tax evasion" = trốn thuế (bất hợp pháp) vs. "tax avoidance" = tránh thuế (hợp pháp nhưng phi đạo đức)

**Ví dụ:**
- All citizens earning above a certain income must pay income tax.
- The government announced a new tax on sugary drinks to combat obesity.
- Small businesses received a tax break to help them recover from the pandemic.
- He was convicted of tax evasion and fined a large sum.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- income tax, sales tax, property tax, carbon tax
- pay / raise / cut / reduce taxes
- tax return, tax rate, tax evasion, tax break

### 12. budget *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** ngân sách; kế hoạch tài chính

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a budget / budgets

**Lưu ý:**
- "On a budget" = tiết kiệm tiền: "traveling on a budget"
- "Budget" dùng như tính từ: "a budget hotel" = khách sạn giá rẻ
- "Within budget" = trong phạm vi ngân sách; "over budget" = vượt ngân sách

**Ví dụ:**
- The government presented its annual budget to parliament yesterday.
- The project was completed on time and within budget.
- We traveled across Europe on a tight budget, staying in hostels.
- The marketing team had to cut its budget by 20% this year.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- annual budget, national budget, tight budget
- set / manage / cut / exceed a budget
- on a budget, within budget, over budget

---

### 13. investment *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** khoản đầu tư (C); sự đầu tư (U)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** an investment / investments (C); investment (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Return on investment" (ROI) = lợi nhuận từ khoản đầu tư — thuật ngữ kinh doanh quan trọng
- "A good investment" = một khoản đầu tư tốt (cũng dùng nghĩa bóng: "Education is a good investment.")
- "Investment bank" = ngân hàng đầu tư

**Ví dụ:**
- The government is seeking foreign investment to develop the country's infrastructure.
- Buying a house was the best investment she ever made.
- Education is an investment in the future that always pays off.
- The company's new investment strategy focuses on renewable energy.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- foreign investment, long-term investment, return on investment (ROI)
- make an investment, attract investment
- investment bank, investment strategy, investment opportunity

### 14. profit *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** lợi nhuận

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a profit / profits (C); profit (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Make a profit" / "turn a profit" = kiếm được lợi nhuận
- "Non-profit" (adj/n) = phi lợi nhuận: "a non-profit organization"
- Đối lập: profit vs. loss

**Ví dụ:**
- The company made a huge profit last quarter, surprising investors.
- The restaurant barely made a profit in its first year of operation.
- She sells handmade jewelry at a profit every weekend at the market.
- The company donated ten percent of its profits to charity.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- make a profit, earn a profit, turn a profit
- gross profit, net profit, profit margin
- for profit, non-profit (organization)

---

### 15. loss *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** sự mất mát (C/U); thua lỗ (C/U)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a loss / losses (C); loss (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "At a loss" = bối rối, không biết phải làm gì: "I'm at a loss for words."
- "Cut your losses" = dừng lại để không thua lỗ thêm (thành ngữ)
- "Job loss" / "hair loss" / "weight loss" = mất việc / rụng tóc / giảm cân

**Ví dụ:**
- The company reported a loss of $2 million in its annual financial statement.
- The loss of her father was the most difficult experience of her life.
- When asked for his opinion, he was completely at a loss for words.
- The team accepted the loss gracefully and congratulated the winners.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- financial loss, job loss, hair loss, weight loss
- suffer a loss, make a loss, cut losses
- at a loss, loss of life, profit and loss

---

### 16. crisis *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** khủng hoảng; thời điểm nguy cấp

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a crisis / crises (bất quy tắc — phát âm: /ˈkraɪsiːz/)

**Lưu ý:**
- Số nhiều bất quy tắc: crisis → crises (không phải "crisises")
- "Mid-life crisis" = khủng hoảng tuổi trung niên (thường khoảng 40–50 tuổi)
- "Crisis management" = quản lý khủng hoảng

**Ví dụ:**
- The financial crisis of 2008 affected economies around the world.
- The country is facing a political crisis following the resignation of the prime minister.
- Climate change is considered the most serious crisis of our time.
- The company had a crisis management plan ready in case of emergency.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- financial crisis, economic crisis, political crisis, climate crisis
- face / cause / manage / resolve a crisis
- in crisis, crisis management, crisis point, mid-life crisis
