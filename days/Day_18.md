## Từ học hôm nay

### 1. collect *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** thu thập

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| collect | collected | collected |

**Lưu ý:**
- Regular — thêm -ed
- Phân biệt với "gather": collect thường có hệ thống hơn, mang tính chọn lọc

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

collect + noun → thu thập/sưu tầm cái gì
- He collects vintage coins as a hobby.
- The researcher collected samples from ten different sites.

collect from → thu thập từ
- The team will collect feedback from users after the launch.
- Data is collected from thousands of participants across the country.

be collected → được thu thập/đón (xe đón)
- The children are collected from school by their parents every afternoon.
- All completed forms will be collected and processed by Friday.

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- collect on: đòi/thu tiền → *The bank collected on the unpaid debt.*

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- collect data, collect stamps, collect evidence
