## Từ học hôm nay

### 1. book *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** đặt chỗ/phòng

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| book | booked | booked |

**Lưu ý:**
- Regular — thêm -ed
- "Book" (British English) = "reserve" — thường dùng cho khách sạn, nhà hàng, vé

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

book + noun → đặt cái gì (vé, phòng, bàn)
- I always book my flights at least two weeks in advance.
- He booked a hotel room for the business trip next month.

book in advance → đặt trước
- She booked a table for four at the Italian restaurant.
- It is advisable to book tickets in advance during peak season.

be fully booked → đã đặt hết chỗ
- The hotel is fully booked during the holiday season.
- The popular restaurant was fully booked every weekend for months.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- book a room, book a table, book in advance

### 2. reserve *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** đặt trước

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| reserve | reserved | reserved |

**Lưu ý:**
- Regular — thêm -d (vì tận cùng bằng -e)
- Formal hơn "book" một chút; dùng cho quyền, chỗ ngồi đặc biệt

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

reserve + noun + for → đặt trước cái gì cho ai
- Please reserve a seat for me at the conference.
- She reserved a parking space for the visiting clients.

reserve judgment → chưa vội phán xét
- They reserved the best table for the VIP guests.
- It is wise to reserve judgment until you have heard all sides of the story.

be reserved for → được dành cho
- The company will reserve the right to change the terms.
- The front row seats are reserved for special guests.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- reserve a seat, reserve the right, reserve judgment
