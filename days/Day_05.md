### 68. build *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** xây dựng

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| build | built | built |

**Lưu ý:**
- build có nghĩa rộng: xây dựng vật lý và trừu tượng (build trust, build skills)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

build + [noun] → Xây dựng cái gì
- They are building a new sports complex near the university campus.
- She built her career from scratch after moving to a new country.

build + on → Xây dựng dựa trên
- This partnership will build a strong foundation for long-term cooperation.
- We should build on the success we achieved last quarter.

build + up → Xây dựng, tích lũy dần
- She built up her savings over ten years through careful budgeting.
- He worked hard to build up his confidence before the presentation.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- build a house, build confidence, build relationships, build trust, build from scratch

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- build up: tích lũy, xây dựng dần → *She built up her savings over ten years.*
- build on: phát triển dựa trên → *We should build on our existing success.*

### 69. stay *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** ở lại, duy trì trạng thái

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| stay | stayed | stayed |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -ed (regular)
- stay + [adjective] (duy trì trạng thái)
- stay + in/at + place (ở lại đâu)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

stay + in/at + place → Ở lại nơi nào
- They stayed at a beachside resort during their anniversary trip.
- I will stay late tonight to finish the proposal before the deadline.

stay + [adjective] → Duy trì trạng thái nào (stay calm, stay healthy)
- She always stays calm and focused during high-pressure situations.
- He tries to stay healthy by exercising and eating well.

stay + to + V → Ở lại để làm gì
- She stayed after class to ask the teacher some questions.
- He stayed to help clean up after the event.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- stay calm, stay focused, stay in touch, stay overnight, stay away from

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- stay up: thức khuya → *She stayed up all night studying.*
- stay out: ở ngoài, không về → *He stayed out until midnight.*
- stay away from: tránh xa → *Stay away from junk food.*

### 70. fall *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** rơi, ngã, giảm xuống

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| fall | fell | fallen |

**Lưu ý:**
- cả ba dạng đều khác nhau
- fall có nhiều nghĩa: rơi, ngã, giảm (prices fall), bước vào (fall asleep)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

fall + down → Ngã xuống
- She fell asleep on the bus and missed her stop.
- The old tree fell down during the storm last night.

fall + in/into → Rơi vào
- Stock prices will fall if the company announces poor quarterly results.
- He fell into a deep depression after losing his job.

fall + [adjective] → Trở nên (fall asleep, fall ill)
- The temperature falls sharply in the mountains after sunset.
- She fell ill just before her important presentation.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- fall asleep, fall in love, fall behind, fall short of, fall into place

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- fall behind: tụt hậu → *Don't fall behind on your studies.*
- fall out: cãi nhau, rụng → *They fell out over money.*
- fall through: thất bại → *The deal fell through at the last minute.*

### 71. cut *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** cắt, giảm

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| cut | cut | cut |

**Lưu ý:**
- V1/V2/V3 đều là "cut"
- cut both ways = có hai mặt
- cut corners = làm cẩu thả

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

cut + [noun] → Cắt cái gì
- The chef cuts vegetables with impressive speed and precision.
- She cut the paper into small pieces for the craft project.

cut + [costs/time] → Giảm chi phí/thời gian
- The budget was cut by 20% due to lower than expected revenue.
- We found a way to cut the processing time in half.

cut + into → Cắt vào, xâm phạm
- We must cut production costs to remain competitive in the market.
- The extra meetings cut into everyone's productive work time.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- cut costs, cut time, cut a deal, cut in half, cut corners

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- cut down on: giảm bớt → *Try to cut down on sugar intake.*
- cut off: cắt đứt, ngắt → *The power was cut off during the storm.*
- cut out: loại bỏ, cắt ra → *Cut out processed food from your diet.*

|---|---|---|

**Lưu ý:**

- The two parties finally reached an agreement after weeks of negotiation.
- She reached her sales target three months ahead of schedule.

reach + out to → Liên lạc với, tiếp cận
- Our marketing campaign will reach over one million potential customers.
- Feel free to reach out to us if you have any questions.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- reach a goal, reach an agreement, reach out, reach the top, reach one's potential

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- reach out to: liên lạc, tiếp cận → *Feel free to reach out to us at any time.*

**Lưu ý:**

### 75. suggest *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** gợi ý, đề xuất

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| suggest | suggested | suggested |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -ed (regular)
- suggest + V-ing (không dùng to V)
- suggest + (that) + S + (should) + V

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

suggest + V-ing → Gợi ý làm gì
- I suggest taking a short break to refresh your mind before continuing.
- She suggested meeting at the café near the office instead.

suggest + (that) + clause → Đề xuất rằng...
- The consultant suggested that the team restructure its workflow for better efficiency.
- He suggested that we reconsider the pricing strategy.

suggest + [noun] → Gợi ý cái gì
- The test results suggest that the new formula is significantly more effective.
- Can you suggest a good restaurant near the conference center?

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- suggest a solution, suggest an idea, strongly suggest, suggest otherwise, suggest changes

### 78. sell *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** bán

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| sell | sold | sold |

**Lưu ý:**
- sell + [noun] + to + sb hoặc sell + sb + sth
- sell out = bán hết hàng

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

sell + [noun] → Bán cái gì
- The street vendor sells fresh fruit juice near the entrance every day.
- He sells handmade crafts at the weekend market.

sell + sth + for + [price] → Bán cái gì với giá bao nhiêu
- She sold her old car and bought a new electric vehicle.
- He sold his paintings for a surprisingly high price at the auction.

sell + to + sb → Bán cho ai
- The limited edition sneakers will sell out within minutes of their release.
- They sold the property to a developer for a large sum.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- sell online, sell at a discount, sell well, sell out, sell for a profit

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- sell out: bán hết, phản bội → *The concert sold out in two hours.*
- sell off: bán tháo → *The company sold off its assets.*

### 82. pull *(verb — regular)*

### 84. receive *(verb — regular)*
