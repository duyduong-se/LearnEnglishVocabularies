### 101. join *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** tham gia, nối lại

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| join | joined | joined |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -ed (regular)
- join + [group/organization] (tham gia)
- join + sb (đi cùng ai)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

join + [organization/group] → Gia nhập tổ chức/nhóm
- She joined the company as a junior developer three years ago.
- He joined the running club to stay fit and meet new people.

join + sb → Đến cùng ai
- I hope you will join us for dinner after the presentation.
- Would you like to join me for a coffee before the meeting starts?

join + in → Tham gia vào hoạt động
- Over 200 participants joined the online seminar from different countries.
- Everyone is welcome to join in the discussion.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- join a club, join forces, join in, join the team, join hands

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- join in: tham gia vào → *Everyone is welcome to join in the discussion.*
- join up: tham gia (quân đội), kết hợp → *The two groups joined up to fight for the cause.*

### 102. create *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** tạo, sáng tạo

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| create | created | created |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -d (regular)
- create mang ý nghĩa làm ra cái gì mới, sáng tạo hơn "make"

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

create + [noun] → Tạo ra cái gì
- She creates engaging social media content for several well-known brands.
- He created a detailed budget plan for the new project.

create + opportunities/problems → Tạo ra cơ hội/vấn đề
- The new transportation system created thousands of jobs in the region.
- Lack of communication can create unnecessary misunderstandings.

be created + by → Được tạo ra bởi
- Our design team will create a fresh visual identity for the brand relaunch.
- This masterpiece was created by one of the country's most celebrated artists.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- create a plan, create opportunities, create value, create content, create awareness

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- create around: xây dựng xung quanh → *The strategy was created around customer needs.*

### 103. stop *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** dừng, ngăn lại

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| stop | stopped | stopped |

**Lưu ý:**
- gấp đôi p trước -ed (regular)
- stop + V-ing (dừng không làm nữa) vs. stop + to V (dừng lại để làm gì)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

stop + V-ing → Dừng việc đang làm
- She stopped checking her phone during meetings to improve focus.
- He finally stopped smoking after years of trying.

stop + to + V → Dừng lại để làm gì khác
- He stopped at the coffee shop to get a drink before heading to work.
- She stopped to read the notice on the bulletin board.

stop + sb + from + V-ing → Ngăn ai làm gì
- The new regulation will stop companies from dumping waste into the river.
- Nothing can stop her from achieving her goals.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- stop suddenly, stop completely, stop working, stop for a break, stop and think

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- stop by: ghé qua → *I'll stop by your office this afternoon.*
- stop over: dừng chân (chuyến bay quá cảnh) → *We stopped over in Dubai for 6 hours.*

### 104. follow *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** theo dõi, theo, tuân theo

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| follow | followed | followed |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -ed (regular)
- follow + [noun] (theo sau)
- follow + instructions (tuân theo)
- follow up (theo dõi tiếp)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

follow + sb/sth → Theo ai/cái gì
- He followed his mentor's advice and applied for the scholarship.
- She followed the recipe carefully to make sure the dish turned out right.

follow + [instructions/rules] → Tuân theo hướng dẫn/quy tắc

- All staff must follow the company's code of conduct.

follow + sb on [social media] → Theo dõi ai trên mạng xã hội
- I will follow up with you next week to see if the issue has been resolved.
- She follows several industry experts on LinkedIn to stay updated.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- follow instructions, follow the rules, follow a trend, follow closely, follow through

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- follow up: theo dõi tiếp → *I'll follow up with an email after the meeting.*
- follow through: hoàn thành những gì đã bắt đầu → *Always follow through on your commitments.*

### 105. help *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** giúp đỡ

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| help | helped | helped |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -ed (regular)
- help + sb + V (bare infinitive hoặc to V đều đúng)
- can't help + V-ing = không thể không làm

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

help + sb + V/to V → Giúp ai làm gì
- Volunteers help elderly residents with their daily chores every week.
- The tutorial helped him understand the complicated programming concept.

help + [noun] → Hỗ trợ/giúp đỡ
- Our support team will help you set up the software on your device.
- Her advice helped the whole team stay on track.

can't help + V-ing → Không thể không làm gì
- I can't help feeling nervous before a big presentation.
- She can't help laughing whenever she sees that video.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- help out, help someone in need, help with, help one another, be of help

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- help out: giúp đỡ trong lúc khó khăn → *She helped out at the shelter on weekends.*

### 106. turn *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** quay, xoay, trở thành

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| turn | turned | turned |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -ed (regular)
- turn + [adjective] (trở thành)
- turn cũng là danh từ (it's your turn)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

turn + [direction] → Quay về hướng nào
- The traffic light turns green every ninety seconds at this intersection.
- Turn left at the corner and the office is on your right.

turn + [adjective] → Trở thành (turn red, turn cold)
- The weather will turn cold again next week, so bring a jacket.
- The discussion turned heated as both sides held firm positions.

turn + [noun] + into → Biến cái gì thành
- She turned her passion for photography into a successful business.
- He managed to turn a difficult situation into a learning opportunity.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- turn left/right, turn red, turn a profit, take a turn, turn around

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- turn on/off: bật/tắt → *Turn on the projector before the meeting.*
- turn up: xuất hiện, vặn to hơn → *She turned up late to the conference.*
- turn down: từ chối, vặn nhỏ hơn → *He turned down the job offer.*
- turn out: hóa ra → *The event turned out to be a great success.*

### 107. start *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** bắt đầu

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| start | started | started |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -ed (regular)
- start + to V hoặc start + V-ing
- start a business = khởi nghiệp

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

start + to + V → Bắt đầu làm gì
- I will start working on the new module as soon as the design is approved.

start + [noun] → Bắt đầu cái gì

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- start over: bắt đầu lại từ đầu → *After the failure, he decided to start over.*
- start out: bắt đầu (sự nghiệp) → *She started out as a junior intern.*

**Lưu ý:**

### 110. work *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** làm việc, vận hành, có tác dụng

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| work | worked | worked |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -ed (regular)
- work có ba nghĩa chính: làm việc (work hard), vận hành (the machine works), có tác dụng (the plan worked)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

work + [at/in + place] → Làm việc ở đâu
- She works as a data analyst for a major financial institution.
- He works in a creative agency downtown.

work + [on + project] → Làm việc trên dự án nào
- The new medication worked better than expected in the clinical trials.
- They are working on a new product that will launch next quarter.

work + out → Luyện tập, giải quyết được
- Our remote teams will work together using the new collaboration platform.
- Everything will work out fine if we stay focused and committed.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- work hard, work together, work efficiently, work on a project, work from home

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- work out: tập thể dục, giải quyết → *Everything will work out in the end.*
- work on: làm việc trên → *We're working on a new product launch.*
- work up to: dần dần đạt đến → *He worked his way up to director.*

### 112. become *(verb — irregular)*

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| become | became | become |

**Lưu ý:**
- V3 cùng dạng V1
- become + [noun] (trở thành) hoặc become + [adj] (trở nên)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

become + [noun] → Trở thành

- He wants to become a certified project manager within two years.

become + known as → Được biết đến là
- Remote work will become the standard practice for many industries in the future.
- She became known as one of the most innovative designers in the industry.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- become famous, become popular, become apparent, become involved, become aware

### 114. increase *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** tăng, tăng lên

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| increase | increased | increased |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -d (regular)
- increase là cả nội động từ (sales increased) và ngoại động từ (increase prices)
- /ɪnˈkriːs/ (verb) vs /ˈɪnkriːs/ (noun)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

increase + [noun] → Tăng cái gì
- Regular exercise increases both physical and mental well-being.
- The company increased its marketing budget by 15%.

increase + by + [percentage] → Tăng lên bao nhiêu %
- The company increased its revenue by 35% in the third quarter.
- Customer satisfaction scores increased by 12% after the new service was introduced.

increase + from...to → Tăng từ...đến
- Investing in employee training will increase overall productivity in the long term.
- The number of users increased from 50,000 to over 200,000 within a year.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- increase significantly, increase rapidly, increase productivity, increase awareness, increase sales

### 117. apply *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** áp dụng, nộp đơn

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| apply | applied | applied |

**Lưu ý:**
- y → ied (regular)
- apply + for (nộp đơn xin)
- apply + to (áp dụng cho)
- apply + [noun] (sử dụng/áp dụng)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

apply + to + [rule/principle] → Áp dụng cho
- The discount will apply to all orders placed before the end of the month.
- The same rules apply to all employees regardless of their position.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- apply for a job, apply a rule, apply pressure, apply knowledge, apply in practice
