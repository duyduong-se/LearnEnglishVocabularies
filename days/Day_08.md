### 118. fill *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** lấp đầy, điền vào

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| fill | filled | filled |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -ed (regular)
- fill + [noun] + with
- fill in/out (điền vào mẫu)
- fill up (đổ đầy)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

fill + [container] + with → Đổ đầy cái gì với
- She filled the room with laughter and positive energy throughout the session.
- He filled the glass with cold water from the dispenser.

fill + in/out + [form] → Điền vào mẫu
- Please fill out the registration form before attending the event.
- She filled in all the required fields on the application.

fill + [position/vacancy] → Điền vào vị trí còn trống
- The company will fill the senior manager vacancy by the end of next month.
- They are looking for the right person to fill the role.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- fill out a form, fill in details, fill with excitement, fill a need, fill a position

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- fill in: điền vào, thay thế tạm thời → *Can you fill in for me tomorrow?*
- fill out: điền vào mẫu → *Please fill out this application form.*
- fill up: đổ đầy, ăn no → *Fill up the tank before the long trip.*

### 119. save *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** cứu, tiết kiệm, lưu

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| save | saved | saved |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -d (regular)
- save có ba nghĩa chính: cứu (save a life), tiết kiệm (save money), lưu (save a file)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

save + sb/sth + from → Cứu ai/cái gì khỏi
- The paramedic saved three lives during the highway accident last night.
- She saved her colleague from making a costly mistake.

save + [money/time] → Tiết kiệm tiền/thời gian
- Using public transport saves both money and the environment.
- This new process will save the team approximately ten hours per week.

save + [file/document] → Lưu tập tin
- Remember to save your work every few minutes to avoid losing data.
- She saved the final version of the report before closing the program.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- save money, save time, save energy, save a life, save face

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- save up: tiết kiệm dần dần → *She saved up for years to buy a house.*
- save on: tiết kiệm trong → *We can save on costs by switching suppliers.*

### 122. affect *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** ảnh hưởng

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| affect | affected | affected |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -ed (regular)
- "affect" (động từ) khác "effect" (danh từ)
- affect + [noun] (ảnh hưởng đến cái gì)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

affect + [noun/sb] → Ảnh hưởng đến cái gì/ai
- Poor sleep directly affects concentration and decision-making abilities.
- The economic crisis affected millions of businesses around the world.

be affected + by → Bị ảnh hưởng bởi
- The new pricing strategy will affect how customers perceive the brand's value.
- Many coastal communities were severely affected by the flooding.

adversely affect → Ảnh hưởng tiêu cực
- Noise pollution adversely affects the quality of life in urban areas.
- Stress can adversely affect both physical and mental health.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- affect performance, affect health, negatively affect, directly affect, deeply affected

### 125. occur *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** xảy ra

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| occur | occurred | occurred |

**Lưu ý:**
- gấp đôi r trước -ed (regular)
- occur (formal) = happen
- occur to sb = nảy ra trong đầu ai

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

[something] + occur(s) → Điều gì xảy ra
- Earthquakes occur most frequently along the tectonic plate boundaries.
- Technical problems will occur less frequently once the update is installed.

occur + in/at → Xảy ra ở đâu/lúc nào
- The accident occurred at the intersection during rush hour.
- Most data breaches occur when employees click on suspicious links.

occur + to + sb → Nảy ra trong đầu ai
- A brilliant idea suddenly occurred to her while she was in the shower.
- It never occurred to him that the plan might fail.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- occur naturally, occur frequently, occur to someone, errors occur, occur simultaneously
