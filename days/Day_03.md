### 35. hold *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** cầm, giữ, tổ chức

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| hold | held | held |

**Lưu ý:**
- hold có nghĩa mở rộng: tổ chức (hold a meeting), chứa đựng (hold water), giữ vững

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

hold + [noun] → Cầm/giữ cái gì
- She held the door open for the elderly man.
- He held the rope tightly to keep from falling.

hold + a meeting/event → Tổ chức cuộc họp/sự kiện
- The company held its annual conference in Singapore last year.
- We will hold an orientation session for all new employees next week.

hold + sb + [adj] → Giữ ai trong trạng thái nào
- The police held him responsible for the accident.
- She held the children still while the doctor examined them.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- hold a meeting, hold hands, hold one's breath, hold a position, hold steady

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- hold on: chờ đợi, bám chắc → *Hold on, I'll be right back.*
- hold back: kiềm chế, cản trở → *Don't hold back your true feelings.*
- hold up: trì hoãn, giơ lên → *Traffic held us up for an hour.*

### 36. bring *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** mang lại, đem đến

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| bring | brought | brought |

**Lưu ý:**
- bring (mang đến chỗ người nói) khác "take" (mang đi chỗ khác)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

bring + [noun] → Mang cái gì đến
- Please bring your ID and supporting documents to the interview.
- Could you bring some snacks for the team meeting?

bring + sb + sth → Mang cái gì cho ai
- She brought fresh flowers to the hospital to cheer up her friend.
- He brought his colleagues coffee every morning.

bring + [noun] + to + place → Mang đến nơi nào
- The new technology will bring significant improvements to our workflow.
- This song brings back good memories of my childhood.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- bring joy, bring results, bring change, bring to attention, bring to life

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- bring up: nhắc đến, nuôi dưỡng → *She brought up an important point.*
- bring about: gây ra → *The new policy brought about positive change.*
- bring back: mang lại, gợi nhớ → *This song brings back good memories.*

### 37. happen *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** xảy ra

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| happen | happened | happened |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -ed (regular)
- happen thường không có tân ngữ trực tiếp
- "happen to" = tình cờ

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

[something] + happen(s) → Điều gì xảy ra
- Accidents happen when people are not paying attention.
- Something unexpected happened during the final exam.

happen + to + sb → Xảy ra với ai
- What happened to him after he left the company?
- I hope nothing bad happens to them on the trip.

happen + to + V → Tình cờ làm gì
- He happened to find the answer while reading an old report.
- I happened to run into her at the supermarket yesterday.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- happen suddenly, happen often, happen by chance, happen again, happen naturally

### 38. write *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** viết

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| write | wrote | written |

**Lưu ý:**
- cả ba dạng đều khác nhau
- write to sb (viết thư cho ai)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

write + [noun] → Viết cái gì (essay, email, report)
- She writes in her journal every evening before going to bed.
- He wrote an excellent research paper on climate change.

write + to + sb → Viết thư cho ai
- I will write a detailed proposal and send it to you by Monday.
- She wrote to the company to complain about the service.

write + [noun] + for → Viết cái gì cho ai/mục đích gì
- He writes articles for several international magazines.
- She wrote the script for the school play.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- write an essay, write a report, write down, write back, write clearly

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- write down: ghi chép lại → *Write down the key points.*
- write off: xóa nợ, xóa sổ → *The debt was written off after ten years.*

### 39. provide *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** cung cấp

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| provide | provided | provided |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -d (regular)
- provide sth for sb hoặc provide sb with sth (hai cấu trúc đều đúng)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

provide + [noun] + for + sb → Cung cấp cái gì cho ai
- The government provides free healthcare for all citizens.
- The school provides textbooks for students who cannot afford them.

provide + sb + with + [noun] → Cung cấp cho ai cái gì
- The company provided employees with all necessary equipment for remote work.
- The trainer provided participants with detailed handouts.

provide + [noun] → Cung cấp dịch vụ/hàng hóa
- This new platform will provide users with real-time data and analytics.
- The organization provides support to families in need.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- provide support, provide information, provide services, provide funding, provide evidence

### 40. sit *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** ngồi

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| sit | sat | sat |

**Lưu ý:**
- "sit down" (ngồi xuống) là cụm hay dùng
- sit for an exam = dự thi

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

sit + down → Ngồi xuống
- Please sit down and wait — the doctor will see you shortly.
- She sat down and began reading through the contract.

sit + in/on + [place] → Ngồi ở đâu
- The students sit in alphabetical order during official examinations.
- We sat around the conference table and discussed the quarterly results.

sit + for + [exam] → Dự kỳ thi
- He sat for the professional certification exam last spring.
- Many students sit for the entrance exam in June.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- sit down, sit still, sit comfortably, sit next to, sit for an exam

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- sit back: thư giãn, không hành động → *He just sat back and watched.*
- sit in on: tham dự (không chính thức) → *She sat in on the board meeting.*

### 41. stand *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** đứng, chịu đựng, đại diện cho

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| stand | stood | stood |

**Lưu ý:**
- stand có nghĩa mở rộng: chịu đựng (can't stand), đại diện (stand for), có giá trị (offer stands)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

stand + [adverb/preposition] → Đứng ở đâu/như thế nào
- Everyone stands up when the national anthem plays.
- She stood at the front of the room and delivered her presentation confidently.

stand + for → Đại diện cho, ủng hộ
- Our organization will stand for equal opportunities for all employees.
- The abbreviation "CEO" stands for Chief Executive Officer.

can't stand + [noun/V-ing] → Không chịu được
- I can't stand the noise from the construction site outside.
- She can't stand being late for appointments.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- stand firm, stand alone, stand a chance, stand for justice, stand out

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- stand out: nổi bật → *Her work always stands out from the rest.*
- stand up for: bảo vệ, đứng về phía → *Always stand up for what is right.*
- stand by: ủng hộ, chờ sẵn → *I'll stand by you no matter what.*

### 42. lose *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** mất, thua

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| lose | lost | lost |

**Lưu ý:**
- lose /luːz/ (động từ) không nhầm với "loose" /luːs/ (tính từ: lỏng lẻo)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

lose + [noun] → Mất cái gì
- I often lose track of time when I'm reading an interesting book.
- She lost her keys and had to call a locksmith.

lose + to + sb → Thua ai
- The team lost the championship match by just one point.
- They lost to a stronger opponent in the semifinal.

lose + one's + [noun] → Mất đi điều gì (lose one's temper)
- If we don't act quickly, we will lose the competitive advantage we have now.
- He tends to lose his temper when things don't go as planned.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- lose weight, lose time, lose focus, lose confidence, lose track of

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- lose out on: bỏ lỡ cơ hội → *Don't lose out on this great opportunity.*

### 43. pay *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** trả tiền, thanh toán

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| pay | paid | paid |

**Lưu ý:**
- pay attention là cụm cố định quan trọng
- pay back = trả lại

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

pay + [amount] + for + [noun] → Trả tiền cho cái gì
- She paid $500 for the new laptop on sale.
- How much did you pay for that course?

pay + sb + [amount] → Trả cho ai bao nhiêu
- The company will pay employees a year-end bonus next December.
- They pay their staff a competitive salary.

pay + attention/respect → Cụm cố định
- Students who pay attention in class tend to perform better on exams.
- Please pay attention to the instructions before starting.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- pay attention, pay a bill, pay a visit, pay in cash, pay off a debt

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- pay back: hoàn trả → *I'll pay you back tomorrow.*
- pay off: có kết quả tốt, trả hết nợ → *Hard work always pays off in the end.*

### 44. meet *(verb — irregular)*

| meet | met | met |

**Lưu ý:**

### 45. include *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** bao gồm

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| include | included | included |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -d (regular)
- "include" không dùng trong tiếp diễn vì là stative verb

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

include + [noun] → Bao gồm cái gì
- The price includes breakfast, lunch, and free airport transfer.
- The package includes free shipping and a one-year warranty.

be included + in → Được bao gồm trong
- The report included detailed statistics and case studies from three countries.
- Service charges are already included in the total bill.

including + [noun] → Bao gồm (dùng như giới từ)
- Several team members, including the project lead, raised concerns.
- Everyone, including the manager, was required to attend.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- include everyone, include details, include a fee, widely included, not included

### 46. continue *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** tiếp tục

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| continue | continued | continued |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -d (regular)
- continue + to V hoặc continue + V-ing đều đúng

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

continue + to + V → Tiếp tục làm gì
- She continues to improve her English skills by reading every day.
- He continued to work even after the power went out.

continue + V-ing → Tiếp tục làm gì (cùng nghĩa)
- The negotiations continued for three days before an agreement was reached.
- They continued discussing the plan despite the disagreements.

continue + [noun] → Tiếp tục cái gì
- The team will continue its research into renewable energy sources next year.
- She continued her studies abroad after graduating locally.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- continue to grow, continue efforts, continue working, continue as planned, continue despite

### 47. set *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** đặt, thiết lập, lặn (mặt trời)

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| set | set | set |

**Lưu ý:**
- V1/V2/V3 đều là "set"
- set có nhiều nghĩa: đặt, thiết lập, chỉnh, lặn

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

set + [noun] → Đặt/thiết lập cái gì
- She always sets clear goals for herself at the beginning of each year.
- He set his alarm for 6 a.m. to avoid being late.

set + [noun] + on/in → Đặt cái gì ở đâu
- The athlete set a new world record at the Olympic Games.
- Please set the documents on the desk before you leave.

set + [goal/record] → Thiết lập mục tiêu/kỷ lục
- We will set up a joint venture with the overseas partner next quarter.
- The manager set a high standard for the entire team to follow.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- set a goal, set a record, set a deadline, set an example, set the stage

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- set up: thiết lập, thành lập → *They set up a new company last year.*
- set out: bắt đầu hành trình, trình bày → *She set out early in the morning.*
- set aside: để dành → *Set aside some money for emergencies.*

### 48. learn *(verb — regular)*

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| learn | learned | learned |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -ed (regular)

learn + [noun/language] → Học cái gì

- learn a language, learn a skill, learn from experience, learn by heart, learn the hard way

### 49. change *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** thay đổi

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| change | changed | changed |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -d (regular)
- change cũng là danh từ (a change)
- change into = biến thành

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

change + [noun] → Thay đổi cái gì
- The weather can change very quickly in mountainous regions.
- She changed her mind about taking the job offer.

change + from...to → Thay đổi từ...sang
- She changed her major from engineering to business management.
- The company changed its name from TechCo to Innovate Inc.

change + into → Biến thành
- The new leadership will change the company's direction significantly.
- The caterpillar eventually changes into a butterfly.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- change one's mind, change plans, change direction, climate change, change dramatically

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- change over: chuyển sang → *The system changed over to digital format.*
- change into: mặc thay đồ, biến thành → *She changed into her work clothes.*

### 50. lead *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** dẫn dắt, lãnh đạo, dẫn đến

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| lead | led | led |

**Lưu ý:**
- lead /liːd/ (động từ), led /lɛd/ (V2/V3)
- không nhầm với "lead" /lɛd/ (danh từ: chì)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

lead + [noun] → Dẫn dắt, chỉ huy ai/cái gì
- She leads a team of twenty engineers at the research institute.
- He has led the department for the past five years.

lead + to + [noun] → Dẫn đến kết quả gì
- Poor communication led to many misunderstandings in the project.
- Regular exercise can lead to significant improvements in health.

lead + sb + to + [place] → Dẫn ai đến đâu
- The new manager will lead the department through its most ambitious expansion phase.
- The guide led the tourists through the ancient ruins.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- lead a team, lead the way, lead to success, lead by example, take the lead

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- lead up to: dẫn đến (sự kiện) → *The events leading up to the crisis.*
