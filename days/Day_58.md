## Từ học hôm nay

### 1. anxiety *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** lo âu (U); mối lo cụ thể (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** anxiety (U); an anxiety / anxieties (C)

**Lưu ý:**
- Phân biệt: anxiety (lo âu — thường kéo dài, có thể là bệnh) vs. worry (lo lắng — thường về một việc cụ thể)
- "social anxiety" = lo âu xã hội (sợ giao tiếp)
- "anxiety disorder" = rối loạn lo âu (chẩn đoán y tế)

**Ví dụ:**
- She experienced severe anxiety before every public speaking event.
- His anxieties about the exam kept him awake most of the night.
- Regular exercise has been shown to significantly reduce anxiety levels.
- There is growing anxiety among parents about the amount of time children spend online.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- social anxiety, anxiety attack, anxiety disorder
- feel anxiety, overcome anxiety, anxiety about sth
- cause anxiety, anxiety levels, deep-seated anxiety

### 2. motivation *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** động lực (U); lý do thúc đẩy (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** motivation (U); a motivation / motivations (C)

**Lưu ý:**
- Phân biệt: motivation (động lực, lý do thúc đẩy) vs. motive (động cơ — thường trong pháp lý, thường tiêu cực: "motive for murder")
- "motivational speaker" = diễn giả truyền cảm hứng
- "intrinsic motivation" = động lực nội tại (làm vì bản thân thích)

**Ví dụ:**
- Her main motivation for studying abroad was to become fluent in three languages.
- The manager struggled to maintain team motivation during the long project.
- The police questioned the suspect's motivations for being at the scene.
- Without clear goals, it's easy to lose motivation and drift without direction.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- inner motivation, lack of motivation, find motivation
- motivation to do sth, the motivation behind
- self-motivation, motivational speaker

### 3. attitude *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** thái độ; quan điểm

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** an attitude / attitudes (C); attitude (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "have attitude" (informal, không mạo từ) = tỏ ra kiêu ngạo, thách thức: "Don't give me attitude!"
- "attitude to/towards" = thái độ đối với: "attitudes towards mental health"
- "attitude problem" = vấn đề thái độ (thường tiêu cực)

**Ví dụ:**
- Her positive attitude in the face of adversity inspired everyone around her.
- Society's attitude towards mental health has changed significantly in recent years.
- He was warned several times about his attitude problem before being dismissed.
- Your attitude towards challenges will determine whether you succeed or give up.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- positive / negative attitude, attitude to life/work
- change your attitude, attitude problem
- have attitude (= kiêu ngạo — informal)

### 4. personality *(noun — both C & U)*

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a personality / personalities (C); personality (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "a TV personality" / "a media personality" = người nổi tiếng trên truyền hình/truyền thông

**Ví dụ:**

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- strong personality, TV personality, personality clash
- have a great personality, personality disorder
- split personality

### 5. behaviour *(noun — uncountable)*
**Nghĩa:** hành vi; cách ứng xử

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** behaviour (BrE) / behavior (AmE) — không có số nhiều

**Lưu ý:**
- BrE: "behaviour"; AmE: "behavior" — cả hai đều đúng
- "on best behaviour" = cố gắng cư xử thật tốt: "I was on my best behaviour."

**Ví dụ:**
- The psychologist studied how childhood experiences affect adult behaviour.
- The children were on their best behaviour during the visit from the school inspector.
- Antisocial behaviour in public spaces has become an increasing concern for local authorities.
- Good behaviour should be rewarded, not just bad behaviour punished.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- good / bad / antisocial behaviour, behaviour pattern
- behaviour change, human behaviour, criminal behaviour
- reward / punish behaviour, model behaviour

### 6. habit *(noun — countable)*

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a habit / habits

**Lưu ý:**
- "kick a habit" = từ bỏ thói quen xấu: "He's trying to kick the smoking habit."

- "Old habits die hard." = Thói quen xấu khó từ bỏ (tục ngữ)

**Ví dụ:**
- She developed the habit of reading for thirty minutes every evening before bed.
- It took him months to kick the habit of checking his phone first thing in the morning.
- He's a creature of habit — he orders the same coffee at the same café every single day.
- Regular exercise is a healthy habit that takes time to build but is worth the effort.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- bad habit, good habit, develop / break a habit
- by habit, out of habit, creature of habit
- kick a habit, habit-forming

### 7. belief *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** niềm tin (U); một niềm tin cụ thể (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a belief / beliefs (C); belief (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "beyond belief" = không thể tin được, khó tin: "His rudeness was beyond belief."
- "in the belief that" = tin rằng, cho rằng: "She acted in the belief that she was helping."
- Phân biệt: belief (niềm tin — trừu tượng) vs. faith (đức tin — thường tôn giáo hoặc rất mạnh mẽ)

**Ví dụ:**
- Her strong belief in education led her to fund scholarships for disadvantaged students.
- The level of waste produced by the event was, quite honestly, beyond belief.
- She gave him money in the belief that he would pay it back within a week.
- Different people hold different beliefs about what happens after we die.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- core belief, religious belief, belief in sth/sb
- beyond belief, in the belief that
- firm / strong belief, shake sb's belief

### 8. principle *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** nguyên tắc (C); nguyên lý (C/U)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a principle / principles (C); principle (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "in principle" = về nguyên tắc (nhưng có thể có vấn đề thực tế): "In principle, I agree."
- "on principle" = vì lý do nguyên tắc đạo đức: "She refused on principle."
- Chú ý đánh vần: "principle" (nguyên tắc) vs. "principal" (hiệu trưởng / chính yếu)

**Ví dụ:**
- She refused to lie, even under pressure — it was against her principles.
- In principle, the idea sounds great, but the costs may make it impossible to implement.
- He is a man of principle who never compromises his values for personal gain.
- The scientific principles underlying the experiment are well established.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- in principle, on principle, core principles
- stand by your principles, against sb's principles
- a man/woman of principle

### 9. meaning *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** ý nghĩa (U/C); nghĩa của từ (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a meaning / meanings (C); meaning (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- Phân biệt: meaning (ý nghĩa của từ/hành động) vs. purpose (mục đích — điều mình sống vì) vs. significance (tầm quan trọng)
- "meaningful" (adj) = có ý nghĩa, sâu sắc
- "meaningless" (adj) = vô nghĩa

**Ví dụ:**
- Many people spend their lives searching for the meaning of life.
- The word "set" has over sixty different meanings in the English language.
- Volunteering gave her life a sense of meaning and purpose she had been missing.
- A look can carry more meaning than words in some situations.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- the meaning of life, find meaning (in), full of meaning
- literal meaning, figurative meaning, hidden meaning
- a meaningful relationship

### 17. courage *(noun — uncountable)*

### 20. honesty *(noun — uncountable)*

- in all honesty, brutal honesty
- honesty is the best policy, show / value honesty

### 23. compassion *(noun — uncountable)*
**Nghĩa:** lòng trắc ẩn; sự cảm thông

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** compassion (không có số nhiều)

**Lưu ý:**
- "compassion fatigue" = mệt mỏi cảm xúc do tiếp xúc quá nhiều với đau khổ (hay gặp ở y tá, nhân viên xã hội)
- Phân biệt: compassion (muốn giúp đỡ người đang đau khổ) vs. empathy (hiểu cảm xúc của người khác) vs. sympathy (thương cảm từ xa)
- "compassionate care" = chăm sóc đầy lòng trắc ẩn

**Ví dụ:**
- She showed great compassion for the victims and worked tirelessly to help them.
- Doctors and nurses can suffer from compassion fatigue if they don't take care of themselves.
- Treating all people with compassion, regardless of their background, is a fundamental human value.
- He listened with compassion as she described what she had been through.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- show / feel compassion, compassion for others
- compassion fatigue, deep compassion, act of compassion
- with compassion, compassionate care
