# Day 01 — Verbs: Basic Actions (Part 1)
🎯 Hôm nay: 17 từ (từ số 1 đến 17) | ⏱ Ước tính: 40–50 phút

### 1. be *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** là, thì, ở

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| am / is / are | was / were | been |
| /æm/ · /ɪz/ · /ɑːr/ | /wʌz/ · /wɜːr/ | /biːn/ |

**Lưu ý:**
- Dạng rút gọn thường dùng khi nói: I'm / She's / They're / isn't / weren't

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

**[Động từ chính]**

S + am/is/are + [noun/adj] → Diễn tả trạng thái, đặc điểm
- She is a doctor.

S + was/were + [noun/adj] → Diễn tả trạng thái trong quá khứ
- They were very tired after the trip.

S + will be + [noun/adj] → Diễn tả trạng thái trong tương lai
- The results will be ready by Friday.

**[Trợ động từ]**

S + am/is/are + V-ing → Thì hiện tại tiếp diễn
- She is working right now.

S + was/were + V-ing → Thì quá khứ tiếp diễn
- I was reading when she called.

S + am/is/are/was/were + V3 → Câu bị động
- The letter was written by him.

**[Câu phủ định & câu hỏi]**

S + am/is/are + not + ... → Phủ định
- She isn't available right now.

Am/Is/Are + S + ...? → Câu hỏi Yes/No
- Are you ready?

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- be careful, be ready, be responsible, be aware, be happy

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- be up to: đang làm gì đó → *What are you up to today?*
- be over: kết thúc → *The meeting is over.*

### 2. have *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** có

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| have (has) | had | had |
| /hæv/ · /hæz/ | /hæd/ | /hæd/ |

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

**[Động từ chính]**

S + have/has + [noun] → Diễn tả sở hữu
- I have two younger brothers.

have + [noun] → Cụm cố định (have lunch, have fun, have a meeting...)
- Let's have a quick break.

**[Trợ động từ]**

S + have/has + V3 → Thì hiện tại hoàn thành
- She has already finished the report.

S + had + V3 → Thì quá khứ hoàn thành
- He had left before I arrived.

**[Câu phủ định & câu hỏi]**

S + don't/doesn't + have + ... → Phủ định
- I don't have time right now.

Do/Does + S + have + ...? → Câu hỏi Yes/No
- Do you have a minute?

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- have breakfast/lunch/dinner, have fun, have time, have a meeting, have experience

### 3. do *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** làm

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| do (does) | did | done |
| /duː/ · /dʌz/ | /dɪd/ | /dʌn/ |

**Lưu ý:**
- Dạng rút gọn phủ định: don't / doesn't / didn't

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

**[Động từ chính]**

do + [noun] → Thực hiện một hành động
- She does her homework every day.

do + one's best → Cố gắng hết sức
- I'll do my best.

**[Trợ động từ — phủ định & câu hỏi]**

S + don't/doesn't + V → Phủ định hiện tại
- I don't understand.

S + didn't + V → Phủ định quá khứ
- He didn't come yesterday.

Do/Does + S + V? → Câu hỏi Yes/No
- Do you like coffee?

What do/does + S + do? → Hỏi nghề nghiệp
- What do you do for a living?

### 4. say *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** nói

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| say (says) | said | said |
| /seɪ/ · /sɛz/ | /sɛd/ | /sɛd/ |

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

S + say + (that) + clause → Nói rằng
- He said that he would come.

S + say + to + sb + that → Nói với ai đó rằng
- She said to her manager that the deadline was too tight.

It is said that... → Người ta nói rằng
- It is said that practice makes perfect.

### 5. get *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** lấy, nhận, trở nên

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| get | got | gotten |
| /ɡɛt/ | /ɡɑːt/ | /ˈɡɑːtən/ |

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

get + [noun] → Nhận / lấy được cái gì
- I got a message from him.

get + [adjective] → Trở nên (diễn tả sự thay đổi)
- It's getting dark.

get + sb + to + V → Nhờ / khiến ai đó làm gì
- I'll get him to fix it.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- get a job, get better, get ready

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- get up: thức dậy → *I get up at 6 every morning.*
- get over: vượt qua → *She finally got over her fear of flying.*
- get back: trở lại / lấy lại → *When did you get back from the trip?*

### 6. make *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** tạo ra, làm

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| make | made | made |
| /meɪk/ | /meɪd/ | /meɪd/ |

**Lưu ý:**
- "make" nhấn mạnh kết quả tạo ra — khác "do" (hành động chung)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

make + [noun] → Tạo ra / làm ra cái gì
- He makes coffee for everyone every morning.

make + [noun cố định] → Cụm cố định thường dùng
- We need to make a decision soon.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- make a decision, make a mistake, make money, make sense

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- make up: bịa đặt / hòa giải → *She made up a story to avoid trouble.*

### 7. go *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** đi

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| go | went | gone |
| /ɡoʊ/ | /wɛnt/ | /ɡɔːn/ |

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

go + to + place → Đi đến nơi nào
- She went to the library.

go + V-ing → Đi làm hoạt động gì
- We went shopping last weekend.

go + [adj] → Trở nên (thường theo chiều xấu)
- Everything went wrong.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- go home, go shopping, go ahead

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- go through: trải qua → *She went through a difficult time.*

### 8. know *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** biết

**Chia thì:**

|---|---|---|
| know | knew | known |
| /noʊ/ | /njuː/ | /noʊn/ |

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

know + [noun] → Biết ai/cái gì

know + (that) + clause → Biết rằng

know + how/what/where + to + V → Biết cách làm gì

### 9. take *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** lấy, mang, dẫn

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| take | took | taken |
| /teɪk/ | /tʊk/ | /ˈteɪkən/ |

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

take + [noun] → Lấy / mang cái gì
- He took a photo of the sunset.

It takes + time → Mất bao lâu
- It took me two hours to finish.

take + sb + to + place → Đưa ai đến đâu
- She took her parents to the airport.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- take a break, take notes, take a photo

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- take off: cất cánh / cởi ra → *The plane took off on time.*
- take over: tiếp quản → *She took over the management position.*

### 10. see *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** nhìn thấy, gặp, hiểu

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| see | saw | seen |
| /siː/ | /sɔː/ | /siːn/ |

**Lưu ý:**
- "see" = nhìn thấy không chủ ý — khác "look" (nhìn có chủ ý)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

see + [noun] → Nhìn thấy ai/cái gì
- Did you see that?

see + sb + V/V-ing → Thấy ai đó đang làm gì
- I saw him walking down the street.

I see → Tôi hiểu rồi (spoken English)
- I see. That makes sense.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- see a doctor

|---|---|---|

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

- Can you come to the office tomorrow?

### 12. think *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** nghĩ, suy nghĩ

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| think | thought | thought |
| /θɪŋk/ | /θɔːt/ | /θɔːt/ |

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

**[Động từ chính]**

think + (that) + clause → Nghĩ rằng, cho rằng (ý kiến)
- I think this movie is really good.

think about/of + [noun/V-ing] → Suy nghĩ về điều gì
- She is thinking of getting a dog.

think + [question word] + clause → Suy nghĩ / không hiểu tại sao
- I can't think where I put my keys.

**[Câu phủ định & câu hỏi]**

I don't think + (that) + clause → Phủ định lịch sự (không dùng "I think... not")
- I don't think he likes coffee.

What do you think about/of + [noun]? → Hỏi ý kiến
- What do you think about this restaurant?

Do you think + (that) + clause? → Hỏi ý kiến Yes/No
- Do you think it will rain today?

### 13. look *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** nhìn, trông có vẻ

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| look | looked | looked |
| /lʊk/ | /lʊkt/ | /lʊkt/ |

**Lưu ý:**
- "look" = nhìn có chủ ý — khác "see" (nhìn thấy không chủ ý)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

**[Động từ chính]**

look at + [noun] → Nhìn vào cái gì (chủ ý)
- She looked at the photo and smiled.

look + [adjective] → Trông có vẻ như thế nào
- You look tired. Are you okay?

look + like + [noun/clause] → Trông giống như / có vẻ như
- It looks like it's going to rain.

**[Câu phủ định & câu hỏi]**

S + don't/doesn't + look + [adj] → Phủ định
- He doesn't look happy today.

What does + S + look + like? → Hỏi ngoại hình / trông như thế nào
- What does the new café look like?

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- look happy/tired/great, look the same, look different, look serious

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- look up: tra cứu → *Look up the word in the dictionary.*
- look after: chăm sóc → *She looks after her younger brother.*
- look forward to: mong chờ → *I'm looking forward to the weekend.*

### 14. want *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** muốn

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| want | wanted | wanted |

**Lưu ý:**
- stative verb — không dùng trong thì tiếp diễn

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

want + [noun] → Muốn cái gì
- She wants a cup of hot tea in the morning.
- The team wants more resources to complete the project.

want + to + V → Muốn làm gì
- I wanted to call you, but I lost my phone.
- He wants to apply for the position abroad.

want + sb + to + V → Muốn ai đó làm gì
- Do you want me to help you with the presentation?
- The manager wants everyone to submit their reports by Friday.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- want badly, want desperately, want more, want nothing, want to know

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- want out: muốn thoát khỏi → *He wants out of this situation.*

### 15. give *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** cho, tặng, đưa

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| give | gave | given |

**Lưu ý:**
- có thể có hai tân ngữ — give sb sth hoặc give sth to sb

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

give + sb + sth → Đưa/tặng cho ai cái gì (double object)
- She gives her students detailed feedback on every assignment.
- He gave me a very useful piece of advice.

give + sth + to + sb → Đưa/tặng cái gì cho ai
- The manager will give us more information about the new project tomorrow.
- Please give this document to the client when they arrive.

give + [noun] → Cụm cố định: give a speech, give advice
- He gave a very impressive speech at the graduation ceremony.
- She gave a short presentation about the new product.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- give advice, give a speech, give permission, give support, give feedback

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- give up: từ bỏ → *Don't give up on your dreams.*
- give away: cho đi, tiết lộ → *He gave away all his old books.*
- give back: trả lại → *Please give back the pen when you're done.*

### 16. use *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** dùng, sử dụng

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| use | used | used |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -d (regular, vì kết thúc bằng -e)
- "used to + V" diễn tả thói quen trong quá khứ (không còn nữa)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

use + [noun] → Sử dụng cái gì
- We use cloud software to collaborate on documents in real time.
- She uses a planner to keep track of her daily tasks.

use + [noun] + to + V → Dùng cái gì để làm gì
- She used her creativity to design an outstanding poster.
- He uses public transport to get to work every day.

used to + V → Thói quen/trạng thái trong quá khứ
- I used to walk to school when I was young.
- She used to work late every night before she changed jobs.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- use a tool, use technology, use energy, use language, use common sense

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- use up: dùng hết → *We used up all the paper in the printer.*

### 17. find *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** tìm thấy, nhận thấy

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| find | found | found |

**Lưu ý:**
- V2 và V3 cùng là "found"
- find cũng có nghĩa là "nhận thấy/cho rằng" (find it easy)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

find + [noun] → Tìm thấy cái gì
- The police found the missing child safe and sound.
- I found a great café near the office.

find + it + [adj] + to + V → Thấy việc gì là...
- I find it very interesting to learn about different cultures.
- She finds it difficult to concentrate in noisy environments.

find + sb/sth + [adj] → Nhận thấy ai/cái gì như thế nào
- He found the instructions confusing and asked for help.
- She found the new system very easy to use.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- find a solution, find time, find out, find it difficult, find one's way

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- find out: khám phá, tìm hiểu → *I need to find out where the meeting is.*
