# Day 01 — Verbs: Basic Actions (Part 1)
🎯 Hôm nay: 17 từ (từ số 1 đến 17) | ⏱ Ước tính: 40–50 phút

---

### 1. be *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** là, thì, ở

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| am / is / are | was / were | been |
| /æm/ · /ɪz/ · /ɑːr/ | /wɒz/ · /wɜːr/ | /biːn/ |

**Lưu ý:**
- Dạng rút gọn thường dùng khi nói: I'm / She's / They're / isn't / weren't

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

**[Động từ chính]**

S + am/is/are + [noun/adj] → Diễn tả trạng thái, đặc điểm
- She is a doctor.

S + was/were + [noun/adj] → Diễn tả trạng thái trong quá khứ
- They were very tired after the trip.

S + will be + [noun/adj] → Diễn tả trạng thái trong tương lai
- The results will be ready by Friday.

**[Trợ động từ]**

S + am/is/are + V-ing → Thì hiện tại tiếp diễn
- She is working right now.

S + was/were + V-ing → Thì quá khứ tiếp diễn
- I was reading when she called.

S + am/is/are/was/were + V3 → Câu bị động
- The letter was written by him.

**[Câu phủ định & câu hỏi]**

S + am/is/are + not + ... → Phủ định
- She isn't available right now.

Am/Is/Are + S + ...? → Câu hỏi Yes/No
- Are you ready?

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- be careful, be ready, be responsible, be aware, be happy

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- be up to: đang làm gì đó → *What are you up to today?*
- be over: kết thúc → *The meeting is over.*

### 2. have *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** có

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| have (has) | had | had |
| /hæv/ · /hæz/ | /hæd/ | /hæd/ |

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

**[Động từ chính]**

S + have/has + [noun] → Diễn tả sở hữu
- I have two younger brothers.

have + [noun] → Cụm cố định (have lunch, have fun, have a meeting...)
- Let's have a quick break.

**[Trợ động từ]**

S + have/has + V3 → Thì hiện tại hoàn thành
- She has already finished the report.

S + had + V3 → Thì quá khứ hoàn thành
- He had left before I arrived.

**[Câu phủ định & câu hỏi]**

S + don't/doesn't + have + ... → Phủ định
- I don't have time right now.

Do/Does + S + have + ...? → Câu hỏi Yes/No
- Do you have a minute?

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- have breakfast/lunch/dinner, have fun, have time, have a meeting, have experience

### 3. do *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** làm

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| do (does) | did | done |
| /duː/ · /dʌz/ | /dɪd/ | /dʌn/ |

**Lưu ý:**
- Dạng rút gọn phủ định: don't / doesn't / didn't

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

**[Động từ chính]**

do + [noun] → Thực hiện một hành động
- She does her homework every day.

do + one's best → Cố gắng hết sức
- I'll do my best.

**[Trợ động từ — phủ định & câu hỏi]**

S + don't/doesn't + V → Phủ định hiện tại
- I don't understand.

S + didn't + V → Phủ định quá khứ
- He didn't come yesterday.

Do/Does + S + V? → Câu hỏi Yes/No
- Do you like coffee?

What do/does + S + do? → Hỏi nghề nghiệp
- What do you do for a living?

### 4. say *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** nói

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| say (says) | said | said |
| /seɪ/ · /sɛz/ | /sɛd/ | /sɛd/ |

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

S + say + (that) + clause → Nói rằng
- He said that he would come.

S + say + to + sb + that → Nói với ai đó rằng
- She said to her manager that the deadline was too tight.

It is said that... → Người ta nói rằng
- It is said that practice makes perfect.

### 5. get *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** lấy, nhận, trở nên

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| get | got | gotten (AmE) / got (BrE) |

**Lưu ý:**
- rất phổ biến trong spoken English
- British English dùng "got" cho cả V3

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

get + [noun] → Lấy hoặc nhận cái gì
- He got a promotion last month.
- She finally got the job she had been waiting for.

get + [adjective] → Trở nên
- She will get better soon with proper rest.
- He got angry when he heard the news.

get + to + place → Đến nơi nào đó
- I usually get to work by bus.
- How long does it take to get to the airport?

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- get a job, get better, get ready, get angry, get permission

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- get up: thức dậy → *I get up at 6 every morning.*
- get along: hòa thuận → *They get along very well.*
- get over: vượt qua → *She finally got over her fear of flying.*

### 6. make *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** tạo ra, làm

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| make | made | made |

**Lưu ý:**
- nhấn mạnh vào kết quả tạo ra
- khác với "do" (hành động chung)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

make + [noun] → Tạo ra, làm ra cái gì
- He makes coffee for everyone in the office each morning.
- She makes handmade cards for her friends.

make + sb + [adj/verb] → Khiến ai đó cảm thấy/làm gì
- This new policy will make it easier for employees to work from home.
- The news made everyone in the room feel anxious.

make + a decision/plan → Đưa ra quyết định/kế hoạch
- We need to make a decision before the deadline.
- The team made a detailed plan for the product launch.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- make a decision, make progress, make a mistake, make money, make sense

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- make up: bịa đặt, hòa giải, trang điểm → *She made up a story to avoid trouble.*
- make out: hiểu, nhìn thấy → *I can't make out what he's saying.*

### 7. go *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** đi

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| go | went | gone |

**Lưu ý:**
- went là từ hoàn toàn khác gốc (suppletive form)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

go + to + place → Đi đến nơi nào
- They go to the gym three times a week.
- She went to the library to return the books.

go + V-ing → Đi làm hoạt động gì (go swimming, go shopping)
- We went shopping at the new mall last weekend.
- I will go hiking in the mountains this summer.

go + [adverb/adjective] → Trở nên xấu đi (go wrong, go bad)
- Everything went wrong during the presentation.
- The milk has gone bad — don't drink it.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- go home, go abroad, go shopping, go wrong, go ahead

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- go on: tiếp tục → *Please go on with your story.*
- go through: trải qua → *She went through a difficult time last year.*
- go over: xem xét lại → *Let's go over the plan one more time.*

### 8. know *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** biết

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| know | knew | known |

**Lưu ý:**
- stative verb (động từ trạng thái) — không dùng trong thì tiếp diễn

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

know + [noun/pronoun] → Biết ai/cái gì
- I know three foreign languages: English, French, and Japanese.
- Do you know the new manager in our department?

know + (that) + clause → Biết rằng...
- I know that this project will be a success.
- She knew that he was not telling the whole truth.

know + how/what/where to + V → Biết cách làm gì
- By the end of this course, you will know how to write professional emails.
- He knows exactly what to say in difficult situations.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- know the answer, know by heart, know for sure, know someone well, know the difference

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- know about: biết về → *Do you know about the new project?*

### 9. take *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** lấy, mang, dẫn

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| take | took | taken |

**Lưu ý:**
- take có nhiều nghĩa tùy ngữ cảnh: lấy, mang theo, dẫn đi, mất (thời gian)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

take + [noun] → Lấy/mang cái gì
- Please take your seat — the meeting is about to start.
- He took a photo of the sunset from the rooftop.

take + time → Mất bao lâu
- It took me two hours to finish the assignment.
- Learning a new skill takes time and patience.

take + sb + to + place → Đưa ai đến đâu
- I will take the kids to school tomorrow morning.
- She took her parents to the airport last night.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- take a break, take action, take notes, take responsibility, take a photo

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- take off: cất cánh, cởi ra → *The plane took off on time.*
- take over: tiếp quản → *She took over the management position.*
- take up: bắt đầu sở thích mới → *He took up painting last year.*

### 10. see *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** nhìn thấy, gặp, hiểu

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| see | saw | seen |

**Lưu ý:**
- stative verb khi nghĩa là "nhìn thấy" (không chủ định)
- dynamic verb khi nghĩa là "gặp gỡ"

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

see + [noun/pronoun] → Nhìn thấy ai/cái gì
- Did you see the news about the earthquake last night?
- I haven't seen a film that good in years.

see + sb + V/V-ing → Thấy ai đó làm gì
- I saw him leave the office early yesterday.
- We saw the children playing in the park.

I see → Tôi hiểu rồi (spoken English)
- I see — so that's why the project was delayed.
- Oh, I see. That makes much more sense now.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- see a doctor, see the point, see clearly, see the difference, see eye to eye

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- see through: nhìn thấu, nhận ra → *She saw through his lies immediately.*
- see off: tiễn → *We went to the airport to see him off.*

### 11. come *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** đến

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| come | came | come |

**Lưu ý:**
- V3 cùng dạng với V1
- come ngược nghĩa với go (người nói ở đích đến)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

come + to + place → Đến nơi nào
- My parents come to visit me every Chinese New Year.
- Can you come to the office early tomorrow?

come + from → Đến từ, xuất phát từ
- This idea came from a suggestion made by a junior employee.
- Where does your motivation come from?

come + true/first/to an end → Dùng trong cụm cố định
- His dream finally came true after years of hard work.
- The concert came to an end at midnight.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- come home, come true, come first, come to mind, come to an end

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- come up with: nghĩ ra → *She came up with a brilliant idea.*
- come across: tình cờ gặp → *I came across an old photo album.*
- come back: trở lại → *When will you come back?*

### 12. think *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** nghĩ, suy nghĩ

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| think | thought | thought |

**Lưu ý:**
- stative verb khi có nghĩa "tin rằng/ý kiến"
- dynamic verb khi đang suy nghĩ tích cực

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

think + (that) + clause → Nghĩ rằng, cho rằng
- I think this proposal is worth considering seriously.
- She thinks that the new policy will benefit everyone.

think about/of + [noun/V-ing] → Suy nghĩ về điều gì
- She thought about the problem for a long time before answering.
- He is thinking of applying for a new position.

think + [question word] + clause → Nghĩ như thế nào
- What do you think will happen next?
- I can't think why she would make such a decision.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- think carefully, think clearly, think twice, think positively, think outside the box

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- think over: suy nghĩ kỹ → *Think it over before you decide.*
- think up: nghĩ ra → *He thought up a clever solution.*

### 13. look *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** nhìn, trông có vẻ

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| look | looked | looked |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -ed (regular)
- "look" là hành động chủ ý, khác "see" (nhìn thấy không chủ ý)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

look at + [noun] → Nhìn vào cái gì (chủ ý)
- Please look at the screen while I explain the diagram.
- She looked at the contract carefully before signing.

look + [adjective] → Trông có vẻ như thế nào
- He looked very nervous before his job interview.
- You look tired — did you sleep well last night?

look + like + [noun/clause] → Trông giống như
- It looks like the meeting will be postponed again.
- This design looks exactly like what the client wanted.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- look happy/tired/great, look forward to, look the same, look different, look serious

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- look up: tra cứu → *Look up the word in the dictionary.*
- look after: chăm sóc → *She looks after her elderly parents.*
- look forward to: mong chờ → *I'm looking forward to the holiday.*

### 14. want *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** muốn

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| want | wanted | wanted |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -ed (regular)
- stative verb — không dùng trong thì tiếp diễn

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

want + [noun] → Muốn cái gì
- She wants a cup of hot tea in the morning.
- The team wants more resources to complete the project.

want + to + V → Muốn làm gì
- I wanted to call you, but I lost my phone.
- He wants to apply for the position abroad.

want + sb + to + V → Muốn ai đó làm gì
- Do you want me to help you with the presentation?
- The manager wants everyone to submit their reports by Friday.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- want badly, want desperately, want more, want nothing, want to know

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- want out: muốn thoát khỏi → *He wants out of this situation.*

### 15. give *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** cho, tặng, đưa

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| give | gave | given |

**Lưu ý:**
- có thể có hai tân ngữ — give sb sth hoặc give sth to sb

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

give + sb + sth → Đưa/tặng cho ai cái gì (double object)
- She gives her students detailed feedback on every assignment.
- He gave me a very useful piece of advice.

give + sth + to + sb → Đưa/tặng cái gì cho ai
- The manager will give us more information about the new project tomorrow.
- Please give this document to the client when they arrive.

give + [noun] → Cụm cố định: give a speech, give advice
- He gave a very impressive speech at the graduation ceremony.
- She gave a short presentation about the new product.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- give advice, give a speech, give permission, give support, give feedback

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- give up: từ bỏ → *Don't give up on your dreams.*
- give away: cho đi, tiết lộ → *He gave away all his old books.*
- give back: trả lại → *Please give back the pen when you're done.*

### 16. use *(verb — regular)*
**Nghĩa:** dùng, sử dụng

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| use | used | used |

**Lưu ý:**
- thêm -d (regular, vì kết thúc bằng -e)
- "used to + V" diễn tả thói quen trong quá khứ (không còn nữa)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

use + [noun] → Sử dụng cái gì
- We use cloud software to collaborate on documents in real time.
- She uses a planner to keep track of her daily tasks.

use + [noun] + to + V → Dùng cái gì để làm gì
- She used her creativity to design an outstanding poster.
- He uses public transport to get to work every day.

used to + V → Thói quen/trạng thái trong quá khứ
- I used to walk to school when I was young.
- She used to work late every night before she changed jobs.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- use a tool, use technology, use energy, use language, use common sense

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- use up: dùng hết → *We used up all the paper in the printer.*

### 17. find *(verb — irregular)*
**Nghĩa:** tìm thấy, nhận thấy

**Chia thì:**
| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| find | found | found |

**Lưu ý:**
- V2 và V3 cùng là "found"
- find cũng có nghĩa là "nhận thấy/cho rằng" (find it easy)

**Cấu trúc & Ví dụ:**

find + [noun] → Tìm thấy cái gì
- The police found the missing child safe and sound.
- I found a great café near the office.

find + it + [adj] + to + V → Thấy việc gì là...
- I find it very interesting to learn about different cultures.
- She finds it difficult to concentrate in noisy environments.

find + sb/sth + [adj] → Nhận thấy ai/cái gì như thế nào
- He found the instructions confusing and asked for help.
- She found the new system very easy to use.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- find a solution, find time, find out, find it difficult, find one's way

**Phrasal Verbs:**
- find out: khám phá, tìm hiểu → *I need to find out where the meeting is.*
