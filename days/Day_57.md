## Từ học hôm nay

### 1. feeling *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** cảm giác; cảm xúc; suy nghĩ/ý kiến

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a feeling / feelings (C); feeling (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "gut feeling" = linh cảm trực quan, bản năng
- "mixed feelings" = cảm xúc lẫn lộn (vừa vui vừa buồn)
- "hurt sb's feelings" = làm tổn thương cảm xúc của ai

**Ví dụ:**
- She had a gut feeling that something was wrong before she even opened the email.
- He left the meeting with mixed feelings — proud of the result but sad about the process.
- Please be careful with your words — it's easy to hurt someone's feelings without realising it.
- I have a feeling that today is going to be a very good day.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- hurt sb's feelings, mixed feelings, gut feeling
- a feeling of joy/sadness, express your feelings
- have a feeling (that...) = linh cảm rằng

### 2. emotion *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** cảm xúc; xúc động

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** an emotion / emotions (C); emotion (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- Phân biệt: emotion (cảm xúc — mạnh hơn) vs. feeling (cảm giác — rộng hơn, nhẹ hơn)
- "emotional intelligence" (EQ) = trí tuệ cảm xúc — khả năng nhận biết và quản lý cảm xúc
- "overcome with emotion" = bị tràn ngập cảm xúc: "She was overcome with emotion at the funeral."

**Ví dụ:**
- The actor displayed raw emotion in the final scene that moved the entire audience.
- Learning to understand and manage your emotions is a crucial life skill.
- She was overcome with emotion when she heard she had won the scholarship.
- The film explores a wide range of human emotions, from joy and love to grief and regret.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- strong emotion, raw emotion, mixed emotions
- express / control / suppress emotions
- overcome with emotion, emotional intelligence (EQ)

### 3. love *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** tình yêu (U); mối tình / người yêu / niềm đam mê (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** love (U); a love / loves (C)

**Lưu ý:**
- "fall in love" = yêu (bắt đầu yêu); "be in love" = đang yêu
- "love at first sight" = tình yêu sét đánh
- "tough love" = tình thương nghiêm khắc (vì lợi ích lâu dài của người được yêu)

**Ví dụ:**
- She fell in love with music at an early age and devoted her life to it.
- He volunteers at the animal shelter purely for the love of it — never for money.
- They say it was love at first sight — they met on a Monday and were engaged by Friday.
- His great love of reading led him to become a librarian and later a novelist.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- fall in love (with), be in love, love at first sight
- love of music/art/life, tough love
- for the love of it

### 4. hate *(noun — uncountable)*
**Nghĩa:** sự căm ghét; thù hận

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** hate (không có số nhiều)

**Lưu ý:**
- "hate crime" = tội ác có động cơ thù hận (thành kiến chủng tộc, tôn giáo...)
- "a pet hate" (BrE) / "a pet peeve" (AmE) = điều mình đặc biệt ghét: "My pet hate is people being late."
- "hate" còn là động từ (rất phổ biến): "I hate waiting."

**Ví dụ:**
- Hate speech online has become a serious problem that platforms struggle to control.
- The law was designed to prevent hate crimes targeting people because of their religion.
- My pet hate is when people check their phones during a conversation.
- The two communities were trapped in a cycle of hate and mistrust that lasted generations.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- full of hate, hate crime, hate speech
- hate and fear, objects of hate
- a pet hate

### 5. fear *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** nỗi sợ hãi; lo lắng

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a fear / fears (C); fear (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "for fear of" = vì sợ: "She didn't speak for fear of saying the wrong thing."
- "face your fears" = đối mặt với nỗi sợ hãi của mình
- "strike fear into sb" = khiến ai đó khiếp sợ

**Ví dụ:**
- Her greatest fear is public speaking, so she has been taking classes to overcome it.
- He kept silent for fear of making the situation worse.
- The only way to conquer a fear is to face it directly and repeatedly.
- There are growing fears that the economy may be heading into recession.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- fear of failure/heights/darkness, face your fears
- in fear, for fear of, without fear
- strike fear into sb, fear factor

### 6. hope *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** hy vọng (U); điều mong muốn (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a hope / hopes (C); hope (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "a ray of hope" = một tia hy vọng (thành ngữ)
- "dash sb's hopes" = dập tan hy vọng của ai
- "Hope springs eternal." = Hy vọng luôn không bao giờ tắt (tục ngữ từ Alexander Pope)

**Ví dụ:**
- Despite the setbacks, she never lost hope and continued to work towards her goal.
- The doctor's positive report gave the family a ray of hope during a very difficult time.
- She entered the competition in the hope of winning the grand prize.
- The new treatment raised hopes for patients who had previously been told there was no cure.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- have hope, lose hope, raise/dash hopes
- a ray of hope, in the hope of
- beyond hope, hope springs eternal

### 7. anger *(noun — uncountable)*
**Nghĩa:** sự tức giận; cơn giận

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** anger (không có số nhiều)

**Lưu ý:**
- "anger management" = kiểm soát cơn giận (cả là khóa học)
- "in anger" = trong lúc tức giận: "Words spoken in anger are often regretted."
- Phân biệt: anger (giận dữ) vs. frustration (thất vọng/bực bội) vs. rage (giận dữ cực độ)

**Ví dụ:**
- She struggled to control her anger and took a deep breath before responding.
- Words spoken in anger can cause lasting damage to a relationship.
- Public anger over the government's decision led to large protests in the capital.
- He channelled his anger into his work, becoming one of the most driven people in the office.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- feel anger, express anger, manage anger
- anger management, a surge of anger, blind anger
- in anger, with anger, public anger

### 8. joy *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** niềm vui (U); điều mang lại vui mừng (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** joy (U); a joy / joys (C)

**Lưu ý:**
- "jump for joy" = cực kỳ vui mừng (thành ngữ)
- "tears of joy" = nước mắt hạnh phúc
- Phân biệt: joy (niềm vui sâu sắc, trong sáng) vs. happiness (hạnh phúc — rộng hơn) vs. pleasure (khoái cảm — thường từ điều cụ thể)

**Ví dụ:**
- She jumped for joy when she found out she had passed all her exams.
- The young pianist is a joy to watch — her passion and skill are extraordinary.
- She cried tears of joy at her daughter's wedding ceremony.
- Gardening is one of the simple joys that brings her peace and satisfaction every day.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- jump for joy, tears of joy
- a joy to watch/be with, the joys of parenthood
- bring / spread joy, full of joy

### 9. sadness *(noun — uncountable)*
**Nghĩa:** nỗi buồn; sự buồn bã

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** sadness (không có số nhiều)

**Lưu ý:**
- Phân biệt: sadness (nỗi buồn — cảm xúc) vs. sorrow (đau buồn — sâu sắc hơn) vs. grief (đau khổ — thường vì mất mát)
- "tinged with sadness" = pha lẫn chút buồn
- "melancholy" = nỗi buồn nhẹ, mơ hồ (từ văn học)

**Ví dụ:**
- There was deep sadness in his eyes as he said goodbye for the last time.
- The news of her sudden passing filled everyone who knew her with sadness.
- The ending of the film was tinged with sadness, even though the main character achieved her goal.
- She smiled through the sadness, determined not to let grief ruin such a beautiful day.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- deep sadness, overwhelming sadness, feel sadness
- tinged with sadness, sense of sadness
- sadness in sb's eyes

### 10. surprise *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** sự ngạc nhiên (U); điều bất ngờ (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a surprise / surprises (C); surprise (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "take sb by surprise" = làm ai bất ngờ, khiến ai không kịp đề phòng
- "to sb's surprise" = khiến ai đó ngạc nhiên: "To everyone's surprise, he resigned."
- "a surprise party" = tiệc bất ngờ (người được tổ chức không biết trước)

**Ví dụ:**
- The news came as a complete surprise — no one expected her to resign so suddenly.
- To his surprise, the audience stood up and applauded for over five minutes.
- They organised a surprise party to celebrate her thirtieth birthday.
- The attack took the defenders completely by surprise, leaving them with no time to react.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- come as a surprise, take sb by surprise, element of surprise
- to my surprise, a surprise party / visit
- surprise attack

### 11. trust *(noun — uncountable)*
**Nghĩa:** lòng tin; sự tín nhiệm

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** trust (không có số nhiều trong nghĩa "lòng tin"); a trust / trusts (C) trong nghĩa tài chính

**Lưu ý:**
- "breach of trust" = vi phạm lòng tin (có thể là tội pháp lý)
- "position of trust" = vị trí được trao quyền và tin tưởng
- "a trust fund" = quỹ ủy thác — tiền để dành cho con cái

**Ví dụ:**
- Trust is the foundation of any healthy relationship, whether personal or professional.
- He betrayed her trust by sharing her personal secrets with their mutual friends.
- Building trust with customers takes years of consistent and honest behaviour.
- Teachers occupy a position of trust and must always act in the best interests of their students.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- build / earn / lose trust, breach of trust
- put your trust in sb/sth, betray sb's trust
- trust issues, position of trust, trust fund

### 12. doubt *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** sự nghi ngờ (U); mối nghi ngờ cụ thể (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a doubt / doubts (C); doubt (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "give sb the benefit of the doubt" = cho ai đó cơ hội, không vội kết luận xấu về họ
- "beyond doubt" / "without a shadow of a doubt" = chắc chắn tuyệt đối
- "when in doubt, don't" = khi không chắc, đừng làm (lời khuyên thực tế)

**Ví dụ:**
- I had serious doubts about the plan but decided to give it a chance.
- The evidence proves beyond doubt that he was not at the scene of the crime.
- He was late twice before, but I'll give him the benefit of the doubt this time.
- She spoke with such confidence that no one had any doubt she was telling the truth.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- have doubts (about), cast doubt on, shadow of a doubt
- beyond doubt, without doubt, no doubt
- in doubt, give sb the benefit of the doubt

### 13. confidence *(noun — uncountable)*
**Nghĩa:** sự tự tin; lòng tin tưởng; bí mật (in confidence)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** confidence (không có số nhiều)

**Lưu ý:**
- "in confidence" = bí mật, riêng tư: "I'm telling you this in confidence."
- "vote of confidence" = bỏ phiếu tín nhiệm (chính trị)
- "confidence trick" / "con" = trò lừa đảo lấy lòng tin

**Ví dụ:**
- Public speaking classes helped her build the confidence she needed for her career.
- She told me in confidence that she was planning to leave the company.
- The prime minister survived the vote of confidence and remains in office.
- His confidence in his own ability allowed him to take on challenges others would avoid.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- self-confidence, confidence in sb/sth, build / boost confidence
- in confidence, vote of confidence
- with confidence, loss of confidence, confidence trick

### 14. pride *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** lòng tự hào (U); điều tự hào (C); đàn sư tử (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** pride (U); a pride of lions (C)

**Lưu ý:**
- "take pride in" = tự hào về: "She takes great pride in her work."
- "Pride comes before a fall." = Kiêu căng thường dẫn đến thất bại (tục ngữ)
- "Pride" (viết hoa) = phong trào tự hào của cộng đồng LGBTQ+

**Ví dụ:**

- He takes great pride in his garden, spending every weekend maintaining it perfectly.
- Remember: pride comes before a fall — stay humble even when things are going well.
- The documentary followed a pride of lions hunting across the savannah.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- take pride in, with pride, burst with pride
- pride and joy, pride comes before a fall
- national pride, false pride, Pride (LGBTQ+ movement)

### 15. shame *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** sự xấu hổ (U); điều đáng tiếc (C: "it's a shame")

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** shame (U); a shame (C, trong "it's a shame")

**Lưu ý:**
- "What a shame!" = Thật đáng tiếc! (thường là tiếc nuối, không nhất thiết liên quan đến xấu hổ)
- "put sb to shame" = làm ai đó trông kém hơn vì sự xuất sắc của mình
- "shame on you!" = đồ vô liêm sỉ! / thật xấu hổ cho bạn!

**Ví dụ:**
- He felt deep shame after realising he had falsely accused an innocent person.
- It's a shame that the concert was cancelled — everyone was looking forward to it.
- Her baking skills put mine to shame — her cakes are absolutely perfect.
- The politician hung his head in shame as the scandal was read out in court.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- feel shame, sense of shame, hang your head in shame
- it's a shame (that), what a shame!, put sb to shame
- shame on you!, shame culture

### 16. guilt *(noun — uncountable)*
**Nghĩa:** tội lỗi; cảm giác có lỗi

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** guilt (không có số nhiều)

**Lưu ý:**
- "guilt trip" = hành động gây cảm giác có lỗi cho ai: "She's always guilt-tripping me."
- "survivor's guilt" = cảm giác có lỗi của người sống sót (sau tai nạn/thảm họa)
- Phân biệt: guilt (cảm giác có lỗi) vs. shame (xấu hổ — liên quan đến hình ảnh bản thân trước người khác)

**Ví dụ:**
- She was overcome with guilt after forgetting her best friend's birthday.
- He admitted his guilt in court and was sentenced to two years in prison.
- Stop guilt-tripping me about eating the last slice of cake — I was hungry!
- The guilt of having lied to his parents weighed on him for weeks.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- feel guilt, overcome with guilt, guilt trip
- sense of guilt, survivor's guilt, guilt-ridden
- plead guilty

### 17. happiness *(noun — uncountable)*
**Nghĩa:** hạnh phúc; niềm vui

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** happiness (không có số nhiều)

**Lưu ý:**
- "the pursuit of happiness" = việc theo đuổi hạnh phúc (từ Tuyên ngôn Độc lập Mỹ)
- "Money can't buy happiness." = Tiền không mua được hạnh phúc (tục ngữ)
- Phân biệt: happiness (hạnh phúc — trạng thái lâu dài) vs. joy (niềm vui — mạnh mẽ hơn nhưng ngắn hơn) vs. pleasure (khoái cảm — từ điều cụ thể)

**Ví dụ:**
- She found true happiness only after she stopped comparing herself to others.
- The Declaration of Independence states that all people have the right to pursue happiness.
- Money can't buy happiness, but financial security certainly reduces stress and worry.
- Helping others brought her a deep sense of happiness that nothing else could match.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- find / seek happiness, bring happiness, pursuit of happiness
- genuine / true happiness, happiness and fulfilment
- money can't buy happiness
