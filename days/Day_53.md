## Từ học hôm nay

### 1. business *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** kinh doanh (U); một doanh nghiệp (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a business / businesses (C); business (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- Uncountable: "Business was slow during the pandemic." (tình hình kinh doanh)
- Countable: "She started a small catering business." (một doanh nghiệp)
- "Mind your own business" = đừng xen vào việc của người khác

**Ví dụ:**
- He went on a business trip to Singapore to meet potential investors.
- She started her own business at the age of 25 with very little capital.
- That's none of your business — I'll deal with my finances myself.
- Many small businesses had to close permanently during the economic downturn.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- do business (with), run a business, go out of business
- business trip, business card, business plan
- mean business, monkey business

---

### 2. company *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** công ty; sự đồng hành (U); khách đến thăm

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a company / companies

**Lưu ý:**
- "Company" (U, không mạo từ) = sự đồng hành: "I enjoy his company."
- "Keep sb company" = ở cạnh ai cho họ vui, không cô đơn
- "Two's company, three's a crowd." = Hai người là đôi, ba người là đám (tục ngữ)

**Ví dụ:**
- The company was founded in 1995 and now employs over 5,000 people.
- She kept her elderly neighbour company by visiting every afternoon.
- I enjoyed his company so much that the hours flew by without me noticing.
- The tech company expanded rapidly and opened offices in fifteen countries.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- start / run / join a company, company policy
- in good company
- keep sb company, part company

---

### 3. job *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** việc làm; công việc cụ thể; nhiệm vụ

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a job / jobs

**Lưu ý:**
- Phân biệt: job (công việc có trả lương) vs. work (U — công việc nói chung) vs. career (sự nghiệp lâu dài)
- "It's a good job that" = may mà (BrE): "It's a good job you brought an umbrella!"
- "On the job" = đang làm việc: "He was injured on the job."

**Ví dụ:**
- She applied for over thirty jobs before finally getting an offer.
- Good job on the presentation — the clients were very impressed.
- It was a good job he checked his emails before leaving — there was a schedule change.
- Many people lost their jobs during the economic crisis.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- full-time / part-time job, dream job, job interview
- apply for a job, get a job, lose a job
- good job!, it's a good job that...

---

### 4. work *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** công việc (U); tác phẩm (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** work (U) / a work, works (C)

**Lưu ý:**
- Uncountable: "Work starts at 9 AM." / "She has a lot of work to do."
- Countable: "This sculpture is a remarkable work of art."
- "The works" = tất cả mọi thứ: "I'll have a burger with the works." (informal)

**Ví dụ:**
- Hard work and perseverance are more important than natural talent in the long run.
- Beethoven's Ninth Symphony is considered one of the greatest works in classical music.
- She struggled to maintain a healthy work-life balance after her promotion.
- The museum has an impressive collection of works by modern artists.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- go to work, at work, hard work, teamwork
- work of art, a work of fiction, the works of Shakespeare
- out of work, overworked, work-life balance

### 5. career *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** sự nghiệp; nghề nghiệp

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a career / careers

**Lưu ý:**
- Phân biệt: career (sự nghiệp lâu dài) vs. job (việc làm cụ thể hiện tại) vs. occupation (nghề nghiệp — formal)
- "Career break" = thời gian tạm nghỉ sự nghiệp (thường để chăm con)
- "Career-minded" = tập trung vào sự nghiệp

**Ví dụ:**
- She decided to pursue a career in medicine after volunteering at a local hospital.
- After years in finance, he made a dramatic career change and became a chef.
- Good communication skills are essential for career advancement in any industry.
- She took a career break for two years to look after her young children.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- pursue a career (in), career path, career change
- build / advance / develop a career
- career advice, career goals, career prospects

### 6. salary *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** lương (trả theo tháng/năm)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a salary / salaries

**Lưu ý:**
- Phân biệt: salary (lương tháng/năm — thường cho văn phòng) vs. wage (lương giờ/ngày — thường cho lao động thủ công) vs. income (thu nhập tổng thể)
- "Gross salary" = lương trước thuế; "net salary" = lương sau thuế
- Negotiate your salary before accepting a job offer

**Ví dụ:**
- She negotiated a higher salary before accepting the job offer.
- The starting salary for new graduates in this field is around $45,000 per year.
- After five years with the company, he finally received a significant salary increase.
- A competitive salary package is important for attracting talented employees.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- annual salary, starting salary, minimum salary
- earn / receive / negotiate a salary
- salary increase / raise, salary package, gross/net salary

---

### 7. income *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** thu nhập

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** an income / incomes (C); income (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "Disposable income" = thu nhập khả dụng (sau khi trừ chi phí thiết yếu)
- "Passive income" = thu nhập thụ động (từ đầu tư, cho thuê nhà...)
- "Income inequality" = bất bình đẳng thu nhập (vấn đề kinh tế xã hội)

**Ví dụ:**
- She earned a modest income from her online freelance work.
- Families on low incomes often struggle to afford quality healthcare and education.
- His goal is to build passive income streams through property investment.
- The government offers tax credits to support people on low incomes.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- high / low income, disposable income, passive income
- earn an income, live on an income
- income tax, income gap, income inequality

---

### 8. price *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** giá (tiền); cái giá phải trả (nghĩa bóng)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a price / prices

**Lưu ý:**
- "At a price" = với cái giá phải trả: "Success came at a price."
- "Put a price on" = định giá cho: "You can't put a price on happiness."
- Phân biệt: price (giá bán) vs. cost (chi phí — thường về tổng chi phí) vs. value (giá trị)

**Ví dụ:**
- The price of housing in major cities has risen dramatically over the past decade.
- She achieved success, but it came at a price — she sacrificed her health and relationships.
- You can't put a price on the happiness that comes from spending time with family.
- Shoppers are looking for good quality products at reasonable prices.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- high / low price, asking price, selling price
- price rise / increase / drop, price tag, price war
- at any price, name your price

---

### 9. cost *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** chi phí (U/C); cái giá phải trả

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a cost / costs (C); cost (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "At all costs" = bằng mọi giá (nhất định phải): "We must win at all costs."
- "At the cost of" = phải trả giá bằng: "He succeeded at the cost of his health."
- "Cost of living" = chi phí sinh hoạt (quan trọng trong kinh tế)

**Ví dụ:**
- The cost of living in big cities has become unaffordable for many young people.
- The company needs to cut costs to remain competitive in the current market.
- We must finish the project on time, at all costs.
- The new policy was introduced at the cost of many workers losing their jobs.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- cut costs, reduce costs, running costs, hidden costs
- at all costs, at the cost of
- cost of living, cost-effective, cost-benefit analysis

---

### 10. value *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** giá trị (U); điều quan trọng (C); values = các giá trị đạo đức

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a value / values (C); value (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- Uncountable: "The painting has increased in value." (giá trị vật chất)
- Countable (plural): "Good parents teach their children strong values." (giá trị đạo đức)
- "Take sth at face value" = chấp nhận đúng theo nghĩa bề mặt, không nghi ngờ

**Ví dụ:**
- The value of the property has tripled since they bought it fifteen years ago.
- Hard work and honesty are the core values that guide her in both life and business.
- Don't take his offer at face value — check the fine print before signing.
- A good education gives young people skills that are of lasting value.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- market value, face value, value for money
- family values, core values, shared values
- of great value, lose / gain value, value judgment

---

### 11. quality *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** chất lượng (U); đặc tính, phẩm chất (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a quality / qualities (C); quality (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- Uncountable: "This product is renowned for its quality." (chất lượng)
- Countable: "Patience is an essential quality for a teacher." (phẩm chất)
- "Quality time" = thời gian có chất lượng (dành cho gia đình, người thân)

**Ví dụ:**
- The factory must maintain strict quality control to meet international standards.
- The quality of life in rural areas has improved significantly over the past decade.
- Empathy is the most important quality a good leader should possess.
- She spends quality time with her children every evening before bed.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- high / low quality, quality control, quality assurance
- quality time, quality of life
- have the quality of, leadership qualities

---

### 12. service *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** dịch vụ (C/U); lần phục vụ (C); lễ tôn giáo (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a service / services (C); service (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- "At your service" = sẵn sàng phục vụ (lịch sự)
- "Do sb a disservice" = vô tình gây hại cho ai
- "National service" = nghĩa vụ quân sự bắt buộc

**Ví dụ:**
- The hotel is known for its excellent customer service and attention to detail.
- The government provides many public services, including education and healthcare.
- I'm at your service — just let me know if you need anything during your stay.
- Criticizing the new teacher in front of the class does her a disservice.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- customer service, public service, emergency service
- provide / offer a service, service industry
- do sb a disservice, at your service

---

### 13. project *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** dự án; đề án

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a project / projects

**Lưu ý:**
- "Project" làm động từ: "project an image" (chiếu hình ảnh) / "project future growth" (dự đoán tăng trưởng)
- "Project manager" = quản lý dự án — vai trò quan trọng trong doanh nghiệp
- "A pilot project" = dự án thí điểm

**Ví dụ:**
- The team worked tirelessly to complete the project before the deadline.
- The government announced a major housing project to address the shortage of affordable homes.
- She was appointed project manager and immediately began assembling her team.
- The company launched a pilot project to test the new software with a small group of users.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- work on a project, complete / finish a project, launch a project
- housing project, research project, pilot project
- project manager, project deadline, on schedule

---

### 14. goal *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** mục tiêu; bàn thắng (thể thao)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a goal / goals

**Lưu ý:**
- "Goal-oriented" (adj) = định hướng mục tiêu: "She is a very goal-oriented person."
- Phân biệt: goal (mục tiêu — cụ thể hơn) vs. aim (mục đích — chung hơn) vs. objective (mục tiêu — thường dùng trong kinh doanh/học thuật)
- "Score a goal" = ghi bàn (thể thao)

**Ví dụ:**
- She set herself the goal of running a marathon before her thirtieth birthday.
- The striker scored two goals in the final minutes to win the match for his team.
- Setting clear goals is the first step toward achieving anything meaningful in life.
- The company's goal is to become the market leader within five years.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- set / achieve / reach a goal, long-term / short-term goal
- score a goal, goal-oriented
- personal goals, career goals, life goals

---

### 15. target *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** mục tiêu (cụ thể, có thể đo được); đích

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a target / targets

**Lưu ý:**
- Phân biệt: target (mục tiêu đo được: "sales target $1M") vs. goal (mục tiêu rộng hơn: "become successful")
- "On target" = đúng tiến độ, đang hướng đến mục tiêu
- "Target market" = thị trường mục tiêu; "target audience" = đối tượng mục tiêu

**Ví dụ:**
- The sales team hit their quarterly target for the first time in two years.
- The marketing campaign is specifically designed to reach its target audience of young professionals.
- We are on target to complete the project by the end of the month.
- The government set ambitious targets for reducing carbon emissions by 2030.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- meet / hit / miss / set a target
- sales target, target market, target audience
- on target, target date, moving target

---

### 16. strategy *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** chiến lược

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a strategy / strategies (C); strategy (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- Phân biệt: strategy (chiến lược — kế hoạch tổng thể) vs. tactic (chiến thuật — hành động cụ thể để thực hiện chiến lược)
- "Exit strategy" = kế hoạch rút khỏi một tình huống/đầu tư
- "Strategic" là tính từ: "strategic planning"

**Ví dụ:**
- The company needs a new strategy to compete in the rapidly changing digital market.
- Before investing, it's essential to have a clear exit strategy in case things go wrong.
- The charity developed a long-term strategy to reduce poverty in the region over ten years.
- Good negotiators always have a strategy before entering important meetings.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- develop / implement / adopt a strategy
- business strategy, marketing strategy, exit strategy
- long-term / short-term strategy, strategic planning

---

### 17. resource *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** tài nguyên; nguồn lực (thường dùng số nhiều: resources)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a resource / resources

**Lưu ý:**
- "Human Resources" (HR) = bộ phận nhân sự của công ty
- "Natural resources" = tài nguyên thiên nhiên (dầu, khí, khoáng sản...)
- "Resourceful" (adj) = có khả năng tìm ra giải pháp sáng tạo khi thiếu nguồn lực

**Ví dụ:**
- The country is rich in natural resources such as oil, gas, and rare minerals.
- The project was delayed because of limited resources and an understaffed team.
- She contacted the Human Resources department to ask about the company's benefits package.
- Being resourceful is a key skill when working with a tight budget.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- natural resources, human resources (HR), financial resources
- allocate / use / waste resources, limited resources
- resource management, resource-rich, tap into resources
