## Từ học hôm nay

### 1. tradition *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** truyền thống

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a tradition / traditions (C); tradition (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- Uncountable (khái niệm chung): "Tradition plays an important role in society."
- Countable (một phong tục cụ thể): "It's a tradition to eat turkey on Thanksgiving."
- "Break with tradition" = phá vỡ truyền thống

**Ví dụ:**
- Our family has a tradition of gathering every Sunday for dinner.
- By tradition, the eldest son inherits the family business.
- She refused to break with tradition and wore a white dress at the wedding.
- Many cultural traditions have been passed down for hundreds of years.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- long-standing tradition, cultural tradition, ancient tradition
- keep / maintain / uphold a tradition, break with tradition
- tradition of doing sth, by tradition

### 2. religion *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** tôn giáo

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a religion / religions (C); religion (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- Uncountable (khái niệm): "Religion has shaped human history for thousands of years."
- Countable (một tôn giáo cụ thể): "Buddhism is one of the world's major religions."
- Phân biệt: religion (tôn giáo) vs. faith (đức tin) vs. belief (niềm tin)

**Ví dụ:**
- Religion has influenced art and culture throughout history.
- Buddhism is one of the world's major religions.
- The constitution guarantees freedom of religion for all citizens.
- People of all religions are welcome in this community.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- world religion, organized religion, major religion
- practice a religion, follow a religion, convert to a religion
- freedom of religion, religion and politics

### 3. language *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** ngôn ngữ

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a language / languages (C); language (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- Uncountable (khả năng ngôn ngữ): "Language is the most powerful tool humans possess."
- Countable (một ngôn ngữ cụ thể): "She speaks three languages fluently."
- "Body language" và "sign language" là cụm cố định (không dùng mạo từ "a" khi nói chung)

**Ví dụ:**
- She speaks three languages fluently: English, French, and Spanish.
- Language is the most powerful tool humans possess.
- The language barrier made communication very difficult.
- Learning a foreign language opens many doors in life and work.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- foreign language, native language, sign language, body language
- learn / speak / master a language, use language
- language skills, language barrier, language learning

### 4. education *(noun — uncountable)*
**Nghĩa:** giáo dục

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** education / (không có số nhiều)

**Lưu ý:**
- Thường không dùng mạo từ khi nói chung: "Education is important."
- Dùng "the" khi xác định cụ thể: "the education system", "the education I received"
- "An education" (nghĩa bóng): "Traveling the world was an education." = một bài học quý giá

**Ví dụ:**
- Education is the key to a better future.
- She received her education at a prestigious university in London.
- The government plans to increase spending on education next year.
- Access to quality education should be a right for every child.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- higher education, primary/secondary education, quality education
- receive / get / provide education
- education system, education policy, education reform

### 5. economy *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** nền kinh tế

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** an economy / economies

**Lưu ý:**
- Thường dùng "the economy" khi nói về nền kinh tế của một quốc gia cụ thể
- "An economy" khi so sánh hoặc miêu tả loại hình kinh tế
- Phân biệt: economy (nền kinh tế) vs. economics (môn kinh tế học — U) vs. economist (nhà kinh tế học)

**Ví dụ:**
- The global economy has been recovering slowly after the pandemic.
- Vietnam has one of the fastest-growing economies in Southeast Asia.
- High inflation is damaging the country's economy.
- A strong economy benefits all members of society.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- global economy, local economy, market economy
- boost / strengthen / grow the economy, damage the economy
- economy grows / shrinks / recovers

### 6. trade *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** thương mại, buôn bán (U); nghề thủ công (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a trade / trades (C); trade (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- Uncountable (thương mại): "International trade is vital for economic growth."
- Countable (một nghề): "He learned a trade as a carpenter."
- "Trade" cũng là động từ: "to trade goods" = buôn bán hàng hóa

**Ví dụ:**
- The two countries signed a free trade agreement last month.
- The trade war between the US and China affected global markets.
- His father taught him the trade of woodworking.
- Many skilled tradespeople are in high demand across the country.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- international trade, free trade, trade agreement
- trade deficit/surplus, trade war, trade policy
- carry on a trade, learn a trade

### 7. industry *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** ngành công nghiệp (C); công nghiệp nói chung (U)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** an industry / industries (C); industry (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- Uncountable (công nghiệp nói chung): "Industry has transformed the modern world."
- Countable (một ngành công nghiệp): "The film industry is worth billions of dollars."
- "Industry" cũng có nghĩa là sự chăm chỉ/cần cù (formal/literary)

**Ví dụ:**
- The automotive industry employs millions of people worldwide.
- Heavy industry caused serious pollution in the region during the 20th century.
- She has worked in the fashion industry for over ten years.
- The tourism industry has recovered strongly since the pandemic ended.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- manufacturing industry, tourism industry, tech industry
- heavy / light industry
- industry leader, industry standard, work in an industry

### 8. technology *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** công nghệ

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a technology / technologies (C); technology (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- Uncountable (công nghệ nói chung): "Technology has changed the way we live."
- Countable (các công nghệ cụ thể): "The company invests in new technologies."
- Viết tắt phổ biến: IT (Information Technology), AI (Artificial Intelligence)

**Ví dụ:**
- Technology has changed the way we communicate dramatically.
- The company invests heavily in new technologies to stay competitive.
- Cutting-edge technology is being used to develop cleaner energy sources.
- Without technology, modern medicine would not be possible.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- modern technology, advanced technology, cutting-edge technology
- develop / use / adopt technology
- information technology (IT), technology company, technology sector

### 9. science *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** khoa học

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a science / sciences (C); science (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- Uncountable (khoa học nói chung): "Science has helped us understand the world."
- Countable (một ngành khoa học): "Medicine is both an art and a science."
- "It's not rocket science!" = Không phải thứ gì quá khó (thành ngữ)

**Ví dụ:**
- Science has helped us understand the origins of the universe.
- Medicine is both an art and a science.
- She studied computer science at university and now works for a tech firm.
- Young people should be encouraged to study science and technology.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- natural science, social science, computer science
- science and technology, science fiction
- do science, study science, science teacher

### 10. art *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** nghệ thuật (U); tác phẩm/bộ môn nghệ thuật (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** an art / arts (C); art (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- Uncountable (nghệ thuật nói chung): "Art reflects the society in which it is created."
- Countable (bộ môn/kỹ năng): "Photography is an art." / "the art of conversation"
- "The arts" = các ngành nghệ thuật (âm nhạc, hội họa, điêu khắc...)

**Ví dụ:**
- The museum has an impressive collection of modern art.
- Photography is an art that requires both technical skill and creativity.
- She has mastered the art of making people feel welcome.
- The government should invest more in supporting the arts.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- fine art, modern art, contemporary art, street art
- work of art, art gallery, art museum, art form
- the art of (doing) sth

### 11. music *(noun — uncountable)*
**Nghĩa:** âm nhạc

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** music / (không có số nhiều — dùng "a piece of music" để đếm)

**Lưu ý:**
- Không dùng "a music" — sai; dùng "a piece of music" nếu muốn đếm
- "The music" khi chỉ một bản nhạc cụ thể đang được phát
- "Musical" (adj): a musical instrument / (n): a musical (vở nhạc kịch)

**Ví dụ:**
- Music has the power to heal the soul and bring people together.
- She plays the piano and writes her own music.
- The background music in the café made the atmosphere very relaxing.
- He composed a beautiful piece of music for the ceremony.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- classical music, pop music, live music, background music
- listen to music, play music, make music, write music
- music industry, music festival, music taste

### 12. sport *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** thể thao (U); môn thể thao (C)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a sport / sports (C); sport (U, BrE)

**Lưu ý:**

- "A good sport" = người biết chịu thua/chơi fair (nghĩa bóng)
- "Take up a sport" = bắt đầu chơi một môn thể thao

**Ví dụ:**
- Football is the most popular sport in the world.
- Regular sport helps improve both physical and mental health.
- She tried many sports before falling in love with swimming.
- Being a good sport means accepting both victory and defeat gracefully.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- team sport, contact sport, extreme sport, outdoor sport
- play sport/sports, do sport, take up a sport
- sport event, sports club, sports day

### 13. war *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** chiến tranh

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a war / wars (C); war (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- Uncountable (chiến tranh như hiện tượng): "War causes immense human suffering."
- Countable (một cuộc chiến cụ thể): "The two countries fought a long war."
- "At war" = đang trong tình trạng chiến tranh: "The two countries are at war."

**Ví dụ:**
- World War II ended in 1945 with the defeat of Nazi Germany.
- The civil war destroyed the country's infrastructure for decades.
- No one wins in a war — everyone suffers.
- The trade war between the two nations damaged both economies.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- civil war, world war, trade war, cold war
- declare war, go to war, fight a war, win/lose a war
- war crime, war zone, at war (with)

### 14. peace *(noun — uncountable)*
**Nghĩa:** hòa bình; sự yên tĩnh

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** peace / (không có số nhiều)

**Lưu ý:**
- Thường không dùng mạo từ: "Peace is valuable."
- "Disturb the peace" = phá vỡ sự yên tĩnh
- "Peace of mind" = sự yên tâm; "at peace" = trong trạng thái bình an

**Ví dụ:**
- The two nations finally signed a peace treaty after years of conflict.
- I just want some peace and quiet after a long day at work.
- Finding inner peace is more valuable than material wealth.
- The peace talks between the two sides collapsed after three days.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- world peace, inner peace, make peace (with)
- peace treaty, peace talks, peace process
- at peace, in peace, keep the peace, disturb the peace

### 15. conflict *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** xung đột; mâu thuẫn

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** a conflict / conflicts (C); conflict (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- Uncountable (xung đột nói chung): "Conflict is inevitable in any relationship."
- Countable (một vụ xung đột cụ thể): "The armed conflict lasted three years."
- "Conflict of interest" = xung đột lợi ích (thuật ngữ pháp lý/kinh doanh)

**Ví dụ:**
- The armed conflict in the region has displaced thousands of civilians.
- There is often a conflict between personal values and professional obligations.
- The judge had to withdraw from the case due to a conflict of interest.
- Open communication can help resolve conflict in the workplace.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- armed conflict, internal conflict, conflict of interest
- resolve / avoid / cause conflict
- in conflict with, conflict between

### 16. agreement *(noun — both C & U)*
**Nghĩa:** thỏa thuận (C); sự đồng ý (U)

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** an agreement / agreements (C); agreement (U)

**Lưu ý:**
- Uncountable (trạng thái đồng ý): "We are all in agreement on this matter."
- Countable (một thỏa thuận cụ thể): "They signed a trade agreement."
- Phân biệt: agreement (thỏa thuận) vs. contract (hợp đồng — có tính pháp lý hơn)

**Ví dụ:**
- The two companies reached an agreement on the terms of the merger.
- There is general agreement that climate change is a serious problem.
- She nodded in agreement when the teacher explained the solution.
- Breaking an agreement can damage your reputation and trust.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- reach / sign / make an agreement, break an agreement
- trade agreement, peace agreement, formal agreement
- in agreement (with), come to an agreement

### 17. election *(noun — countable)*
**Nghĩa:** cuộc bầu cử

**Số ít / Số nhiều:** an election / elections

**Lưu ý:**
- "Call an election" = tuyên bố tổ chức bầu cử
- "Landslide election" = thắng cử với đa số phiếu áp đảo
- Liên quan: vote (bỏ phiếu), candidate (ứng cử viên), ballot (lá phiếu)

**Ví dụ:**
- The presidential election will be held next November.
- He won the election by a large margin, gaining over 60% of the vote.
- The party decided to call an early election to strengthen its position.
- Free and fair elections are the foundation of a democratic society.

**Collocation phổ biến:**
- general election, presidential election, local election
- hold / win / lose an election, call an election
- election campaign, election result, election day
